Founder · BestDateWeather

Trinidad and Tobago, micro-state of the Lesser Antilles in the south of the Caribbean 7 miles off Venezuela: 1.4 million inhabitants on 2 main islands (Trinidad 1,840 sq miles + Tobago 120 sq miles). Independent of the United Kingdom 1962, republic since 1976, Commonwealth member, official language English + Trinidadian English creoles + French. Cultural particularities = birthplace of the steel pan (steel drum, metal percussion instrument invented in Trinidad 1930-1940 by metallurgical workers using oil drums = the only musical instrument invented in the 20th century universally recognised) + calypso + soca + limbo = musical cradle of the Caribbean. Capital Port of Spain + Trinidad Carnival = one of the largest carnivals in the world (rivalling Rio + Notting Hill London). January-December are my favorite months (6.9/10): 73-88°F, 31-32% rain, this is the ideal dry season. Trinidad never tops 6.9/10 (hot humid tropical climate year-round). From January to May, it's the dry season: 73-90°F, this is the tourist high season (Carnival February-March + dry season). From June to November, it's the wet season: 50-78% rainfall, outside the main cyclonic zone (Trinidad-Tobago is south of the Hurricane Belt at 10°N latitude = rarely hit by hurricanes unlike northern Caribbean, one of the major advantages of the southern islands). Highlights: Port of Spain capital (Trinidad, urban Anglo-Creole Afro-Caribbean atmosphere, Queen's Park Savannah large central square, National Museum and Art Gallery, Royal Botanic Gardens 1820), Trinidad Carnival (world cultural iconic, February-March depending on Easter date, 2 days of Mardi Gras + Ash Wednesday with masked costumed parades "masquerade" + steel pan competitions Panorama + soca music = unique world cultural iconic experience, one of the 5 largest carnivals in the world with Rio + Salvador + Notting Hill + Cologne, hotel prices x4-x5 during), Maracas Bay (the most famous beach in Trinidad, golden sand, Atlantic waves, iconic bake and shark sandwich to eat), Asa Wright Nature Center (top world ornithological reserve in the Northern Range jungle, endemic birds jacamar + manakin + macaw), Caroni Bird Sanctuary (sanctuary of the Scarlet Ibis = scarlet ibis the national bird of Trinidad, twilight observation by boat when thousands of red ibises return to sleep at sunset), Pitch Lake at La Brea (the largest natural asphalt lake in the world 100 hectares, exploited since the British colonial era to tar roads worldwide, unique geological iconic), Tobago neighbouring island (30 min by ferry or 25 min by plane, smaller and more touristic, Pigeon Point Beach ranked among the most beautiful beaches in the Caribbean, Buccoo Reef top regional snorkelling, Argyle Falls waterfall, Tobago Main Ridge Forest Reserve = the oldest protected forest reserve in the world 1776). Try doubles (flatbread with spicy chickpeas, iconic national dish), bake and shark (grilled shark sandwich), pelau (Caribbean sauce chicken rice), roti (Indian heritage grand mass), curry chicken, callaloo, rum punch, Carib beer + Stag beer + Angostura rum (the famous Angostura bitters was invented in Trinidad 1875 by the German doctor Johann Siegert to treat Bolivar's soldiers = world iconic of cocktails). Port of Spain Piarco airport (POS).

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 72-82°F, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 61°F + tmax ≥ 79°F)
