Context

Our verdict

Windhoek is above all the logistic base for Namibia. At 5,600 ft altitude on the central plateau, temperatures stay surprising: from May to August, nights drop to 46°F while days stay at 72-77°F. September is my favorite month (9.5/10): 55-84°F, 2% rain, clear sky to reach Sossusvlei, Etosha or the Skeleton Coast. From December to March, the rainy season brings 25-33% stormy showers (often in late afternoon) and temperatures climb to 86-90°F. The tracks can become difficult, some lodges close. It's the only period when the Namib re-greens: a rare and photogenic face of the desert. April-May are a good transition: ending rains, golden light, temperatures back to mild.
