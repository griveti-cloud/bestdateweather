Colombia, South American country in the extreme north-west of the continent: 52 million inhabitants, only South American country with access to both Caribbean AND Pacific. Considered the "most diverse" of South America (Andes + Caribbean + Pacific + Amazon + eastern Llanos plains). Image transformed since 2016 (peace agreement with the FARC, end of the longest civil conflict on the continent), strong tourism growth. September is my favorite month (7.7/10) in Andean zone: 48-64°F, 34% rain. From August to September and December-February, it's the tourist high season: climate varies by altitude (hot coastal 82°F, cool mountainous 64°F). From March to June and October-November, it's the rainy season in Andean zone. All year round, the absence of marked seasons (apart from rainy/dry) allows visiting (Colombia = country of "micro-climates" by altitude). Regions to know: Bogotá (capital 8,700 ft, Gold Museum — see dedicated review), Cartagena de Indias (Caribbean UNESCO — see dedicated review), Medellín ("city of eternal spring" 4,900 ft, transformed since the Pablo Escobar era died in 1993, Comuna 13 graffiti and urban cable car), Cali (capital of Colombian salsa), Eje Cafetero ("coffee axis", Salento, Filandia, Cocora Valley with its giant 200 ft wax palm trees, coffee fincas to visit), San Andrés and Providencia (Caribbean islands 465 miles off the Colombian continental coast, closer to Nicaragua), Tayrona National Park (virgin Caribbean, jungle hike to Cabo San Juan), Colombian Amazon (Leticia, Brazil-Peru border). Safety: very improved situation since 2016, but avoid the rural zones of the south (Putumayo, Cauca, Nariño) where some dissident groups persist. Bogotá and Medellín = standard urban vigilance. Try bandeja paisa, ajiaco, arepa, lechona, Geisha coffee, aguardiente.

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 72-82°F, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 61°F + tmax ≥ 79°F)
