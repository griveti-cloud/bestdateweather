Context

Our verdict

Seville is the quintessence of Andalusia: Alcazar, cathedral, flamenco, tapas. May is my favorite month (9.5/10): 59-82°F, 15% rain, jacarandas in bloom everywhere, Feria de Abril (2 weeks after Easter) for the flamenco festival par excellence. July-August drop to 6.9/10 due to dry heatwave (72-99°F, sometimes 113°F): Sevillanos living in air conditioning, going out after 10 PM, the city near-dead during the day. From December to February, expect 43-66°F and 18-21% rain: mild, ideal for visiting without crowds, oranges falling in the streets. Holy Week (Semana Santa) in April is the absolute tourist peak: prices x4-x5, processions every night. Alcazar (booking required), Cathedral and Giralda, Plaza de España (María Luisa Park), Triana on the other side of the Guadalquivir for authentic flamenco. Try a Cruzcampo with serranitos. March-April and October-November are the best tourism/weather compromises.
