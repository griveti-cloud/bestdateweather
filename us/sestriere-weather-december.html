Context

Our verdict

Sestrière is part of the Milky Way (Voie Lactée), one of the largest Franco-Italian cross-border areas: 250 miles of pistes connected with Montgenèvre, Sansicario and Claviere. Host of the Turin 2006 Winter Olympics. July-August are my favorite months (8.0/10): 43-66°F, 26-30% rain in afternoon storms, good for hiking and mountain bike. From December to April, expect 12/37°F: ski season, reliable snow thanks to altitude (6,700 ft, one of the highest Italian resorts). Dry continental climate, pistes mainly north-facing. From May and October-November, it's the slow shoulder season: resort semi-closed. Turin 1h30 by car, Briançon 1h via the Montgenèvre pass. The resort has aged architecturally, less charming than Cortina or Bormio, but the skiing remains excellent and the passes affordable.
