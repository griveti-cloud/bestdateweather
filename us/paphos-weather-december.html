Context

Our verdict

Paphos (Pafos), former royal capital of Cyprus in the south-west of the island: 35,000 inhabitants. UNESCO 1980 for its exceptionally rich archaeological site. According to Greek mythology, birthplace of Aphrodite (the goddess of love would have been born from the sea foam at Petra tou Romiou 16 miles from Paphos). European Capital of Culture 2017. June is my favorite month (9.8/10): 66-84°F, 1% rain, sea at 73°F, ideal temperatures. May-October stay excellent (8.6-9.4/10). July-August drop to 6.9/10 due to the heat (75-90°F). From November to March, expect 46-66°F and 13-37% rain: mild for the Mediterranean. Highlights: Paphos Archaeological Park (UNESCO 1980, former Nea Paphos = capital of Cyprus 4th century BC to 4th century AD, destroyed by earthquake 332 AD, vestiges still being excavated, main attraction = exceptional Roman mosaics in the villas House of Dionysus, House of Aion, House of Theseus, House of Orpheus, 3rd-4th century AD, among the best preserved in the Mediterranean world), Tombs of the Kings (UNESCO, Hellenistic-Roman necropolis 4th century BC with monumental tombs sculpted in the rock, similar to Petra but more modest), Byzantine Castle of Saracen (13th century on the medieval port of Kato Pafos), Saint Paul/Pierre Pillar Church (where Saint Paul was allegedly flogged on the column in 45 AD according to Christian tradition, place of pilgrimage), Saint Salomon Catacombs (early Christian catacombs), Baths of Aphrodite 22 miles away in Polis (natural pool in the rock where the goddess would have bathed according to mythology, 30 min easy trail hike), Petra tou Romiou 16 miles away (legendary birthplace of Aphrodite, beach with iconic rocks, iconic landscape of Cyprus), Avakas Gorge (spectacular gorge 2h round trip hike), Akamas Peninsula (wild peninsula at the extreme west, hikes with endemic Cyprus tulip orchids, Lara beach turtle nesting), Coral Bay (main sand beach of the region, turquoise water), Lara Beach (protected green turtle nesting beach May-August). Excursions: Limassol 1h away, Troodos Mountains 1h30 away (mountains with UNESCO painted churches), Kykkos Monastery 1h30 away, Larnaca 1h30 away, Nicosia 2h away. Try Cypriot meze (institution), grilled halloumi, souvla skewers, sheftalies, commandaria (ancient sweet wine), zivania (grape eau-de-vie), Cypriot coffee. Paphos airport (PFO) with direct European charter flights, one of the main airports for the south-west coast of Cyprus.
