Context

Our verdict

Dushanbe, capital of Tajikistan in Central Asia: 850,000 inhabitants at 2,600 ft altitude at the foot of the Pamir. Former Soviet republic of Central Asia (1924-1991), poorest country of the former USSR, bordering Afghanistan-China-Kyrgyzstan-Uzbekistan. Modernised but little touristic capital, base for Pamir Highway expeditions. September is my favorite month (9.3/10): 57-86°F, 2% rain, this is the ideal dry season. June stays excellent (8.9/10). From May to October, it's the tourist high season: 55-99°F. July-August drop to 6.9/10 due to the heatwave (64-97°F). From November to March, expect 27/61°F and 26-45% rain: continental cold, snow possible. Highlights: Statue of Ismoil Somoni (the highest monument in Tajikistan, tribute to the founder of the Samanid dynasty 9th-10th century recognised as spiritual father of the Tajik nation), Dushanbe Flag (one of the highest flagpoles in the world 540 ft, flag 200 ft × 98 ft), National Museum of Antiquities (reclining Buddha statue 43 ft discovered in 1966 in a cave, iconic view), Mehrgon Bazaar (traditional central market, Pamir dried fruits, spices), Haji Yakoub Mosque, Doustii Square, Palace of Nations (presidential palace). Essential Tajikistan excursions: Pamir Highway ("M41", one of the most legendary roads in the world, 780 miles from Khorog to Osh Kyrgyzstan through the Pamir high plateaus 15,300 ft Ak-Baital pass, lunar-Himalayan landscapes, 4x4 expedition 7-15 days), Lake Iskanderkul 2h away (alpine lake named after Alexander the Great passed here, emerald water at 7,200 ft), Wakhan Valley (Afghan border, Hindu Kush mountains visible to the south, unique Pamiri Ismaili communities), Khujand (2nd city, 2500 years old). Try osh plov (carrot-mutton rice, Tajik national dish), laghman (noodles), shashlik (skewers), Tajik naan (patterned flatbread), green tea with jam. Logistics: Dushanbe airport (DYU), electronic visa available.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

When is the best time to visit Dushanbe?
It depends on the activity. For skiing: January (ski score 4.3/10). For hiking/suiner: October (8.9/10, 22.0°F).
Can you ski in Dushanbe in winter?
Yes, it's peak ski season. Average ski score December–February: 3.8/10. Cold temperatures (9°F max in January) and frequent snowfall.
Is it warm in Dushanbe in suiner?
In October, temperatures average 22.0°F. Ideal for hiking and outdoor activities.
What is the wettest month in Dushanbe?
January is the wettest month with January has the lowest climate score of the year.
