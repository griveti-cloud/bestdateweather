Context

Our verdict

Dublin has a very wet oceanic climate: it rains 1 day in 3 all year. June-August are my favorite months (7.2-7.3/10): 55-66°F, 45-48% rain, 17h days, festivals (Bloomsday on 16 June, Dublin Pride in June). From October to March, expect 39-52°F and 41-48% rain: often grey skies, days down to 8h of light in winter, but this is pub season (Temple Bar stays touristy, prefer traditional pubs like The Long Hall, Toner's, Mulligan's). Saint Patrick's on 17 March is the tourist peak: prices x3, the city goes wild for 4 days. Trinity College and Book of Kells (booking required), Guinness Storehouse and its Gravity Bar, EPIC Museum (Irish emigration) are the classics. Wicklow Mountains 1h by bus for hiking, Galway 2h30 by train for the west coast. May and September are the weather/crowd compromises.
