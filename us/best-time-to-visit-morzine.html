Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Morzine, in the Portes du Soleil: 3000 inhabitants at 3,300 ft, one of France's largest village resorts (wooden chalet architecture, no concrete blocks), linked to Avoriaz by cable car. August is my favorite month (8.2/10): 55-73°F, 40% rain, hiking and MTB (Morzine = France's downhill MTB capital with Crankworx). From June to September, expect 41-73°F: very active summer season (one of the most dynamic Alpine resorts in summer), Pass'Portes du Soleil MTB in June with 375 miles of trails. From December to March, expect 25/43°F and 42-47% precipitation often as snow: prime ski season. Pistes: 375 miles Portes du Soleil (12 linked resorts including Morzine + Avoriaz + Châtel on the French side, Champéry + Champoussin + Les Crosets on the Swiss side), single ski pass, one of the world's largest ski areas. Pleney and Nyon-Pointe de Nyon = 2 main slopes at Morzine, accessible by cable car from the village center. Avoriaz 15 min by cable car (village-resort built in wood in 1966 by Jacques Labro, car-free, horse-drawn shuttles). Crowd: very many Brits (40% of winter clientele), families, snowboarders. More accessible than Megève price-wise. Hotels: Hôtel Le Tremplin, La Bergerie, Mountain Mavericks (British chalets) — $110-$550/night. Try tartiflette, raclette, fondue, diots aux pommes. Logistics: Morzine 1h15 from Geneva by car/taxi (tourist transfers common), 50 miles from the Swiss border.

How we calculate the score

For mountain destinations, we calculate two scores per month — skiing and hiking — and display the higher of the two.

⛷️Ski score DEC → MAY
  • 40% snow cover (slope T° corrected by lapse rate -3.6°F/1000ft)
  • 40% winter thermal comfort (23–41°F optimal)
  • 20% sunshine
  • Off-season penalty: ×0.70 adjacent months · ×0.35 outside season
🥾Hiking score JUN → SEP
  • 40% temperature (50–72°F optimal)
  • 40% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 20% sunshine

Frequently asked questions

When is the best time to visit Morzine-Avoriaz?
It depends on the activity. For skiing: January (ski score 8.7/10). For hiking/suiner: January (8.7/10, 4.0°F).
Can you ski in Morzine-Avoriaz in winter?
Yes, it's peak ski season. Average ski score December–February: 8.4/10. Cold temperatures (4°F max in January) and frequent snowfall.
Is it warm in Morzine-Avoriaz in suiner?
In January, temperatures average 4.0°F. Ideal for hiking and outdoor activities.
What is the wettest month in Morzine-Avoriaz?
November is the wettest month with November has the lowest climate score of the year.
