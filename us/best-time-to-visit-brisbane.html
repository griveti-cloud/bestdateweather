G
Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Brisbane is calmer than Sydney or Melbourne: capital of Queensland without the crowds, river that meanders, subtropical climate. November is my favorite month (8.7/10): 64-82°F, 38% rain in brief showers, 13h days. From December to March, expect 68-86°F with 40-54% precipitation: subtropical wet season, sometimes severe floods (2011, 2022). From June to August (austral winter), plan 50-72°F and 17-23% rain: mild climate, ideal for South Bank, Story Bridge climb, Mount Coot-tha. Brisbane mainly serves as a base: Gold Coast 1h away (Surfers Paradise, Burleigh), Sunshine Coast 1h30 away (Noosa, Glass House Mountains), Stradbroke Island and Moreton Island by ferry for dolphins/dugongs. The Fortitude Valley and West End café scene deserves exploration. Direct flights from Singapore, Doha, LA.

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 72-82°F, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 61°F + tmax ≥ 79°F)
