Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Formentera is Ibiza's quiet sister: no airport, access only by ferry from Ibiza (30 minutes). Flat island (12 miles long), ideal by bicycle. Caribbean-style turquoise beaches. July is my favorite month (9.7/10): 75-79°F, 1% rain, sea at 77°F. From July to August, the invasion is total: luxury Italians and French, prices x3, restaurants overflowing. Famous beaches: Ses Illetes (regularly ranked among Europe's most beautiful), Cala Saona, Migjorn. From November to March, expect 55-66°F and 17-28% rain: the island almost completely hibernates, 80% of restaurants and hotels close. May-June and September-October are the real sweet spots: still summer weather, pleasant cycling, and the chance to enjoy the beaches almost alone. No nightlife (unlike Ibiza): this is a contemplation destination, not a party one. Faro de la Mola for sunset.

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 72-82°F, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 61°F + tmax ≥ 79°F)
