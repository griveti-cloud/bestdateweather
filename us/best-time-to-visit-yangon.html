Frequently asked questions

January is the best month with 88°F highs, 68°F lows, 10.8h of sunshine and 8% rainy days. The months of January & February offer comparable conditions.
Yangon averages 10.8 sunshine hours per day in January. It is the sunniest month with a climate score of 6.9/10.
July is the most unfavourable month with 99% rainy days and the lowest climate score of the year.
In January, overnight lows reach 68°F, while afternoon highs average 88°F.
In winter (December-February), Yangon scores 6.9/10 on average — Acceptable conditions for cultural visits.. The best month overall remains January with a score of 6.9/10.
July is the most difficult month to visit Yangon, with 99% rainy days. Climate conditions are at their lowest of the year during this period.
