What to expect in November

Autumn in Cabo San Lucas is often underrated: 29°F, golden light and declining crowds. An interesting window. Rain is virtually absent (4% of days) — ideal for planning without a backup. A good balance between weather and crowds, even if March (9.5/10) remains theoretically better.
