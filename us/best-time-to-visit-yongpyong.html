Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Yongpyong, South Korean ski resort in Gangwon province 125 miles east of Seoul: 5000 permanent inhabitants (15,000 in ski season). Host of the PyeongChang Winter Olympics 2018 (alpine slalom + giant slalom events, opening-closing ceremonies 3.7 miles away at Pyeongchang Olympic Plaza), massively modernised for the Olympics (KTX high-speed train lines directly from Incheon Seoul airport in 2h). Pistes: 17 miles Yongpyong Resort (modest vs Alps: 375 miles 3 Vallées, but one of the largest areas in South Korea), altitude 750-4,800 ft, vertical drop 2,300 ft. June-July are my favorite months (7.6-7.9/10): 55-75°F, 43-57% rain, ideal Korean summer temperatures. Yongpyong never tops 7.9/10 (Korean continental humid climate). From June to September, it's the summer high season: 46-77°F, Mount Balwangsan hiking, 36-hole golf, water parks, active summer-tourism resort atmosphere. From December to March, expect 14/45°F and 16-28% precipitation often as snow: this is ski high season. iconic pistes: Rainbow Slope (Rainbow 1-2-3-4, iconic exposed descent of the 2018 Olympics, one of the most difficult descents in South Korea), Mega Green (family), Pink Slope. Particularities: Yongpyong = filming base of the famous K-drama "Winter Sonata" (Winter 2002) with Bae Yong-Joon = cult Korean romantic drama that popularised Yongpyong throughout East Asia and triggered the "Hallyu" = Korean cultural wave 2003-2010, place of pilgrimage for Japanese and Chinese fans, statue of the Winter Sonata scene to see at the foot of the slopes. Off-piste: forbidden in South Korea (protected national parks). Modern Snowpark for freestyle. Winter activities: alpine ski, snowboard, cross-country ski, snowmobile, ice luge, Korean hot spring spa experiences (jjimjilbang) traditional must in winter. No "aurora season" at this latitude (37°N). Audience: Koreans in the majority (Seoul 2h by KTX), Chinese and Japanese tourism groups for the Winter Sonata drama, few Western Europeans (prefer Niseko Hokkaido Japan or the Alps). Hotels: Dragon Valley Hotel, Yongpyong Resort Tower Condominium, prices $110-$330/night cheaper than Niseko. Excursions: Pyeongchang Olympic Plaza 3.7 miles away (2018 Olympics memorial with Olympic Cauldron), Alpensia Resort 3 miles away (host of nordic + biathlon 2018 Olympics events), Gangneung 1h away (Sea of Japan coastal city, host of 2018 Olympics ice events, beaches in summer), DMZ North Korea border 4h by bus ("Demilitarized Zone", essential excursion from Seoul, the most militarised border in the world, Joint Security Area JSA Panmunjeom where Trump met Kim Jong-un in 2019), Seoul 2h by KTX (capital, already covered by BDW). Try bibimbap (Korean mixed-rice national dish), bulgogi (grilled beef), galbi (grilled ribs), kimchi (omnipresent fermented cabbage), tteokbokki (spicy rice cakes), soju (Korean national alcohol), makgeolli (fermented rice wine). Yangyang airport (YNY) 1h away, alternatively Incheon Seoul airport (ICN) 2h by KTX high-speed train.

How we calculate the score

For mountain destinations, we calculate two scores per month — skiing and hiking — and display the higher of the two.

⛷️Ski score DEC → MAY
  • 40% snow cover (slope T° corrected by lapse rate -3.6°F/1000ft)
  • 40% winter thermal comfort (23–41°F optimal)
  • 20% sunshine
  • Off-season penalty: ×0.70 adjacent months · ×0.35 outside season
🥾Hiking score JUN → SEP
  • 40% temperature (50–72°F optimal)
  • 40% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 20% sunshine

Frequently asked questions

When is the best time to visit Yongpyong?
It depends on the activity. For skiing: February (ski score 8.7/10). For hiking/suiner: February (8.7/10, 1.0°F).
Can you ski in Yongpyong in winter?
Yes, it's peak ski season. Average ski score December–February: 8.4/10. Cold temperatures (-2°F max in January) and frequent snowfall.
Is it warm in Yongpyong in suiner?
In February, temperatures average 1.0°F. Ideal for hiking and outdoor activities.
What is the wettest month in Yongpyong?
August is the wettest month with August has the lowest climate score of the year.
