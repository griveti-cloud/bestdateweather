Context

Our verdict

Monaco, sovereign principality of 1 sq miles: 38,000 inhabitants (one of the world's smallest countries), enclaved in France on the Italian border, ruled by the Grimaldi dynasty since 1297. July-August are my favorite months (9.5/10): 70-82°F, 11-14% rain, sea at 75°F, Formula 1 Grand Prix late May (the most prestigious on the calendar, urban circuit race at 180 miles/h in the narrow streets, hotel prices x10 during the event). From June to September, it's the tourist high season: 63-82°F, hotel prices x3-x5 throughout the season, events (Yacht Show late September, ATP tennis tournament April). From December to February, expect 39-55°F and 19-25% rain: mild for France, more peaceful atmosphere, prices halved. Highlights: Casino de Monte-Carlo (Belle Époque, 1863, Place du Casino with Hôtel de Paris and Café de Paris, casino access $20 + dress code required), Prince's Palace (changing of the guard every day at 11:55, apartments open to the public outside season) and Cathedral (where Grace Kelly and Rainier III are buried), Oceanographic Museum (Cousteau, founded by Prince Albert I, perched on the cliff), Exotic Garden (giant cacti, Observatory Cave), Japanese Garden, Casino Café de Paris (free slot machines, more accessible atmosphere). Old Monaco (the "Rock") with its Fort Antoine. Sport: AS Monaco football at Stade Louis II. Try barbagiuan (chard-stuffed pastry), socca (neighbouring Niçoise pancake), pissaladière. Logistics: Monaco 25 min from Nice by train (spectacular underground station), no airport (Nice 25 min by helicopter for $165/person).
