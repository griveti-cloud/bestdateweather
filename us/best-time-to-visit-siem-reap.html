Frequently asked questions

January is the best month with 88°F highs, 72°F lows, 10.5h of sunshine and 7% rainy days. The months of January & December offer comparable conditions.
Siem Reap averages 10.5 sunshine hours per day in January. It is the sunniest month with a climate score of 8.3/10.
September is the most unfavourable month with 91% rainy days and the lowest climate score of the year.
In January, overnight lows reach 72°F, while afternoon highs average 88°F.
In winter (December-February), Siem Reap scores 8.2/10 on average — Acceptable conditions for cultural visits.. The best month overall remains January with a score of 8.3/10.
