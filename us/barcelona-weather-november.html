What to expect in November

Autumn in Barcelona is often underrated: 17°F, golden light and declining crowds. An interesting window. Expect rain on 26% of days — a light raincoat in the bag is recommended. Not the best window, but acceptable if dates are constrained. July (9.5/10) is significantly better if possible.
