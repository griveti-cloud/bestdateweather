Context

Our verdict

Lyon shows its best face from June to September. The Saône quays come alive until midnight, the Vieux-Lyon terraces overflow. July (9.2/10) and August (9.1) are my favorite months: 63-82°F, 14h of light, 21% rain in midsummer. Dry and hot, without Marseille's furnace. From December to February, the grey settles in, 5h of sun and temperatures clinging to the ground. Except on 8 December for the Festival of Lights, when the city transforms for 4 days. September stays a good late option: still mild temperatures (55-73°F), Beaujolais announcing its release, return-to-school less crowded than in tourist capitals. For bouchons and winter cuisine, November works too: moderate rain, quieter atmosphere.
