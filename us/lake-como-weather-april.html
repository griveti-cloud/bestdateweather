The month in detail

What to expect in April

Spring marks the start of the good season in Lake Como. Temperatures rise (17.0°F) and tourists have not yet arrived en masse. Expect rain on 42.0% of days — a light raincoat in the bag is recommended. Not the best window, but acceptable if dates are constrained. July (8.4/10) is significantly better if possible.

Context

Our verdict

Lake Como, in an inverted-Y shape, 31 miles long, 1,300 ft deep (one of the deepest in Europe). George Clooney and Hollywood villas (Bellagio especially). July-August are my favorite months (8.4/10): 70-82°F, 43-50% rain in afternoon storms, swimming in the lake (water at 75°F). From December to February, expect 39-52°F and 24-30% rain: often grey sky (mist over the lake), many villas and restaurants close. Highlights: Como (Duomo, funicular to Brunate for the view), Bellagio (pearl of the lake, gardens of Villa Melzi and Villa Serbelloni), Varenna (charming village, Villa Monastero), Tremezzo (Villa Carlotta, Italian gardens), Villa del Balbianello at Lenno (used in Star Wars Episode II and James Bond Casino Royale). Navigazione Lago di Como boats serving all the villages. Try risotto al pesce persico, missoltini (dried fish), polenta uncia, local cheeses (Bitto). Milan 1h by train, Switzerland (Lugano) 30 min by car. May-June and September-October are the best crowd/weather compromises.
