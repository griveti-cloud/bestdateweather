What to expect in March

Spring marks the start of the good season in Bahamas. Temperatures rise (26.0°F) and tourists have not yet arrived en masse. Expect rain on 27.0% of days — a light raincoat in the bag is recommended. A good balance between weather and crowds, even if March (8.7/10) remains theoretically better.

Context

Our verdict

The Bahamas is the archipelago of 700 islands 50 miles from Florida: Nassau (capital), Paradise Island (Atlantis Resort), Exumas (swimming pigs). March is my favorite month (8.7/10): 72-79°F, 27% rain, sea at 77°F, end of the American high season. From June to November, it's the cyclone season: 58-66% rainfall, real hurricane risk (Dorian 2019 category 5 devastated the Abacos). From December to May, expect 70-82°F: North American high season, hotel prices x2-x3. Highlights: Nassau (capital, Atlantis Resort at Paradise Island, Queen's Staircase, Pirates Museum), Junkanoo (Bahamian carnival, 26 December and 1 January). Islands to explore: Exumas (Pig Beach for the viral swimming pigs, Thunderball Grotto where James Bond filmed, Iguana Cay), Eleuthera (Pink Sands Beach, Glass Window cliffs), Andros (the largest island, Tongue of the Ocean diving, blue holes), Bimini (the closest to Florida, Hemingway lived here). Cruisers: Nassau is one of the most frequented ports in the world by cruises (several liners/day). Try conch fritters/salad (lambis), Bahama Mama cocktail, rock lobster. Currencies: USD accepted everywhere (Bahamian dollar 1:1 parity).
