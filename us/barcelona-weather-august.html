Context

Our verdict

Barcelona is living its overtourism crisis: 12 million visitors/year for 1.6 million inhabitants, anti-tourist protests in 2024. The city council has banned Airbnb rentals in residential zones by 2028. June stays my favorite month (9.3/10): 64-79°F, 19% rain, terraces until midnight, festivals (Sónar, Primavera). July-August are hot and packed (70-84°F, beaches crowded). From December to March, expect 39-63°F and 14-27% rain: a paradoxically excellent period for the classics (Sagrada Familia, Park Güell, Barri Gòtic) without crowds, without heat. The Sagrada Familia is due to complete its central tower in 2026. The façade construction is set for later. Park Güell now requires booking. The markets (Boqueria, Sant Antoni) stay alive on weekday mornings. May and September-October are the best compromises. Sitges 30 minutes by train to escape the throngs.
