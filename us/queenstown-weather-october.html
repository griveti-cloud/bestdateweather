Local context

What to expect in October

Off-season for skiing Queenstown — lifts are closed. Consider hiking, mountain biking or summer activities at altitude. With 51% of rainy days, always have indoor alternatives planned.

Seasonality

Seasonal specificities in October

October installs alpine autumn. Spectacular larch colors (brilliant gold) and beeches in valleys. First snow at altitude. Huts gradually close. Hiking still accessible in mid-mountain. Deer rut in forests. Rock-bottom rates between seasons.
