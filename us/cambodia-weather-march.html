What to expect in March

March is peak dry season in Cambodia. The air is warm and humidity more bearable than in the rainy season. Rain risk remains low (19% of days), leaving good room for outdoor activities. Not the best window, but acceptable if dates are constrained. December (8.2/10) is significantly better if possible.
