What to expect in April

Spring marks the start of the good season in Trinidad. Temperatures rise (31°F) and tourists have not yet arrived en masse. Expect rain on 30% of days — a light raincoat in the bag is recommended. A good balance between weather and crowds, even if January (9.6/10) remains theoretically better.
