Pondicherry (Puducherry since 2006), former French trading post in south-east India on the Coromandel coast: 245,000 inhabitants. French trading post from 1674 to 1962 (288 years, the longest continuous French presence in India, exchanged with the Indians at independence in 1962). Unique tropical-colonial atmosphere with a preserved French quarter, signs in French, French cafés-crêperies, old street names in French (Rue Romain Rolland, Rue Suffren). Model city of Auroville nearby (community utopia). February is my favorite month (9.1/10): 73-84°F, 10% rain, this is the ideal dry season. January stays excellent (8.8/10). From November to March, it's the tourist high season: 72-86°F, sea at 79°F. From April to June, expect 81-93°F: pre-monsoon heatwave. From July to October, it's the south-west then north-east monsoon: 45-66% rainfall, rough sea, tropical storms. Highlights: French quarter ("White Town", geometrically gridded à la française, yellow-and-white-colored houses with balconies, bougainvilleas, pedestrian streets), Promenade (Goubert Avenue along the sea, 1 miles, 13 ft Gandhi statue, Mediterranean evening atmosphere), Sri Aurobindo Ashram (created 1926 by the Indian philosopher-revolutionary Sri Aurobindo and his French companion "The Mother" Mirra Alfassa from Paris, world-famous spiritual site), Auroville 7 miles to the north ("City of Dawn", utopian community founded 1968 by The Mother with UNESCO, 3000 inhabitants of 60 nationalities living without money, religion or government, Matrimandir = 95 ft geodesic golden sphere = the spiritual center, limited access), Sacred Heart of Jesus Church (19th-century Gothic with iconic yellow façade), Notre-Dame des Anges, Notre-Dame de la Conception, Goubert Market (spices, fruits, flowers), Tamil quarter ("Black Town" colorful and chaotic, contrast with the gridded White Town), Paradise Beach and Auroville Beach (9 miles away, cleaner than the urban Pondi beach). Try Franco-Tamil fusion cuisine (famous in Pondicherry): Pondicherry fish curry (with Creole influences), French-style cassoulet in some restaurants, butter croissants, French coffee that stands up, French baguette, Indian wines. Logistics: Chennai airport (MAA) 2h30 by bus/taxi, alternative Pondicherry airport (PNY) with limited domestic flights.

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 72-82°F, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 61°F + tmax ≥ 79°F)
