Context

Our verdict

Oslo is one of Europe's most natural capitals: forests 15 minutes away (Nordmarka), 40 islands in the Oslofjord. June-July are my favorite months (7.6-7.7/10): 54-70°F, 40-42% rain, 18h days, swimming at the fjord islands (Hovedøya by ferry from Aker Brygge), festivals (Øyafestivalen early August). From December to February, expect 23/36°F and 35-41% precipitation (often snow): cross-country skiing direct from the center (Holmenkollen), aurora borealis possible some winters. Vigeland Park (sculptures), Munch Museum (relocated in 2021), Opera House whose roof you walk up, Bygdøy for the museums (Viking, Fram, Kon-Tiki) are the classics. Oslo stays very expensive: restaurant meal $33-$55, beer $11-$13. May-June and September are the real sweet spots price/weather. Bergen 6h by train (one of Europe's most beautiful rail journeys).
