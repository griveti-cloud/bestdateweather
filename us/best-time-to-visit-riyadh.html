Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Riyadh (Ar-Riyad), capital of Saudi Arabia on the central plateau of Najd at 2,000 ft altitude: 7 million inhabitants intra-muros, 9 million in urban area = the largest city in the Arab world by surface area. Historically ultra-conservative capital, massive tourist opening since 2019 (e-visa, lifting of driving ban for women 2018, opening of cinemas-concerts-international events under Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman MBS's Vision 2030). March is my favorite month (8.5/10): 61-82°F, 9% rain, this is the ideal dry season. November stays excellent (8.5/10). From November to March, it's the tourist high season: 48-82°F, dry climate and cool in the evening (sometimes 41°F in January night). From May to September drop to 3.1/10 due to extreme heatwave (79-111°F, sometimes 122°F+, arid desert climate). Highlights: Kingdom Center (Burj Al Mamlaka, iconic 990 ft skyscraper of Riyadh opened 2002, distinctive architecture with arch at the top, Skybridge accessible to visitors with 360° panorama over Riyadh at sunset), Al Faisaliyah Tower (other iconic skyscraper 880 ft with sphere at the top housing restaurant), Diriyah (UNESCO 2010, former historic capital of Saudi Arabia, cradle of the Al Saud dynasty 15th-19th century, At-Turaif District = mud brick quarter preserved in majestic restoration project "Diriyah Gate" 50 billion USD to make the "Arab Versailles"), Masmak Fort (historic fortress 1865 where Abdulaziz reconquered Riyadh 1902 founding the modern Saudi kingdom, museum), Souk Al Zal (traditional central souk with antiques, carpets, oud perfumes), Edge of the World 1h30 from Riyadh ("Jebel Fihrayn", spectacular 980 ft high cliffs in the desert, panorama over the infinite desert horizon), National Museum of Saudi Arabia (national museum, Islamic pre-Islamic art-archaeology), Red Sand Dunes (red dunes accessible 1h from Riyadh, 4x4 dune-bashing), Riyadh Season (annual winter festival October-March since 2019, international concerts Justin Bieber/Mariah Carey/Eminem played = major Saudi cultural opening, "sportwashing" and "artwashing" controversy). Saudi Arabia excursions: Al-Ula 1h flight (see dedicated review), Mecca 1h flight (forbidden to non-Muslims), Medina 1h flight (forbidden to non-Muslims). Safety: Riyadh globally safe, near non-existent crime thanks to strict sharia (very severe penalties), but travel subject to conservative dress code, alcohol strictly forbidden (prison sentences + flogging for foreigners), homosexuality forbidden (death penalty in theory). Try kabsa (Saudi spicy rice, national dish, Bedouin iconic), mandi (rice with lamb cooked in earth-buried oven), harees, jareesh, mutabbaq, Arabic coffee with Medjool dates, Ajwa dates (the most prestigious date in the Arab world, IGP). King Khalid Riyadh airport (RUH).

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 72-82°F, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 61°F + tmax ≥ 79°F)

What is the best time to visit Riyadh?
March is the best month with 82°F highs, 61°F lows, 10.7h of sunshine and 9.0% rainy days. The months of March & November offer comparable conditions.
How many hours of sunshine does Riyadh get in March?
Riyadh averages 10.7 sunshine hours per day in March. It is the sunniest month with a climate score of 9.4/10.

What are the temperatures at night in Riyadh in March?
In March, overnight lows reach 61°F, while afternoon highs average 82°F.
Is Riyadh good in winter?
In winter (December-February), Riyadh scores 8.8/10 on average — Acceptable conditions for cultural visits.. The best month overall remains March with a score of 9.4/10.
When should you avoid Riyadh?
June is the most difficult month to visit Riyadh, with June has the lowest climate score of the year. Climate conditions are at their lowest of the year during this period.
