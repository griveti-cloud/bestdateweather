Frequently asked questions

January is the best month with 72°F highs, 55°F lows, 12.7h of sunshine and 26% rainy days.
Hobart averages 12.7 sunshine hours per day in January. It is the sunniest month with a climate score of 8.5/10.
June is the most unfavourable month with 27% rainy days and the lowest climate score of the year.
In January, overnight lows reach 55°F, while afternoon highs average 72°F.
In winter (December-February), Hobart scores 8.2/10 on average — Acceptable conditions for cultural visits.. The best month overall remains January with a score of 8.5/10.
