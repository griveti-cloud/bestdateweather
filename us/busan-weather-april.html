What to expect in April

Spring marks the start of the good season in Busan. Temperatures rise (18.0°F) and tourists have not yet arrived en masse. Expect rain on 26.0% of days — a light raincoat in the bag is recommended. A good balance between weather and crowds, even if May (8.2/10) remains theoretically better.

Context

Our verdict

Busan is Korea's 2nd city and the country's largest port: 3.4 million inhabitants, 7 beaches, 4 fish markets. May-June are my favorite months (8.2/10): 55-79°F, 28-38% rain, good temperature for Haeundae beach without the summer crowds. From July to September, expect 68-86°F and 38-53% precipitation: typhoon season (Maemi 2003 left a mark), but also the Busan International Film Festival early October. From December to February, expect 27/46°F and 12-18% rain: dry cold, sea too cold for swimming but hiking on the Haeundae and Galmet trails. Highlights: Haeundae and Gwangalli for the urban beaches, Gamcheon Culture Village (the "Korean Santorini", colorful houses), Jagalchi Fish Market (the largest in Korea), Beomeosa (major Buddhist temple), Diamond Bridge at night. Try milmyeon (cold noodles specialty), seafood porridge, dwaeji gukbap.
