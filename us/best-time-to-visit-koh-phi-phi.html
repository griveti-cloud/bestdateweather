Koh Phi Phi, archipelago of 6 islands in the Andaman Sea in southern Thailand, halfway between Phuket and Krabi. The 2 inhabited islands: Phi Phi Don (4000 inhabitants, hotels and restaurants) and Phi Phi Leh (uninhabited, this is the Maya Bay from The Beach with Leonardo DiCaprio in 2000). Vertical limestone cliffs erupting from the turquoise sea. From January to March are my favorite months (6.9/10): 77-84°F, 23-31% rain, the ideal dry season. Koh Phi Phi never tops 6.9/10. From November to April, it's the tourist high season: 77-84°F, sea at 82°F, extreme crowds at Maya Bay and on Phi Phi Don, hotel prices x3-x4. From May to October, it's the monsoon: 65-88% rainfall, ferries often canceled in rough seas, many hotels close. Highlights: Maya Bay (Phi Phi Leh, iconic bay from The Beach, closed 2018-2022 to allow regeneration of coral destroyed by 5000 visitors/day, reopened under strict quotas since January 2022 = max 4000 visitors/day, swimming forbidden, 1h access limit, stay duration capped), Phi Phi Don Viewpoint (3 levels, 30 min stair climb from Tonsai Bay, iconic panorama over the hourglass-shaped Tonsai-Loh Dalum bays), Bamboo Island (Koh Mai Phai, white sand beach), Mosquito Island, Pileh Lagoon (emerald-green lagoon between cliffs), Loh Samah Bay (facing Maya Bay), Monkey Beach (on Phi Phi Don, sometimes aggressive wild macaques), Long Beach (quiet, view of Phi Phi Leh). Diving: cult sites around Phi Phi (Anemone Reef, Shark Point, King Cruiser Wreck, Hin Muang/Hin Daeng to the south), PADI training among the cheapest in the world. Tonsai Village = pedestrian center with restaurants/bars/clubs, no roads or cars on the island (only carts). Nightlife lively (Slinky Bar, Reggae Bar, fire shows). Try Thai green curry, fresh seafood BBQ, banana-Nutella pancakes from the market, fruit shakes. Logistics: ferries from Phuket (2h) or Krabi (1h30). No airport on the island.

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 72-82°F, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 61°F + tmax ≥ 79°F)
