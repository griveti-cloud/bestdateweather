Ras Al Khaimah (RAK), northernmost emirate of the United Arab Emirates 62 miles north-east of Dubai: 350,000 inhabitants. Calmer and more traditional emirate than Dubai/Abu Dhabi, preserved natural landscapes (Hajar mountains, beaches, deserts) = "nature" alternative to Dubai skyscrapers 1h by road. March is my favorite month (9.2/10): 68-82°F, 7% rain, this is the ideal dry season. December-February stay excellent (8.7-9.0/10). From November to April, it's the tourist high season: 61-90°F, sea at 72-77°F, hotel prices x2-x3. From May to October drop to 3.2-6.8/10 due to extreme heatwave (82-104°F, sometimes 122°F+ with oppressive coastal humidity). Highlights: Jebel Jais (6,300 ft, highest mountain in the UAE, accessible by 22 miles panoramic switchback road from the village of Jais, climate 50°F cooler than at sea level = unique in the UAE = snow possible exceptionally in winter, houses the Jebel Jais Flight = the longest zipline in the world 1.8 miles Guinness Book, speed 93 miles/h, signature experience of RAK), RAK Old Town (more local traditional old town than Dubai, preserved historic Arab atmosphere), National Museum of RAK (in former fort), Dhayah Fort (16th-century mountain fort, panorama), Al Jazeera Al Hamra (former pearl fishing village abandoned 1968 when the inhabitants moved after UAE unification, memorial atmosphere unique in the UAE, preserved architectural ghost), Desert dunes around RAK (4x4 dune-bashing more accessible and less touristic than in Dubai), RAK public beaches (white sand, turquoise water, less frequented than Dubai/Abu Dhabi), Suwaidi Pearl Farm (modernised pearl farm, demonstration of traditional pearl fishing which was the main historical pre-oil UAE industry), Khatt Hot Springs (hot springs), Wadi Shawka (spectacular wadi for hiking). Activities: Jebel Jais Flight zipline (signature, 650 AED ≈ $182), Jais via ferrata (the 1st via ferrata of the Gulf), Jebel Jais hiking (recent trails), desert safari 4x4 + night in Bedouin camp with dinner-belly dance show, Sea of Oman diving (RAK is on the Persian Gulf but 1h from Fujairah diving sites on the Sea of Oman). Hotels: Waldorf Astoria RAK, Rixos Bab Al Bahr, Hilton Marjan Island (5*+ all-inclusive on the artificial Al Marjan Island), prices 30-50% lower than Dubai for equal quality. Wynn Resort Casino = first legal casino in the UAE under construction on Al Marjan Island, opening planned 2027, major transformation of RAK to become a Vegas-style Middle East destination. Try Emirati machboos, harees, luqaimat (date beignets), Arabic coffee. Ras Al Khaimah airport (RKT) with European charter flights, alternative to Dubai DXB 1h by car.

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 72-82°F, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 61°F + tmax ≥ 79°F)
