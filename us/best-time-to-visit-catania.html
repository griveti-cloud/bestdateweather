Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Catania, 2nd city of Sicily: 310,000 inhabitants at the foot of the Etna (11,000 ft, the highest active volcano in Europe, in near-permanent eruption). Town rebuilt after the 1693 earthquake (60,000 deaths, 17 towns destroyed in eastern Sicily) in black Etna lava stone and white limestone, giving its characteristic baroque checkred black-white appearance. UNESCO 2002 (Val di Noto). June is my favorite month (9.7/10): 70-84°F, 7% rain, ideal temperatures before the heatwave. July-August drop to 6.9/10 due to the heat (75-91°F, frequent sirocco sometimes reaches 113°F in the open air with sand wind). September stays excellent (8.9/10). From November to March, expect 46-68°F and 21-31% rain: mild for the Mediterranean, snow on Etna in winter (skiing possible December-March on 2 stations Etna south and Etna north). Catania highlights: Piazza del Duomo (central baroque square, Fontana dell'Elefante = lava elephant Liotru = symbol of the city, Sant'Agata Cathedral dedicated to the patron saint), Via Etnea (main 2 miles street leading towards the Etna, perspectives on the volcano), Roman Theater (1st century BC in black lava, surprising conservation), Pescheria Quarter (the liveliest, daily fish market in the morning), Castello Ursino (13th century, castle of Frederick II), Bellini Theater (opera). Mount Etna (30 min by car, access Rifugio Sapienza 6,200 ft south side, cable car towards 8,200 ft, 4x4 jeep towards 9,500 ft south crater, mandatory accompaniment to go to the 11,000 ft summit, possibility of snowshoeing excursion in winter, spectacular view in eruption with night lava flows depending on the activity of the moment). Excursions: Taormina 1h away (already covered by BDW), Syracuse 1h away (UNESCO), Valley of the Temples Agrigento 2h30 away (UNESCO), Aeolian Islands by boat from Milazzo. Try pasta alla Norma (aubergine pasta with salted ricotta, iconic dish of Catania), arancini (fried rice balls, in Catania they are conical against round in Palermo = internal Sicilian war), Sicilian granita (typical breakfast), cannoli, Etna Rosso wines (Nerello Mascalese grape on volcanic soils). Catania-Fontanarossa airport (CTA).

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 72-82°F, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 61°F + tmax ≥ 79°F)
