Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Madagascar, the world's 4th largest island: 28 million inhabitants on 226,600 sq miles off the coast in the Indian Ocean, separated from Africa for 165 million years = record endemism (90% of fauna and flora unique worldwide, 100+ lemur species). Capital Antananarivo at 4,300 ft altitude. September is my favorite month (9.0/10): 54-75°F, 6% rain, end of dry season, ideal temperatures on the highlands. From April to November, it's the ideal dry season for most of the country (except permanently wetter east coast): 50-81°F, 5-36% rainfall depending on region. From December to March, it's the rainy season and cyclones: 45-75% rainfall, real cyclone risk on the east and north coasts (Cyclone Batsirai 2022, Freddy 2023 caused 100+ deaths). Regions to know: Antananarivo ("Tana", capital, see dedicated review), Avenue of the Baobabs near Morondava (west coast, 6 Adansonia grandidieri 98 ft baobabs, Madagascar's iconic, sunset iconic photo), Andasibe-Mantadia National Park (rainforest 3h from Tana, observation of Indri Indri = the largest lemur 9.5 kg, morning call audible at 2 miles), Ranomafana National Park (UNESCO 2007, another major lemur park with the "golden lemur"), Tsingy de Bemaraha (UNESCO, extremely sharp limestone formations in the heart of a national park, vertiginous hiking), Île Sainte-Marie (Nosy Boraha, east coast, former 17th-18th century pirate island, humpback whales July-September), Nosy Be in the north-west (main beach tourist island — see dedicated review), Nosy Iranja, Nosy Komba (paradise islands offshore), Antsirabe ("Madagascar's Vichy", thermal springs). Safety: Madagascar is broadly safe for tourism, vigilance in Tana and Toamasina (theft), red zone in the arid south-west "Triangle of Death" and some southern rural areas after dahalo (rural banditry). Try romazava (beef stew), ravitoto, mofo gasy (sweet morning fritter), THB (Three Horses Beer, local pride), Malagasy wine (Lazan'i Betsileo from the Ambalavao region), arranged rum (vanilla + fruits + clove).

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 72-82°F, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 61°F + tmax ≥ 79°F)
