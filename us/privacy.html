2. Data Collected

2.1 Navigation data (Google Analytics)

This site uses Google Analytics 4 to measure its audience. Data collected includes:

  • Pages visited and visit duration

This data is anonymized — no personal identifier is collected. IP addresses are truncated before transmission to Google.

2.2 Data not collected

This site does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any form data (there is no contact form with data submission).

3. Cookies

This site uses the following cookies:

  • _ga, _ga_XXXXXXXX — Google Analytics, 13-month duration. Anonymized audience measurement.
  • Partner cookies — only deposited if you click on a partner link and visit the partner site.

No advertising or profiling cookies are deposited directly by this site.

Refusing cookies

You can disable Google Analytics via the official extension: tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout. You can also manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.
