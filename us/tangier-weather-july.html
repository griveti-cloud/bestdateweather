Context

Our verdict

Tangier is Morocco's Mediterranean gateway: ferry from Tarifa in 1h, ferry to Algeciras, high-speed rail link with Casablanca and Rabat. July is my favorite month (9.6/10): 68-84°F, 1% rain, Atlantic breezes. August curiously drops to 6.9/10 in our data despite 70-86°F: European tourist peak, city overflowing (Moroccans abroad return en masse), inflated rates, packed beaches. June and September are the best months for swimming in calm. From December to March, expect 50-64°F and 25-38% rain: damp and windy, but the kasbah and the medina take on their most local face. The Café Hafa (Bowles, Burroughs, Stones) keeps its bohemian charm. The Caves of Hercules and Cap Spartel can be visited as a half-day trip. Asilah 28 miles to the south (white medina painted by artists) complements it well.
