Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Gudauri, main ski resort of Georgia on the Georgian Military Way 75 miles north of Tbilisi: 1500 inhabitants at 7,200 ft altitude at the foot of the Greater Caucasus massif. Resort developed in the Soviet era 1960 then massively modernised 2010-2025 (Emirati-Azerbaijani investments), become one of the rising ski resorts in Eurasia thanks to exceptional snow + low prices. August is my favorite month (7.6/10): 48-66°F, 31% rain, ideal temperatures for Caucasian alpine hiking. Gudauri never tops 7.6/10 (Caucasian alpine climate with narrow season). From June to September, it's the summer high season: 41-70°F, Georgian Caucasus hiking, mountain bike (Gudauri Bike Park summer), paragliding (one of the most famous spots in the Caucasus for paragliding with panoramic spot), Mt Kazbek 16,600 ft visible from Gudauri = 4-5 day alpine ascent reserved for confirmed mountaineers. From December to April, expect 14/32°F and 28-46% precipitation often as snow: ski high season (ski season among the longest in Europe November-May thanks to altitude 2200-10,800 ft). Pistes: 43 miles Gudauri (modest vs Alps: 375 miles 3 Valleys, but one of the largest areas in the Caucasus and the former Soviet bloc with Krasnaya Polyana Russia), altitude 2200-10,900 ft, vertical drop 3,600 ft, 8 lifts including modern Doppelmayr cable cars. Signature pistes: Sadzele (summit 10,800 ft, 360° panorama over Georgian-Russian Caucasus + Mt Kazbek), Bidara (main descent), Kobi (north side). Off-piste: Gudauri is one of the emerging heli-ski bases in Europe (authorised in Georgia unlike France-Germany where heli-ski is forbidden), operators Heliski Georgia + Caucasus Heliski offer virgin descents in the Greater Caucasus 8,200 ft+ vertical drop in 1 descent, prices $220-$550/day vs $880-$1,650 Alps equivalent = considered the best value for money for heli-ski in the world, attracts international freeriders and pros. Strengths: very low prices (passes $44-$55/day vs $77-$99 Alps, 4* hotels $88-$220/night vs $330-$880), snowfall among the best in Europe (3-16 ft of powder in season), few crowds compared to congested Alps. Audience: Georgians in the majority (Tbilisi 2h by car), Russians (before the Ukraine war 2022), Israelis (direct flight Tbilisi-Tel Aviv 4h), Polish-Czech-Ukrainian, few Westerners. Hotels: Quadrum Resort (4*+ opened 2018 modern architecture), Hotel Marco Polo, Rooms Hotel Kazbegi (4* in Stepantsminda 30 min from Gudauri to combine ski-cultural tourism), prices $88-$330/night. Excursions: Stepantsminda-Kazbegi 30 min away (mountain village with Gergeti Trinity Church 14th-century perched at 7,100 ft = signature visual of Georgia), Tbilisi 2h away (capital, already covered). Georgian Military Way = legendary Soviet-Tsarist road connecting Tbilisi to Vladikavkaz Russia via the Cross Pass 7,800 ft, crossing the most spectacular landscapes of the Georgian Caucasus, to travel by car-marshrutka. Try Adjarian khachapuri (signature cheese bread, open boat version with egg), khinkali (Georgian dumplings), mtsvadi (skewers), satsivi (chicken in walnut sauce), qvevri Saperavi/Rkatsiteli wines (Georgia = cradle of wine 8000 years ago), chacha (Georgian grape eau-de-vie).

How we calculate the score

For mountain destinations, we calculate two scores per month — skiing and hiking — and display the higher of the two.

  • 20% sunshine
  • Off-season penalty: ×0.70 adjacent months · ×0.35 outside season
🥾Hiking score JUN → SEP
  • 40% temperature (50–72°F optimal)
  • 40% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 20% sunshine

Frequently asked questions

When is the best time to visit Gudauri?
It depends on the activity. For skiing: March (ski score 9.0/10). For hiking/suiner: March (9.0/10, 1.0°F).
Can you ski in Gudauri in winter?
Yes, it's peak ski season. Average ski score December–February: 8.1/10. Cold temperatures (-3°F max in January) and frequent snowfall.
Is it warm in Gudauri in suiner?
In March, temperatures average 1.0°F. Ideal for hiking and outdoor activities.
What is the wettest month in Gudauri?
May is the wettest month with May has the lowest climate score of the year.
