Context

Our verdict

Bay of Islands, in the New Zealand Northland: 144 islands, turquoise water, historic base of the founding of New Zealand (Treaty of Waitangi 1840 between Maori and the British Crown). January is my favorite month (8.4/10): 63-75°F, 39% rain, subtropical austral summer (warmer climate than Auckland). From June to August (austral winter), expect 50-61°F with 37-43% rain: mild for NZ. Highlights: Waitangi Treaty Grounds (the country's founding place, marae, museum), Russell ("first capital", ferry from Paihia), Paihia (tourist center), Hole in the Rock (boat excursion, pierced rock arch), dolphin tours (dolphins year-round), Cape Reinga 3h away (the most accessible northernmost point of NZ, where Maori souls leave the world). 90 Mile Beach (actually 56 miles, accessible by 4×4 bus on the sand). Activities: sailing (catamaran day trip 100-150$, 3-day excursion 500$), diving (wreck of the Rainbow Warrior sunk by France at Matauri Bay), kayak. Try snapper (local fish), kiwi fruit, hokey pokey ice cream. Bay of Islands = tropical NZ destination for New Zealanders (January school holidays). Direct flight Auckland-Bay of Islands (Kerikeri) in 35 min, or 3h by road.
