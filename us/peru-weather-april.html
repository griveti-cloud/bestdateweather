What to expect in April

Spring marks the start of the good season in Peru. Temperatures rise (23°F) and tourists have not yet arrived en masse. Rain is virtually absent (4% of days) — ideal for planning without a backup. A good balance between weather and crowds, even if February (9.0/10) remains theoretically better.
