Context

Our verdict

Kigali surprises: the cleanest city in Africa, high security, advanced digital services. At 4,900 ft altitude, mild climate year-round (59-81°F constant). July is my favorite month (9.4/10): 4% rain, clear sky. From March to May and October-December, these are the marked rainy seasons (43-76% showers, especially in April), often in late afternoon. Morning visits remain workable. The Gisozi Genocide Memorial remains the obligatory passage to understand the country. The Kimihurura quarter concentrates the café and restaurant scene. Kigali is above all the base to reach the Volcanoes National Park (3h by road) where the gorilla treks take place (1500$ permit, to book 6+ months ahead). Lake Kivu at Gisenyi for relaxation. Hotel rates aligned with European capitals.
