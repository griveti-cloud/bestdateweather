Context

Our verdict

Hurghada lives off diving tourism and all-inclusive resorts. Red Sea, reefs accessible from the beach. Desert climate. April is my favorite month (9.6/10): 68-82°F, 0% rain, sea at 73°F. From June to September, expect 81-95°F: dry heat stays bearable by the sea but exhausting on desert excursions. Diving boats leave early (5am-6am) to return before noon. From December to February, plan 57-72°F and the wind can turn icy in the evening. Diving stays possible in 5mm wetsuit. The major sites: Giftun Islands (snorkelling), Abu Nuhas (wrecks), Elphinstone Reef for hammerhead sharks (experienced divers). The resort suffers from chaotic urban development of the 2000s: choose your resort carefully. Excursions to Luxor (5h by road, plan 1 night minimum on site).
