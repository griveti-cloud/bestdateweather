Context

Our verdict

Hakuba, Japanese valley in Nagano prefecture (Japanese Alps): 9000 inhabitants at 700-3,900 ft, town-valley that hosted the alpine events of the Nagano 1998 Winter Olympics. Composed of 10 distinct resorts connected by free shuttles with a single pass. June is my favorite month (7.6/10): 57-73°F, 58% rain in storms, Japanese alpine hiking, but Hakuba is above all a winter destination. From June to September, expect 48-84°F: calm summer season (above 4,900 ft pleasant climate, in valley bottom 2,300 ft hot-humid). From December to March, expect 21/46°F and 51-73% precipitation often as snow (10m/season, among the snowiest resorts in the world, legendary "Japan Powder" climate): ski high season. 10 Hakuba Valley resorts: Happo-One (the main one, 26 pistes, former 1998 Olympic descent piste), Hakuba 47 and Goryu (snowboarders), Tsugaike (family, snowpark), Iwatake, Sanosaka, Norikura, Cortina (Mount Yari view), Hakuba Highland, Goryu Toomi. 1 Hakuba Valley pass for 200+ km of pistes. Off-piste legendary: Hakuba Backcountry attracts freeriders from around the world (Australians in the majority, they who popularised the Japan Powder), guides mandatory (fatal avalanches every season). Onsen characteristic: Mimizuku no Yu, Daionji, Hakuba Shio no Michi (rotenburo outdoor baths with view of the snow-covered summits). Audience: Japanese in the majority, Australians (40% of foreigners), Singaporeans, growing Europeans. Hotels: Hakuba Tokyu, Hotel Gracery, traditional ryokans ($220-$1,650/night). Try miso ramen, local soba, Nagano sake. Logistics: Hakuba 4h by train+bus from Tokyo (Shinkansen Nagano + 1h bus), 1h by car from Nagano.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

When is the best time to visit Hakuba?
It depends on the activity. For skiing: February (ski score 8.9/10). For hiking/suiner: February (8.9/10, 4.0°F).
Can you ski in Hakuba in winter?
Yes, it's peak ski season. Average ski score December–February: 8.4/10. Cold temperatures (2°F max in January) and frequent snowfall.
Is it warm in Hakuba in suiner?
In February, temperatures average 4.0°F. Ideal for hiking and outdoor activities.
What is the wettest month in Hakuba?
August is the wettest month with August has the lowest climate score of the year.
