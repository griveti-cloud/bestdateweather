Hangzhou, capital of Zhejiang province in China: 12 million inhabitants 125 miles south-west of Shanghai, former capital of the Southern Song dynasty (1127-1279), nicknamed "paradise on earth" by Marco Polo who visited it in the 13th century ("the largest and noblest city in the world"). World headquarters of Alibaba (Jack Ma founded the company here in 1999). September is my favorite month (7.9/10): 72-84°F, 47% rain, ideal temperatures after the heatwave. From September to November, it's the tourist high season: 50-84°F, fragrant osmanthus flowers in September, red maples in November. From April to May, expect 55-79°F: spring with new Longjing tea leaves (the spring harvest before the "qing ming" festival 5 April gives the most prestigious and expensive tea). From June to August, expect 72-93°F with 49-62% rain: stifling heat typical of the delta. From December to February, expect 36-54°F and 23-39% rain: humid cold. Highlights: West Lake (Xi Hu, UNESCO, one of the most famous natural landscapes in China, model for traditional Chinese landscape paintings, walk around 9 miles or by dragon boat 2h, the most beautiful views: Leifeng Pagoda to the south, Su Causeway, Bai Causeway, 3 Pools Mirroring the Moon), Lingyin Temple ("Temple of the Soul's Retreat", one of the largest Chan/Zen monasteries in southern China, founded in 326), Six Harmonies Pagoda (Liuhe Ta, on the banks of the Qiantang river, view of the Qiantang Bore famous tidal wave phenomenon mid-September), Hefang Street (restored traditional pedestrian street, shops and street food), Longjing tea plantations ("Dragon Well", the most prestigious Chinese green tea, to visit in the Meijiawu or Longjing villages 30 min away). National Tea Museum. Try dongpo pork (sweet-savoury braised pork, Hangzhou dish invented by the poet Su Dongpo 11th century), beggar's chicken (chicken wrapped in lotus leaves and clay), Longjing tea.

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 72-82°F, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 61°F + tmax ≥ 79°F)
