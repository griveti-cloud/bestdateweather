Context

Our verdict

Durban is the other face of South Africa: less photographed than Cape Town but more tropical. Subtropical humid climate, sea always warm (72-79°F). January stays my favorite month (8.6/10) despite 38% rain: these showers are brief, the warm Indian Ocean catches up everything. From May to August (local austral winter), expect 54-75°F with 12-19% rain. Paradoxically, it's the best season for humpback whales offshore and for hiking in the Drakensberg 3h by road. The Golden Mile remains workable year-round (rare in Africa). From November to March, expect 30-45% precipitation in stormy showers: cyclonic season of neighbouring Mozambique, with sometimes repercussions. The strong Indian community gives Durban a separate cuisine (bunny chow, curry rotis), especially in Grey Street.
