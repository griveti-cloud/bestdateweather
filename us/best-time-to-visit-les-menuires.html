G
Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Les Menuires, in the Three Valleys: 6,100 ft, village resort built in the 60s-70s (concrete "ski machine" architecture), often considered "the cheapest of the 3 Valleys" against Courchevel/Méribel/Val Thorens, but with access to the largest ski area in the world (375 miles). July-August are my favorite months (7.9/10): 45-68°F, 37-38% rain in storms, alpine hiking, mountain bike, but Les Menuires is above all a winter resort (many hotels and restaurants closed in summer). From December to April, expect 14/43°F and 36-58% precipitation often as snow: ski high season, one of the snowiest resorts in the French Alps thanks to altitude. 3 Valleys pistes: 375 miles total, direct access from Les Menuires to Val Thorens (10 min by chairlift) and Méribel (20 min by cable car). La Masse = local peak (9,200 ft, Mont Blanc panorama). Audience: well-off French and Dutch families, snowboarders (Béranger Snowpark), contrast with festive Val Thorens and smart Méribel. Architecture: resort renovated since the 2000s with the Reberty 1850 chalet-architecture quarter (smarter), while the central La Croisette stays 70s. Hotels: Le Kaya (4*), L'Ours Blanc, Les Loges Vanoise (4*), $165-$770/night. Try tartiflette, savoyarde fondue, raclette, beaufort. Logistics: Les Menuires 2h15 from Lyon by car, Paris-Moûtiers train + 50-min bus.

How we calculate the score

For mountain destinations, we calculate two scores per month — skiing and hiking — and display the higher of the two.

⛷️Ski score DEC → MAY
  • 40% snow cover (slope T° corrected by lapse rate -3.6°F/1000ft)
  • 40% winter thermal comfort (23–41°F optimal)
  • 20% sunshine
  • Off-season penalty: ×0.70 adjacent months · ×0.35 outside season
🥾Hiking score JUN → SEP
  • 40% temperature (50–72°F optimal)
  • 40% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 20% sunshine

Frequently asked questions

When is the best time to visit Les Menuires?
It depends on the activity. For skiing: March (ski score 9.1/10). For hiking/suiner: March (9.1/10, 3.0°F).
Can you ski in Les Menuires in winter?
Yes, it's peak ski season. Average ski score December–February: 8.5/10. Cold temperatures (-1°F max in January) and frequent snowfall.
Is it warm in Les Menuires in suiner?
In March, temperatures average 3.0°F. Ideal for hiking and outdoor activities.
What is the wettest month in Les Menuires?
November is the wettest month with November has the lowest climate score of the year.
