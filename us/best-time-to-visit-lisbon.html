Best time by type of trip

🏖️BeachJuly–August — Cascais, Setúbal, Comporta. Water 20–22°C, refreshing Nortada breeze.
🌊Surf & BodyboardOctober–March — Nazaré (world-class giant waves), Peniche.

👨‍👩‍👧FamilyJune or September — swimmable sea, pleasant warmth, fewer crowds.
🥾Sintra & ArrábidaApril–May or October — before/after the heat, walkable trails.
