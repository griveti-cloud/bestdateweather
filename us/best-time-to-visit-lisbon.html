Frequently asked questions

July is the best month with 81°F highs, 64°F lows, 13.3h of sunshine and 3% rainy days. The months of July & August offer comparable conditions.
Lisbon averages 13.3 sunshine hours per day in July. It is the sunniest month with a climate score of 9.7/10.
January is the most unfavourable month with 26% rainy days and the lowest climate score of the year.
In July, overnight lows reach 64°F, while afternoon highs average 81°F.
In winter (December-February), Lisbon scores 7.3/10 on average — Acceptable conditions for cultural visits.. The best month overall remains July with a score of 9.7/10.
