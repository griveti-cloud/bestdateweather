Revelstoke, ski resort in British Columbia (Canada) in the Selkirk Mountains: 7500 inhabitants at 1,500 ft, historic stop town on the Canadian Pacific transcontinental railway (1885). Modern ski resort opened in 2007, already among the best in North America for freeriders. July-August are my favorite months (8.9/10): 55-79°F, 27-35% rain, Selkirk Mountains hiking, kayaking on Columbia River, mountain bike. From June to September, expect 39-79°F: very active summer season (Revelstoke is also a summer adventure hub: mountain bike, kayak, climbing, hiking). From December to March, expect 21/43°F and 56-61% precipitation often as snow (12-15m/season, among the snowiest in North America): ski high season. Revelstoke Mountain Resort pistes: 75 pistes, 5 lifts, record vertical drop in North America 5,600 ft (the largest on the continent), altitude 512-7,300 ft. Cat skiing and heli skiing: Revelstoke is one of the world spots for heli-ski (off-piste from helicopter, 30,000-330 ft of vertical drop/day with guide, prices 800-2000$/day). CMH Heli Skiing = world pioneer of heli-ski since 1965. Audience: advanced freeriders from around the world, ski professionals, few beginners (Revelstoke is known for its committed black and expert pistes). Hotels: Sutton Place Hotel Revelstoke Mountain Resort, Best Western Plus, private lodges nearby, 150-600 CAD/night. Glacier National Park nearby (Rogers Pass, 4-16 ft of snow/year, road regularly closed for avalanches). Try bison, Sockeye salmon, micro-brewery beers (Mt. Begbie Brewing). Logistics: Revelstoke 6h from Vancouver by car, 4h from Calgary, regional airport + transfer.

How we calculate the score

For mountain destinations, we calculate two scores per month — skiing and hiking — and display the higher of the two.

⛷️Ski score DEC → MAY
  • 40% snow cover (slope T° corrected by lapse rate -3.6°F/1000ft)
  • 40% winter thermal comfort (23–41°F optimal)
  • 20% sunshine
  • Off-season penalty: ×0.70 adjacent months · ×0.35 outside season
🥾Hiking score JUN → SEP
  • 40% temperature (50–72°F optimal)
  • 40% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 20% sunshine

Frequently asked questions

When is the best time to visit Revelstoke?
It depends on the activity. For skiing: March (ski score 9.1/10). For hiking/suiner: March (9.1/10, 6.0°F).
Can you ski in Revelstoke in winter?
Yes, it's peak ski season. Average ski score December–February: 7.6/10. Cold temperatures (0°F max in January) and frequent snowfall.
Is it warm in Revelstoke in suiner?
In March, temperatures average 6.0°F. Ideal for hiking and outdoor activities.
What is the wettest month in Revelstoke?
October is the wettest month with October has the lowest climate score of the year.
