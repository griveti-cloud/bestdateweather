Frequently asked questions

January is the best month with 90°F highs, 73°F lows, 11.0h of sunshine and 12% rainy days. The months of January & February offer comparable conditions.
Phnom Penh averages 11.0 sunshine hours per day in January. It is the sunniest month with a climate score of 6.9/10.
May is the most unfavourable month with 82% rainy days and the lowest climate score of the year.
In January, overnight lows reach 73°F, while afternoon highs average 90°F.
In winter (December-February), Phnom Penh scores 6.9/10 on average — Acceptable conditions for cultural visits.. The best month overall remains January with a score of 6.9/10.
May is the most difficult month to visit Phnom Penh, with 82% rainy days. Climate conditions are at their lowest of the year during this period.
