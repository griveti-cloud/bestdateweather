Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Sapporo is Japan's 5th city: capital of Hokkaido, 1.9 million inhabitants, modern grid-pattern city (rare in Japan), eponymous beer. July is my favorite month (8.2/10): 64-77°F, 40% rain, ideal temperatures when the rest of Japan is stifling, festivals (Sapporo Summer Festival late July, Sapporo Bon Odori in August). From December to March, expect 14/39°F and 40-52% precipitation (often abundant snow, Sapporo receives 16 ft of snow/year, among the snowiest in the world): this is the season of the Sapporo Snow Festival (1st week of February, Odori Park, giant ice sculptures, 2 million visitors), of skiing at Niseko (2h by bus), Furano (2h30) or closer Teine (1h). Highlights: Clock Tower (1878, symbol of the city), Mount Moiwa (cable car for the night view over the city, among the 3 best night panoramas in Japan with Hakodate and Nagasaki), Susukino (entertainment district), Hokkaido University (historic campus with poplar avenue), Otaru 30 min away (romantic canal, glassware). Try Sapporo-style miso ramen (at Sumire or Aji no Sanpei), Genghis Khan (grilled lamb, local dish), Sapporo beer (museum and brewery visitable). Direct Tokyo-Sapporo flight in 1h30, Hokkaido Shinkansen train (4h from Tokyo).

How we calculate the score

For mountain destinations, we calculate two scores per month — skiing and hiking — and display the higher of the two.

⛷️Ski score DEC → MAY
  • 40% snow cover (slope T° corrected by lapse rate -3.6°F/1000ft)
  • 40% winter thermal comfort (23–41°F optimal)
  • 20% sunshine
  • Off-season penalty: ×0.70 adjacent months · ×0.35 outside season
🥾Hiking score JUN → SEP
  • 40% temperature (50–72°F optimal)
  • 40% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 20% sunshine

Frequently asked questions

When is the best time to visit Sapporo?
It depends on the activity. For skiing: March (ski score 9.1/10). For hiking/suiner: March (9.1/10, 4.0°F).
Can you ski in Sapporo in winter?
Yes, it's peak ski season. Average ski score December–February: 8.1/10. Cold temperatures (-2°F max in January) and frequent snowfall.
Is it warm in Sapporo in suiner?
In March, temperatures average 4.0°F. Ideal for hiking and outdoor activities.
What is the wettest month in Sapporo?
November is the wettest month with November has the lowest climate score of the year.
