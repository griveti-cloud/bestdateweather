Hintertux, Austrian ski resort on the Hintertux Glacier in the Zillertal in Tyrol: 700 inhabitants at 1500-10,700 ft altitude. The only ski area in Austria open 365 days a year thanks to the 10,700 ft glacier, one of the rare European resorts allowing summer skiing in June-August. June is my favorite month (4.5/10): 43-59°F, 49% rain, ideal temperatures for summer skiing on the glacier. Hintertux never tops 4.5/10 (alpine glacial climate with limited season at lower altitude). From December to April, it's ski high season: 14/28°F, snow guaranteed. From May to October, summer skiing only on the 9,800 ft+ glacier (lower pistes closed), alpine hikes downhill. Pistes: 53 miles Hintertux Glacier domain, altitude 1500-10,700 ft, vertical drop 5,700 ft (one of the largest in Europe), 22 lifts. iconic pistes: Olperer (black glacial descent), Gefrorene Wand ("Frozen Wall", red piste at 10,700 ft, arrival point of the Gletscherbus 3 cable car), Schwarze Pfanne (7 miles glacial descent, one of the longest in Austria). Off-piste: Hintertux is one of the most respected off-piste bases in Austria thanks to the glacier which guarantees exceptional powder even in April-May. Snowpark Hintertux Pleisten = renowned freestyle. Cable cars: successive Gletscherbus 1-2-3 from 4,900 ft to Gefrorene Wand 10,700 ft in 25 min, one of the fastest alpine glacier systems. Sommerbergalm (intermediate station 6,900 ft with panoramic restaurants), Gletscher-Spalten ("glacier crevasses" in organized guided visit). Summer activities: skiing and snowboarding on the glacier (unique summer season in Europe), via ferrata, 5-lake Schlegeisspeicher hike, mountain bike, paragliding. Audience: Germans in the majority (Munich 2h by car), Austrians, Dutch, many British, national ski teams that train in summer (Lindsey Vonn, Marcel Hirscher came to Hintertux for pre-season preparation). Hotels: Hotel Hohenhaus (4*+), Hotel Neuhintertux, prices $165-$495/night in ski high season. Excursions: Mayrhofen 30 min away (Zillertal valley capital, festive après-ski), Innsbruck 1h away, Salzburg 2h away. Try Tiroler Gröstl, Kaiserschmarrn, Tyrolean IGP Speck, local plum Schnaps, glühwein.

How we calculate the score

For mountain destinations, we calculate two scores per month — skiing and hiking — and display the higher of the two.

⛷️Ski score DEC → MAY
  • 40% snow cover (slope T° corrected by lapse rate -3.6°F/1000ft)
  • 40% winter thermal comfort (23–41°F optimal)
  • 20% sunshine
  • Off-season penalty: ×0.70 adjacent months · ×0.35 outside season
🥾Hiking score JUN → SEP
  • 40% temperature (50–72°F optimal)
  • 40% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 20% sunshine

Questions

Frequently asked questions

When is the best time to visit Hintertux?
It depends on the activity. For skiing: July (ski score 8.5/10). For hiking/suiner: July (8.5/10, 9.0°F).
Can you ski in Hintertux in winter?
Yes, it's peak ski season. Average ski score December–February: 6.4/10. Cold temperatures (-5°F max in January) and frequent snowfall.
Is it warm in Hintertux in suiner?
In July, temperatures average 9.0°F. Ideal for hiking and outdoor activities.
What is the wettest month in Hintertux?
December is the wettest month with December has the lowest climate score of the year.
