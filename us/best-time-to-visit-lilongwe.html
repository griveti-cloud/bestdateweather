Lilongwe, capital of Malawi in southern Africa: 990,000 inhabitants. Planned capital built ex-nihilo in the 1970s (previous capital = Zomba and Blantyre remains the economic capital), calmer atmosphere than Lagos-Nairobi-Kampala. Country among the poorest in Africa but one of the most peaceful and stable, nicknamed "The Warm Heart of Africa" for the legendary hospitality of Malawians. September is my favorite month (9.5/10): 55-84°F, 4% rain, this is the ideal austral spring. July-October stay excellent (8.4-8.7/10). From May to October, it's the cool dry season: 45-84°F thanks to altitude (Lilongwe at 3,400 ft), ideal temperate-tropical altitude climate. From November to April, it's the rainy season: 27-72% rainfall, but stable temperatures 59-84°F, greener landscapes. Permanent climate: Lilongwe never tops 84°F in maximum average and rarely descends below 45°F in minimum average thanks to its altitude 3,400 ft, ideal "Eternal Spring" tropical altitude climate. Lilongwe highlights: Lilongwe Wildlife Center (wildlife sanctuary saved from poaching, tourist iconic of Lilongwe), Old Lilongwe / Old Town (local atmosphere, traditional central market), Capital City (new administrative quarter planned 1970s, large verdant avenues), Kamuzu Mausoleum (mausoleum of founding President Hastings Kamuzu Banda 1898-1997 dictator 1964-1994 then overthrown by first multiparty elections), Malawi Parliament, Tobacco Auction Floors ("Tobacco Exchange", Malawi is the largest African exporter of tobacco, spectacular morning auctions April-October economic iconic). Essential Malawi excursions = this is why one visits Malawi: Lake Malawi / Lake Niassa (UNESCO 1984, the 9th largest lake in the world 11,400 sq miles, the 3rd in Africa after Victoria and Tanganyika, nicknamed "Lake of Stars" by David Livingstone 1859, paradisiacal freshwater beaches golden sand + crystal water + 1000+ colorful endemic cichlid species = top world for aquariophily, Cape Maclear + Nkhata Bay + Likoma Island = main lake beach destinations), Liwonde National Park (accessible safari park, elephants + lions + leopards + hippopotamuses + buffaloes, reintroduction of cheetah 2017 = beautiful conservation success), Majete Wildlife Reserve ("African Parks" remarkable rehabilitation success, complete Big Five reintroduced), Mount Mulanje in the south (endemic Mulanje Cedar, 9,800 ft granite plateau hike iconic), Nyika Plateau in the north (surprising alpine landscapes at 7,200 ft altitude, pastures as far as the eye can see with antelopes), Zomba Plateau (former colonial capital, panoramic plateau 5,900 ft). Safety: Malawi among the safest African countries, moderate urban crime in Lilongwe. Try nsima (iconic southern African corn porridge, food base), chambo (Lake Malawi grilled cichlid iconic), kachumbari (salad), thobwa (fermented millet drink), Carlsberg Malawi (the only Carlsberg brewery in sub-Saharan Africa, heritage of Scandinavian development aid links), local Kuche Kuche beer. Lilongwe Kamuzu airport (LLW).

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 72-82°F, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 61°F + tmax ≥ 79°F)
