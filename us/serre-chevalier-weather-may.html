Context

Our verdict

Serre Chevalier (1200-9,200 ft) is the largest area in the Southern Alps: 155 miles of pistes, 4 villages (Briançon, Chantemerle, Villeneuve, Le Monêtier-les-Bains). Sunnier climate than the Northern Alps (Briançon claims 300 days/year of sunshine). July-August are my favorite months (8.5/10): 48-72°F, 27-35% rain, good for hiking towards the Pic de Rochebrune or the Serre-Chevalier lakes. From December to March, expect 16/37°F: ski season. More stable climate than in the Northern Alps (fewer Atlantic fronts). Briançon (UNESCO Vauban) remains the cultural base, the highest town in Europe at 4,400 ft. The Grands Bains du Monêtier (sulphurous thermal waters) after a day of skiing. Marseille 4h by car, Turin 2h via the Montgenèvre pass. Oulx TGV station (Italy) optional for travelers from Paris-Lyon.
