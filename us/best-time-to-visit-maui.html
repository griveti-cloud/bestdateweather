Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Maui, the "Magic Isle" of Hawaii, is more diverse than Oahu, more accessible than Big Island. Stable tropical climate. July is my favorite month (8.8/10): 66-79°F, 32% rain in brief showers on the west side (Kihei and Wailea are nearly always dry, Hana on the east is water-soaked). The Road to Hana (68 miles, 600 bends, 56 single-lane bridges) remains the reference road trip: leave early and plan 8h round trip. Haleakalā (10,000 ft) for sunrise above the clouds (NPS booking mandatory). From December to May, expect 59-73°F and 38-56% rain: this is humpback whale season (outings from Lahaina, November-May). Lahaina burned in August 2023: the historic town remains largely destroyed, check current state before going. The high tourist season remains December-April: prices x2-x3, beaches that fill up. May-June and September-October are the real sweet spots.

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 72-82°F, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 61°F + tmax ≥ 79°F)
