When to visit Chamonix?

The decision rests on several criteria. Climate score, tourist crowds and feasible activities.

Verdict: In Chamonix, March and February clearly stand out. November concentrates the least favorable conditions.
G
Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

At Chamonix, the question isn't when, but why. For skiing, March remains my pick: long days already (10h of sunlight), still maximum snow cover on the Grands Montets glacier, transformed snow that forgives mistakes. February is colder (28/45°F) but great for powder. During Christmas and February school breaks, prices double or triple, lifts have queues, restaurants turn people away. For hiking, July-August open up Mont Blanc and the great routes (Refuges Albert 1er, Couvercle), but it's packed. September stays my favorite in summer: stable weather, refuges still open until the 20th, a third of the crowds. October keeps a mild climate (tmax 59°F, little rain) and remains excellent for self-guided hiking — even though the lifts close and refuges shutter. November marks the real transition: everything closes, weather often bleak, this is for those who love wide empty spaces.

How we calculate the score

For mountain destinations, we calculate two scores per month — skiing and hiking — and display the higher of the two.

⛷️Ski score DEC → MAY
  • 40% snow cover (slope T° corrected by lapse rate -3.6°F/1000ft)
  • 40% winter thermal comfort (23–41°F optimal)
  • 20% sunshine
  • Off-season penalty: ×0.70 adjacent months · ×0.35 outside season
🥾Hiking score JUN → SEP
  • 40% temperature (50–72°F optimal)
  • 40% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 20% sunshine

Questions

Frequently asked questions

When is the best time to visit Chamonix?
It depends on the activity. For skiing: March (ski score 9.1/10). For hiking/suiner: March (9.1/10, 8.0°F).
Can you ski in Chamonix in winter?
Yes, it's peak ski season. Average ski score December–February: 8.3/10. Cold temperatures (4°F max in January) and frequent snowfall.
Is it warm in Chamonix in suiner?
In March, temperatures average 8.0°F. Ideal for hiking and outdoor activities.
What is the wettest month in Chamonix?
November is the wettest month with November has the lowest climate score of the year.
