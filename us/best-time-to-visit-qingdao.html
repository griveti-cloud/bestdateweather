Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Qingdao, coastal city of Shandong on the Yellow Sea in China: 9.5 million inhabitants. Former German concession 1898-1914 which bequeathed unique colonial architecture in China + the Tsingtao brewery (1903 by the Germans, today the 2nd largest brewery in China and the most internationally exported Chinese brand). Summer capital of Chinese seaside tourism ("China's Switzerland" marketing nickname). September is my favorite month (8.7/10): 63-79°F, 13% rain, ideal temperatures. May-October stay excellent (7.7-8.7/10). From May to October, it's the tourist high season: 55-82°F, sea at 72°F in August. From June to August, expect 64-86°F with typical sea fogs ("haiwu" Yellow Sea fogs iconic of the climate of Qingdao), the wettest season. From December to March, expect 27/48°F and 5-10% rain: dry winter cold of north-eastern China, snow possible. Highlights: Qingdao Old Town (preserved 19th-20th century German colonial architecture, unique atmosphere in China = looks more like Munich than a Chinese city), St Michael's Cathedral (Catholic cathedral 1934 German neo-Romanesque iconic view), Governor's Mansion (1908 residence of the German governor of Qingdao, become museum), Xinhaoshan Park ("Signal Hill Park", iconic panorama over Qingdao + Yellow Sea + beaches), Tsingtao Beer Museum (Tsingtao brewery founded 1903, visit + tasting, the international beer festival of Qingdao August = Chinese Oktoberfest equivalent, 6 million annual visitors), Qingdao beaches = the main summer beach attraction: No.1 Bathing Beach, No.2-3-6 Bathing Beaches, golden sand, Yellow Sea water 72°F in summer (warmer than European North Sea), lively local atmosphere July-August with millions of domestic Chinese tourists, Zhanqiao Pier ("1892 historic jetty", iconic view of Qingdao with octagonal Huilan pavilion at the end 1,400 ft), Mt Laoshan 19 miles away ("sacred mountain of Taoism", 3,700 ft, highest point of coastal Shandong, panorama over the sea + Taoist temples Taiqing Palace 9th century), May Fourth Square (commemorative square of the May Fourth Movement 1919 = anti-Versailles Chinese nationalist movement triggered in Qingdao when the Allies returned the city to Japan rather than China, political iconic of modern Chinese nationalism, red sculpture "Wind of May" iconic view), Olympic Sailing Center (2008 Olympic center where the sailing events of the 2008 Beijing Olympics were held = Qingdao co-host of the Olympics thanks to its favourable bay). Excursions: Beijing 4h by high-speed train (already covered), Shanghai 6h by train, Tai Shan 4h away (UNESCO, the most prestigious sacred mountain of Taoism in China), Confucius Temple Qufu 4h away (UNESCO, birthplace of Confucius). Try fresh draught Tsingtao Beer (the famous "plastic bag beer" = local institution where the inhabitants buy their beer in sealed plastic bag to take home), Yellow Sea seafood (Qingdao = capital of seafood in northern China), shengjianbao fried dumplings, Shandong hot pot, Chinese Changyu wines (founded by neighbouring Qingdao). Qingdao Jiaodong airport (TAO).

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 72-82°F, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 61°F + tmax ≥ 79°F)
