En résumé : January in Brussels is pretty gritty—expect gray skies, frequent rain, and temperatures hovering just above freezing. This is ideal if you're the type who loves moody European winters, cozy cafés, and dodging crowds at museums and chocolateries. Pack layers and waterproofs, but honestly, the city's charm shines brightest when you're bundled up with hot chocolate in hand.
