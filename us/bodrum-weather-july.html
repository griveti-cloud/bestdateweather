Context

Our verdict

Bodrum, smart beach resort in south-west Turkey on the Aegean Sea: 175,000 inhabitants. Former Halicarnassus = birthplace of Herodotus ("father of History", 484 BC) and site of the Mausoleum of Halicarnassus = one of the 7 Wonders of the ancient world (tomb of Mausolus king of Caria 350 BC, destroyed by earthquakes in the Middle Ages, visitable remains). Nicknamed "Turkish Saint-Tropez" for its smart-bohemian blend. May is my favorite month (9.1/10): 63-77°F, 15% rain, sea at 66°F, ideal temperatures before the heatwave. June-July-August drop to 6.9/10 due to the heat (72-93°F) and the extreme crowds (Russians, Israelis, Europeans, Istanbulites on yachts in the majority, hotel prices x4-x5). September-October stay excellent (6.9-8.7/10). From November to March, expect 48-68°F and 13-44% rain: mild for the Mediterranean, calm, ideal for visiting without crowds. Highlights: Saint Peter's Castle (Bodrum Castle, 15th century built by the Knights Hospitaller of Rhodes with stones recovered from the Mausoleum, houses the Museum of Underwater Archaeology = one of the best in the world, ancient wrecks: Uluburun 14th century BC, Yassıada 7th century), Mausoleum of Halicarnassus (foundation remains, explanatory panels, memorial atmosphere more than spectacular as destroyed), Ancient theater (4th century BC, panoramic view over the bay), Bodrum Marina (port with luxury yachts, seaside restaurants), Bodrum Bazaar (Çarşı, oriental atmosphere), Bardakçı Beach (close to center), Yahşi Beach, Gümbet (party tourist neighbouring resort), Türkbükü ("Saint-Tropez of Bodrum", smart, no beach but pontoons on the water, seaside restaurants), Yalıkavak (smart marina port, 5*+ hotels, luxury boutiques), Gümüşlük (picturesque western village, feet in the water at sunset), Bitez (windsurf), Ortakent. Excursions: Kos 1h by ferry (Greek island already covered by BDW), Pamukkale 3h away, Cleopatra Island (white sand unique in the world brought from Egypt by Mark Antony for Cleopatra according to legend). Try Turkish meze, balık ekmek (fish sandwich), kebab, raki (Turkish anisette), Turkish coffee. Bodrum-Milas airport (BJV).
