Context

Our verdict

Verbier, premium Swiss ski resort at the heart of the 4 Vallées: 3000 permanent inhabitants (15,000 in season), traditional village (old wooden chalets) at 4,900 ft. Famous for its committing pistes and legendary off-piste. August is my favorite month (7.8/10): 48-68°F, 44% rain in storms, hiking and mountain bike (DH Verbier global festival late July/early August). From June to September, expect 43-68°F: summer season, paragliding, 18-hole golf (Les Esserts, the highest in Europe at 5,900 ft), Verbier Festival classical music mid-July to early August. From December to April, expect 18/39°F and 43-58% precipitation often as snow: this is ski high season (255 miles of pistes 4 Vallées Verbier+Nendaz+Veysonnaz+Thyon+La Tzoumaz). World-class off-piste: Vallon d'Arbi, Stairway to Heaven, Mont-Fort (10,900 ft, summit of the area, glacier). Xtreme Verbier (March-April freeride competition, Freeride World Tour final, on the north face of the Bec des Rosses, one of the wildest off-piste descents in the world). Logistics: Verbier 2h from Geneva by car, 3h by train + bus from Le Châble. Very expensive: restaurant meals $33-$55, ski pass $77/day, hotels $220-$880/night in high season. Audience: European and British ski elite (Verbier nicknamed "Britney's playground" for the English jet set), Swedish and Belgian royal teenagers. Try Valais raclette, half-and-half fondue, Valais wine (Fendant, Petite Arvine).

Frequently asked questions

When is the best time to visit Verbier?
It depends on the activity. For skiing: March (ski score 9.1/10). For hiking/suiner: March (9.1/10, 5.0°F).
Can you ski in Verbier in winter?
Yes, it's peak ski season. Average ski score December–February: 8.5/10. Cold temperatures (1°F max in January) and frequent snowfall.
Is it warm in Verbier in suiner?
In March, temperatures average 5.0°F. Ideal for hiking and outdoor activities.
What is the wettest month in Verbier?
November is the wettest month with November has the lowest climate score of the year.
