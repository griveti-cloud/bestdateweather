Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Kathmandu, capital of Nepal: 1 million inhabitants in the city, 2.5 in the urban area. At 4,600 ft altitude in a mountainous valley, 7 UNESCO sites. Rebuilt after the devastating earthquake of April 2015 (9000 deaths, 600,000 houses destroyed). April is my favorite month (8.2/10): 57-77°F, 42% rain, ideal temperatures, rhododendrons in bloom. From March to May, it's the high trekking season (8.0-8.2/10): 50-79°F, clear morning skies on the summits (Everest, Annapurnas). From June to September, it's the monsoon: 90-100% rainfall, leeches on the trails, summits hidden, but the greenest landscapes (rare period to visit Mustang and Dolpo, valleys in the rain shadow). From October to November, it's the 2nd high trekking season (8.0-8.1/10): 50-75°F, the clearest skies of the year after the monsoon, but crowds on the Annapurna and Everest BC. From December to February, expect 41-66°F: cold at altitude, treks difficult above 13,100 ft. Highlights: Durbar Square (historic center, partially destroyed in 2015), Pashupatinath (Hindu temple, cremations on the ghats), Boudhanath Stupa (one of the world's largest Buddhist stupas, Tibetan quarter), Swayambhunath ("Monkey Temple", stupa on a hill), Patan and Bhaktapur (medieval city-states). Classic treks from here: Annapurna Circuit (15-21 days), Annapurna Base Camp (10-14 days), Everest Base Camp (12-16 days, Lukla flight). Try momos, dal bhat, sel roti.

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 72-82°F, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 61°F + tmax ≥ 79°F)

Frequently asked questions

What is the best time to visit Kathmandu?
November is the best month with 70°F highs, 50°F lows, 9.9h of sunshine and 9.0% rainy days. The months of November & April offer comparable conditions.
How many hours of sunshine does Kathmandu get in November?
Kathmandu averages 9.9 sunshine hours per day in November. It is the sunniest month with a climate score of 8.7/10.

What are the temperatures at night in Kathmandu in November?
In November, overnight lows reach 50°F, while afternoon highs average 70°F.
Is Kathmandu good in winter?
In winter (December-February), Kathmandu scores 8.2/10 on average — Acceptable conditions for cultural visits.. The best month overall remains November with a score of 8.7/10.
When should you avoid Kathmandu?
July is the most difficult month to visit Kathmandu, with July has the lowest climate score of the year. Climate conditions are at their lowest of the year during this period.
