Context

Our verdict

Annaba, port town of north-east Algeria on the Mediterranean coast 375 miles east of Algiers: 460,000 inhabitants. Former Roman Hippo founded by the Phoenicians 7th century BC, Numidian then Roman capital, place where Saint Augustine was bishop 396-430 AD = one of the most influential Church Fathers ("Confessions" + "The City of God"). Coastal town preserved from international tourism, traditional Algerian atmosphere. May is my favorite month (8.7/10): 57-72°F, 12% rain, this is the ideal Mediterranean spring. April-June-October stay excellent (8.0-8.5/10). From April to June and September-October, these are the best seasons: 54-84°F, ideal Mediterranean climate. From July to August, expect 72-88°F with warm sea 77°F, local seaside season. From November to March, expect 43-66°F and 30-44% rain: mild for the Mediterranean. Highlights: Saint Augustine Basilica (1900 on the hill of Hippo, neo-Byzantine, one of the most beautiful churches in North Africa, houses a relic of Saint Augustine's forearm), Hippo Ruins (Roman vestiges from the 1st-5th century AD, early Christian basilica where Saint Augustine officiated, Roman theater, forum), Cours de la Révolution ("Annaba Course", main pedestrian shaded avenue lined with 19th-century French colonial arcades), Place de l'Indépendance, Sidi Bou Merouane Mosque (11th century), Salah-Bey Mosque, Rizi Amor Beach (Mediterranean urban beach), Cape de Garde (north-east cape with lighthouse and preserved beaches), Hippo Museum (Roman + Carthaginian archaeological collection). Excursions: Constantine 2h away (see dedicated review), Tipaza 7h away (UNESCO 1982 Roman-Punic ruins), El Kala National Park 1h away (RAMSAR wetland, lakes and migratory birds), Tunisian border Tabarka 1h away. Safety: Annaba globally safe, but Algeria in general under France Diplomacy vigilance for residual terrorism (weakened but present AQMI movement in southern Saharan regions), moderate urban crime in Annaba. Visa required for most nationalities. Try Algerian couscous (national iconic), chorba frik (lamb barley soup), méchoui (spit-roast lamb), makroudh (semolina-date pastry), mint tea, Arabic coffee with cardamom. Annaba Rabah Bitat airport (AAE).
