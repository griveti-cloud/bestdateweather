Context

Our verdict

Mogadishu (Muqdisho), capital of Somalia on the Indian Ocean south-east coast: 2.6 million inhabitants. One of the most dangerous cities in the world since 1991 by total collapse of the Somali state ("failed state" type 1) = 33 years of civil war, Al-Shabaab Islamist terrorism since 2006, sea piracy (popularised by the film "Captain Phillips" 2013), chronic famines. Before 1991, Mogadishu was "the pearl of the Indian Ocean" = elegant Swahili-Italian capital (Italian colonial heritage 1889-1960) with iconic Moorish-Art Deco architecture. January is my favorite month (9.2/10): 73-86°F, 10% rain, this is the ideal dry season. February-March-December stay excellent (8.7-9.1/10). From December to March, it's the dry season "Jilaal": 73-90°F, sea at 81°F, ideal dry tropical climate on paper. From June to August, it's the rainy season "Hagaa": 50-72% rainfall. From April to May and October-November, these are the intermediate humid seasons "Gu" and "Deyr". Highlights (before 1991): Old town of Mogadishu Hamar Weyne and Shangani (historic Swahili-Arab-Italian atmosphere, narrow alleys, 13th-15th century mosques), Arba'a Rukun Mosque ("4 pillars", 1269, one of the oldest mosques in sub-Saharan Africa), Fakhr ad-Din Mosque (1269), Cathedral of Our Lady of the Rosary of Mogadishu (1928 Italian colonial heritage, one of the largest cathedrals in Africa, severely damaged during the civil war, partially rebuilt), Bakaara Market (the largest market in Mogadishu, but also epicenter of urban violence since 1991, site of the Battle of Mogadishu October 1993 = "Black Hawk Down" the US operation that ended badly with 18 US deaths, popularised by Ridley Scott's 2001 film), Lido Beach (urban beach), Garesa Citadel (19th century), Italian Lighthouse of Mogadishu (colonial heritage). Safety: absolutely inadvisable France Diplomacy for the entirety of Somalia, active Al-Shabaab terrorism (periodic attacks in Mogadishu including deadly attacks Maka Al-Mukarama Hotel 2017 + Zoobe Junction 2017 600 deaths), Western kidnappings, formally and absolutely inadvisable travel except government military escorts. Somaliland (northern region self-proclaimed independent 1991 but not internationally recognised, capital Hargeisa) is significantly safer = sometimes visited by adventurous travelers, relative peace for 30 years, but administratively Somalia. Try bariis iskukaris (Somali iconic spiced rice), suqaar (sliced beef), sambuus (Somali samosas Indian heritage), anjero (iconic fermented flatbread), Somali tea with cardamom + cinnamon. Mogadishu Aden Adde airport (MGQ).
