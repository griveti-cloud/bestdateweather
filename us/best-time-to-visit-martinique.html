Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Martinique, French overseas department in the Lesser Antilles: 420 sq miles, 360,000 inhabitants, the "island of flowers" (in Creole "Madinina"). January is my favorite month (8.1/10): 73-82°F, 58% rain, this is the carême (dry season), sea at 81°F. From December to May, it's the tourist high season: 72-86°F, hotel prices x2-x3, From July to October, it's the active hurricane season ("hivernage"): 70-81% rainfall, real hurricane risk (Maria 2017 hit). From June and November are the best shoulder seasons. Highlights: Fort-de-France (capital, spice market), Saint-Pierre (former "Little Paris of the Antilles", destroyed by the eruption of Mount Pelée on 8 May 1902 = 30,000 deaths in 1 minute, only 1 survivor Cyparis in his cell, ruins visitable), Mount Pelée (4,600 ft, 6h round-trip hike, still active volcano), Trois-Îlets (birthplace of Empress Josephine), Diamond Rock (Le Diamant, iconic rock to the south), Pointe du Bout (hotel zone), Anses-d'Arlet (iconic beachside church), Plage des Salines (the most famous, south), Tartane (Caravelle, wild coast), Habitations Clément (rum, former plantation), Saint-James (rum). Activities: diving (Diamond Rock, Saint-Pierre), Canal des Esclaves hike, Grenadines cruises south (Saint Lucia 30 min by helicopter). Try cod accras, chicken colombo, Creole boudin, ti-punch, planteur, AOC Martinique rum (the world's only AOC for rum).

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 72-82°F, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 61°F + tmax ≥ 79°F)

What is the best time to visit Martinique?
February is the best month with 82°F highs, 72°F lows, 10.8h of sunshine and 57.0% rainy days. The months of February & March offer comparable conditions.
How many hours of sunshine does Martinique get in February?
Martinique averages 10.8 sunshine hours per day in February. It is the sunniest month with a climate score of 8.5/10.

What are the temperatures at night in Martinique in February?
In February, overnight lows reach 72°F, while afternoon highs average 82°F.
Is Martinique good in winter?
In winter (December-February), Martinique scores 8.4/10 on average — Acceptable conditions for cultural visits.. The best month overall remains February with a score of 8.5/10.
When should you avoid Martinique?
July is the most difficult month to visit Martinique, with July has the lowest climate score of the year. Climate conditions are at their lowest of the year during this period.
