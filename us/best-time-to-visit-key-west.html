Frequently asked questions

April is the best month with 79°F highs, 75°F lows, 12.5h of sunshine and 23% rainy days.
Key West averages 12.5 sunshine hours per day in April. It is the sunniest month with a climate score of 9.0/10.
July is the most unfavourable month with 53% rainy days and the lowest climate score of the year.
In April, overnight lows reach 75°F, while afternoon highs average 79°F.
In winter (December-February), Key West scores 8.5/10 on average — Acceptable conditions for cultural visits.. The best month overall remains April with a score of 9.0/10.
July is the most difficult month to visit Key West, with 53% rainy days. Climate conditions are at their lowest of the year during this period.
