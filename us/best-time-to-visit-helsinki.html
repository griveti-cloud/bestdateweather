When to visit Helsinki?

The decision rests on several criteria. Climate score, tourist crowds and feasible activities.

Verdict: In Helsinki, June and July clearly stand out. December concentrates the least favorable conditions.
G
Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Helsinki has two faces: bright and warm summer, freezing dark winter. June-July are my favorite months (7.6/10): 54-68°F, 35-41% rain, midnight sun (almost 19h of daylight), public saunas with dips into the Baltic (Löyly, Allas Sea Pool), Suomenlinna (UNESCO fortress) by 15 min ferry, festivals (Flow Festival mid-August). From December to February, expect 25/36°F and 40-47% precipitation often as snow: only 6h of daylight end of December. Christmas markets and Helsinki Christmas Village season. Aurora borealis rare in Helsinki (too far south), you need to head up to Rovaniemi (Lapland) or Levi. Helsinki Cathedral, Temppeliaukio (church in the rock), Design District for Finnish design. Helsinki stays expensive: restaurant meals $28-$44. Tallinn 2h by ferry (Estonia-Finland crossing). May and August are the best crowd/weather compromises.

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 72-82°F, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 61°F + tmax ≥ 79°F)
