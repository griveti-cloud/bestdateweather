FAQ

Frequently asked questions

July is the best month with 64°F highs, 54°F lows, 7.2h of sunshine and 53% rainy days. The months of July & August offer comparable conditions.
Edinburgh averages 7.2 sunshine hours per day in July. It is the sunniest month with a climate score of 7.2/10.
January is the most unfavourable month with 42% rainy days and the lowest climate score of the year.
In July, overnight lows reach 54°F, while afternoon highs average 64°F.
In winter (December-February), Edinburgh scores 4.0/10 on average — Difficult period — prefer the peak season.. The best month overall remains July with a score of 7.2/10.
January is the most difficult month to visit Edinburgh, with 42% rainy days. Climate conditions are at their lowest of the year during this period.
