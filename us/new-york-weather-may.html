What to expect in May

Spring marks the start of the good season in New York. Temperatures rise (21°F) and tourists have not yet arrived en masse. Expect rain on 41% of days — a light raincoat in the bag is recommended. Not the best window, but acceptable if dates are constrained. June (8.3/10) is significantly better if possible.
