Context

Our verdict

Interlaken, Swiss town between Lakes Brienz and Thun in the Bernese Oberland: 6000 inhabitants at 1,900 ft, the main tourist base for visiting Jungfraujoch and the Oberland (Grindelwald, Wengen, Mürren 30-45 min away). August is my favorite month (8.1/10): 61-77°F, 49% rain, terraces, cruises on the 2 lakes (Brienz and Thun), paragliding (Interlaken = world capital of paragliding with central landing on Höhematte the central meadow). From June to September, it's the tourist high season: 57-77°F, climate moderated by the lakes. From December to March, expect 34-48°F and 45-54% precipitation often as snow: skiing at the neighbouring resorts (Grindelwald 30 min, Mürren 45 min), little snow in Interlaken itself (1,900 ft), Bahnhofstrasse Christmas market. Highlights: Höhematte (large central meadow with view of the Jungfrau-Mönch-Eiger), Harder Kulm (4,300 ft, 8 min funicular, panoramic view over the 2 lakes and the Oberland), Schynige Platte (6,800 ft, cog railway from Wilderswil, panorama with alpine botanical garden), Lake Thun (cruises on the Blümlisalp 1906 steamboat), Lake Brienz (cruises to Iseltwald + Giessbachfälle waterfall), Trümmelbachfälle 30 min away (10 intra-glacial waterfalls accessible by lift). Activities: tandem paragliding from Beatenberg or Niederhorn (landing at Höhematte, 200 CHF), canyoning in the Saxetenbach, rafting, Jungfraujoch by train (3h round trip, 220 CHF). Try rösti, raclette, fondue, Bündnerfleisch (Grisons cured beef). Logistics: Interlaken 2h from Zurich, 1h from Bern by train.

Frequently asked questions

When is the best time to visit Interlaken?
It depends on the activity. For skiing: February (ski score 9.0/10). For hiking/suiner: February (9.0/10, 9.0°F).
Can you ski in Interlaken in winter?
Yes, it's peak ski season. Average ski score December–February: 8.7/10. Cold temperatures (7°F max in January) and frequent snowfall.
Is it warm in Interlaken in suiner?
In February, temperatures average 9.0°F. Ideal for hiking and outdoor activities.
What is the wettest month in Interlaken?
May is the wettest month with May has the lowest climate score of the year.
