Adelaide, capital of South Australia: 1.3 million inhabitants, calmer city than Sydney/Melbourne, British urban design with green belt (Adelaide Park Lands), capital of Australian wines. January is my favorite month (9.6/10): 61-82°F, 14% rain, ideal austral summer. From June to August (austral winter), expect 46-57°F and 43-44% rain: cold and humid for Australia. December-March are the high festival season: Adelaide Festival of Arts and Fringe Festival (the 2nd largest arts festival in the world after Edinburgh) in February-March. Highlights: Adelaide Central Market (since 1869), Art Gallery of South Australia, Adelaide Botanic Garden, North Terrace (museums). Beaches: Glenelg (by tram from the center), Henley Beach. Wine excursions: Barossa Valley 1h away (Shiraz, Penfolds Grange), McLaren Vale 45 min away (Shiraz, Cabernet), Adelaide Hills (Pinot Noir, Chardonnay), Clare Valley 2h away (Riesling). Kangaroo Island 2h by ferry (endemic wildlife, Remarkable Rocks, Seal Bay). Coober Pedy 8h away (underground opal town). Try pie floater (pie on split pea soup, local dish), German pastries (Hahndorf German village). Adelaide is less frequented by Europeans than Sydney/Melbourne, more local.

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 72-82°F, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 61°F + tmax ≥ 79°F)

What is the best time to visit Adelaide?
January is the best month with 82°F highs, 61°F lows, 13.8h of sunshine and 14.0% rainy days. The months of January & February offer comparable conditions.
How many hours of sunshine does Adelaide get in January?
Adelaide averages 13.8 sunshine hours per day in January. It is the sunniest month with a climate score of 9.6/10.

What are the temperatures at night in Adelaide in January?
In January, overnight lows reach 61°F, while afternoon highs average 82°F.
Is Adelaide good in winter?
In winter (December-February), Adelaide scores 9.5/10 on average — Acceptable conditions for cultural visits.. The best month overall remains January with a score of 9.6/10.
When should you avoid Adelaide?
