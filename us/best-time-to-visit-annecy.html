Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Annecy, the "Venice of the Alps": 130,000 inhabitants, alpine lake (the purest in Europe according to analyzes), surrounding mountains (La Tournette 7,700 ft). July-August are my favorite months (8.8-8.9/10): 61-81°F, 31-35% rain, lake swimming (water at 72°F in midsummer), paragliding from Le Semnoz, lake circuit by bike (25 miles). From December to February, expect 32-50°F and 39-45% rain or snow: skiing at La Clusaz, Le Grand-Bornand or Megève (45 min). The International Animation Film Festival mid-June (one of the largest in the world) animates the city. Highlights: Old town with its canals and the Palais de l'Île, Annecy Castle, Pont des Amours, Sunday morning markets (very famous). Lake Annecy: boat ride, public beaches (Albigny free, Impérial paid), picturesque villages (Talloires, Veyrier-du-Lac). Try tartiflette, savoyarde fondue, omble chevalier (lake fish). Geneva 45 min by car, Chamonix 1h away. May-June and September are the best crowd/weather compromises. Direct flight Geneva or Lyon, TGV from Paris (3h45).

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 72-82°F, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 61°F + tmax ≥ 79°F)
