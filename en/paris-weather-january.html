En résumé : January in Paris is genuinely cold and gray—perfect if you're the type who loves cozy cafés, empty museums, and moody Seine walks without crowds. Expect chilly temperatures around 2–7°C and frequent drizzle, but fewer tourists mean you'll actually experience the city's authentic rhythm. Pack layers and embrace the romance of a quieter, wintry Paris.
