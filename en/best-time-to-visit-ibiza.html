By trip type

Best time by type of trip

🎉Parties & ClubsJuly–August — Amnesia, Ushuaïa, Pacha. Club season June to September.
🏖️Coves & BeachesJune or October — Cala Conta, Cala Bassa — crystal-clear and peaceful.
🌅SunsetCafé del Mar toute la saison — June remains the best light-to-crowds balance.
👨‍👩‍👧FamilyJune — before club peak season, affordable beaches, reasonable prices.
💰BudgetOctober — club season over, prices -40%, empty coves, summer weather.
