Context

Our verdict

São Miguel, the largest of the Azores (746 km²): Portuguese island in the Atlantic, 1500 km from Lisbon, green volcanic landscapes (European Hawaii). July-August are my favourite months (8.2-8.3/10): 18-24°C, 46-48% rain, mild temperatures, swimming in the Atlantic (water at 22°C). From December to March, expect 12-17°C and 52-56% precipitation: near-continuous rain (humid oceanic climate), but the greenest landscapes. Capricious weather island: 4 seasons in one day possible. Highlights: Sete Cidades (crater with green and blue lakes, panorama from Vista do Rei or Boca do Inferno), Lagoa do Fogo (lake in wilder crater), Furnas (volcanic valley with geysers, free hot springs Poça da Dona Beija or Terra Nostra), Cozido das Furnas (traditional dish cooked in the volcanic earth, to order the day before), Ponta Delgada (capital), Vila Franca do Campo (volcanic atoll offshore). Whale watching April-October (sperm whales year-round, blue whales March-June). Tea plantations Gorreana (the only European plantation, since 1883). Try Azores pineapple, São Jorge cheese, Pico wine, queijadas. May-June and September-October are the best tourism/weather compromises. Direct flight Lisbon (2h) or Porto.
