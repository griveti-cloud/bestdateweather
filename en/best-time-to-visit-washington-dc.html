G
Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Washington DC is the federal capital: 700,000 inhabitants, but 6 million in the urban area. Monumental city centre around the National Mall. June stays my favourite month (8.6/10): 18-29°C, 41% rain, but also rising humidity. July-August drop to 6.9/10 due to stifling humid heat (21-31°C, feels like 38°C+). From December to February, expect -2/10°C: dry cold, occasional blizzards. March-April is cherry blossom season (around the Tidal Basin, Japanese gift from 1912, peak around 1-7 April, Cherry Blossom Festival), major tourist peak. September-October are my real sweet spots: 10-26°C, clear sky, foliage. Highlights (free): National Mall with Lincoln Memorial, Washington Monument (562 steps), Capitol, White House (booking via congressman), Smithsonian Museums (19 museums all free: Air and Space, American History, Natural History, African American History), Holocaust Memorial Museum, Arlington Cemetery. Georgetown for the charming side. Try half-smoke at Ben's Chili Bowl. Efficient metro. 4h by train from NYC (Acela), 1h from Baltimore.

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 22–28°C, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 16°C + tmax ≥ 26°C)
