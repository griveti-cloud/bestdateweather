Context

Our verdict

Barbados is the British Caribbean: left-hand driving, national cricket, rum (Mount Gay, founded in 1703, the world's oldest distillery). April stays my favourite month (8.6/10): 23-28°C, 41% rain, sea at 27°C. The outer coral reef (west coast) protects from heavy swells: Carlisle Bay, sheltered beach good for swimming. The east coast (Bathsheba, Soup Bowl) keeps the wild Atlantic: world-class surf, dangerous swimming. From August to October, it's hurricane season with 70-77% rainfall: tropical storm risk, but off the main path (Barbados is protected by its position east of the Caribbean arc). Bridgetown is UNESCO for its colonial historic centre (Nidhe Israel synagogue from 1654, one of the oldest in the Americas). From December to March stays the American and British high season: prices x2-x3. May-June and November are excellent compromises crowd/weather/price.
