Context

Our verdict

Puerto Vallarta is the chic beach resort of the Mexican Pacific coast: Sierra Madre Occidental plunging into the sea, 250,000 inhabitants. April is my favourite month (9.5/10): 18-27°C, 0% rain, sea at 24°C, ideal temperatures before the hot summer. From November to May, it's the dry season: 0-8% rain, constant sky, American and Canadian period (Snowbirds). From June to October, it's the rainy/hurricane season: 43-85% rainfall, humid heat (24-31°C), moderate hurricane risk (Pacific side less exposed than Atlantic). Highlights: Malecón (1.5 km seaside promenade, sculptures, Los Arcos), Zona Romántica (old town, Playa Los Muertos), Iglesia de Nuestra Señora de Guadalupe (iconic church with copper crown), Mercado Municipal. Beaches: Playa Los Muertos (centre, lively), Conchas Chinas (quieter), Yelapa (village accessible only by boat, 45 min, very quiet). Sayulita 45 min away (bohemian surfer village), Marietas Islands by boat (Playa Escondida, limited access 116 visitors/day), Sierra Madre for canopy/zipline. Try ceviche tostadas, birria de res, agua de jamaica. Significant LGBT community (Pride in May).
