Frequently asked questions

April is the best month with 27°C highs, 15°C lows, 11.5h of sunshine and 25% rainy days.
Pokhara averages 11.5 sunshine hours per day in April. It is the sunniest month with a climate score of 8.9/10.
July is the most unfavourable month with 97% rainy days and the lowest climate score of the year.
In April, overnight lows reach 15°C, while afternoon highs average 27°C.
In winter (December-February), Pokhara scores 7.9/10 on average — Acceptable conditions for cultural visits.. The best month overall remains April with a score of 8.9/10.
July is the most difficult month to visit Pokhara, with 97% rainy days. Climate conditions are at their lowest of the year during this period.
