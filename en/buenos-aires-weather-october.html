Context

Our verdict

Buenos Aires, Argentine capital: 15 million in the urban area, "South America's Paris", massive European heritage (Italians and Spaniards), tango, beef, Maradona/Messi. January-December are my favourite months (9.1/10): 21-28°C, 26-27% rain, austral summer, 14h days, terraces packed in Palermo, asados (Argentine BBQs). From June to August (austral winter), expect 8-16°C and 19-26% rain: mild for winter but cool (Argentinians feel it as cold), tango and opera season (Teatro Colón). From April to May and September-November are the best shoulder seasons: 11-24°C, jacarandas in bloom in November. Major districts: San Telmo (old bohemian quarter, antiques market on Sundays, open-air tango shows), La Boca (colourful Caminito, Boca Juniors' Bombonera stadium, touristy but worth seeing), Recoleta (chic, cemetery where Eva Perón rests, MALBA Latin American art museum), Palermo (Soho/Hollywood subdivisions trendy, restaurants, nightlife), Puerto Madero (rehabilitated docks, modern zone), Centre (Avenida 9 de Julio = "the world's widest avenue" 16 lanes, Obelisk, Casa Rosada, Plaza de Mayo). Tango: milongas (tango bars to dance, La Catedral more local), tourist shows (Café Tortoni). Try asado (parilla La Cabrera, Don Julio), empanadas, choripán, dulce de leche, alfajores, Malbec wine. Football: River Plate vs Boca Juniors = the most passionate Superclasico in the world. Tigre 1h away for the Paraná delta by boat. Direct flight Paris-Buenos Aires in 14h.
