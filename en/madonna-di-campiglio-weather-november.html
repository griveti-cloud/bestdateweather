Context

Our verdict

Madonna di Campiglio, in the Italian Trentino: 700 inhabitants at 1550m in the heart of the Brenta Dolomites (UNESCO 2009, iconic Dolomitic landscapes), reputed as the "Italian Saint Moritz" for its smartness and prices. August is my favourite month (7.3/10): 9-19°C, 70% rain in altitude storms, Brenta hiking, via ferrata. From June to September, expect 5-19°C: active summer season (climbing, climbing in the Brenta = cradle of Italian climbing, Cesare Maestri). From December to April, expect -11/7°C and 26-44% precipitation often as snow: ski high season. Pistes: 156 km Skiarea Campiglio Dolomiti di Brenta (Madonna + Pinzolo + Folgarida + Marilleva, 1 single ski pass), connected to Dolomiti Superski by shuttle buses (1200 km of pistes in 12 Dolomitic resorts, 1 single pass). 3 Tre (cult late-December World Cup slalom on the Canalone Miramonti piste, one of the most famous pistes on the circuit). Pistes: Spinale, 5 Laghi, Pradalago, Grostè (3000m, Brenta panorama). Off-piste Brenta (with guide). Audience: Italian aristocracy (Milanese, Venetian), well-off Italian families, smarter than Cortina. Hotels: Hotel DV Chalet, Hotel Alpen Suite, Lefay Resort & Spa (5*+), 200-2000€/night in high season. Carlo Magno Hotel = historic palace. Try canederli, polenta, casunziei, speck, Trentino wines (Pinot Bianco, Müller-Thurgau). Logistics: Madonna 2h30 from Milan or Verona by car, 4h from Munich.
