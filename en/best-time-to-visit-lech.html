G
Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Lech am Arlberg, ultra-smart Austrian ski resort: 1500 inhabitants at 1450m, skier quotas limited to 14,000 per day (one of the only resorts in the world with a voluntary quota to preserve the experience). Linked to Zürs-St. Anton by Ski Arlberg (305 km). July is my favourite month (5.3/10): 9-19°C, 65% rain in altitude storms. Lech remains above all a winter resort. From December to April, expect -8/5°C and 42-68% precipitation often as snow: ski high season, one of the snowiest in Europe (10m/year, Arlberg = the "cradle of snow"). Pistes: 305 km Ski Arlberg with Zürs-St. Anton-Stuben, 87 lifts. White Ring = 22 km loop circuit to ski (Lech-Zürs-Zug-Lech), world record of skiers on annual circuit. Off-piste: Mehlsack, Madloch, Roggspitze (with guide). Legendary pistes: Schindlergrat, Auenfeldjet (cable car between Lech and Warth for 340 km of pistes total). Audience: ultra-smart (Hotels Aurelio, Almhof Schneider, Krone, 5*+, 500-3000€/night), European and Eastern aristocracy, well-off Dutch and Belgians. The Dutch royal family has been coming for decades (Princess Friso fatal accident in 2012). Wellness: all the 5* have their iconic spas (expensive = 100€+/session). Try Käsespätzle, Wiener Schnitzel. Logistics: Lech 2h from Zurich, 1h30 from Innsbruck.

How we calculate the score

For mountain destinations, we calculate two scores per month — skiing and hiking — and display the higher of the two.

⛷️Ski score DEC → MAY
  • 40% snow cover (slope T° corrected by lapse rate -6.5°C/1000m)
  • 40% winter thermal comfort (-5 to 5°C optimal)
  • 20% sunshine
  • Off-season penalty: ×0.70 adjacent months · ×0.35 outside season
🥾Hiking score JUN → SEP
  • 40% temperature (10–22°C optimal)
  • 40% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 20% sunshine

Frequently asked questions

When is the best time to visit Lech?
It depends on the activity. For skiing: March (ski score 8.9/10). For hiking/summer: March (8.9/10, 5.0°C).
Can you ski in Lech in winter?
Yes, it's peak ski season. Average ski score December–February: 8.3/10. Cold temperatures (0°C max in January) and frequent snowfall.
Is it warm in Lech in summer?
In March, temperatures average 5.0°C. Best period for hiking and outdoor activities.
What is the wettest month in Lech?
November is the wettest month with November has the lowest climate score of the year.
