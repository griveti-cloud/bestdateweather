Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Aqaba, Jordan's only port on the Red Sea at the far south of the country: 150,000 inhabitants on the Israeli border (Eilat 8 km away), Saudi and Egyptian (Gulf of Aqaba shared by 4 countries). Special economic duty-free zone status since 2001 (tax-free shopping). Jordan's capital of seaside tourism and Red Sea diving. November is my favourite month (9.3/10): 13-26°C, 5% rain, sea temperature 25°C, ideal temperatures. December-March stay excellent (8.4-9.2/10). From November to April, it's the tourist high season: 13-30°C, sea at 22-26°C, hotel prices x2-x3. From June to August drop to 3.4-3.9/10 due to extreme heatwave (24-41°C, sometimes 47°C+). From April to May and September-October, expect 18-37°C: warm intermediate. From January to February, expect 9-22°C: mild for the Middle East, ideal for visiting without heat. Highlights: Aqaba beaches (free public beaches but limited, private beaches of the 4-5* hotels-resorts recommended: Movenpick, Tala Bay 17 km to the south), Aqaba Marine Park (protected marine park with snorkelling and diving accessible from the beach), Underwater site Cedar Pride (80m wreck of a Lebanese cargo ship deliberately scuttled 1985 for an artificial reef, unmissable dive accessible from the coast 7m deep), Tank Memorial (M42 Duster tank wreck scuttled 1999 at 6m depth, snorkelling-friendly, iconic for underwater photographers), Red Sea diving = main Aqaba activity (warm waters 22-28°C, visibility 20-40m, 1200 fish species, multicoloured corals, PADI Open Water training 4-5 days 350-450€), Aqaba Mamluk Fortress (16th century, taken by T.E. Lawrence and the Bedouin army in July 1917 during the Arab Revolt, turning point of WWI in the Middle East), Aqaba Archaeological Museum, Aqaba Flag (gigantic mast 132m high flying the flag of the 1916 Arab Revolt, iconic view of the city visible from Israel and Egypt). Essential excursions: Wadi Rum 1h by car (the great Jordanian desert of Lawrence of Arabia, mentioned in Petra), Petra 2h by car (UNESCO, Jordan's major attraction, descend from Aqaba for a day if a short stay), Eilat Israel 8 km away (border crossable on foot at the Yitzhak Rabin Border Crossing 8h-20h, different culture-atmosphere, possibility of seeing Egypt Taba). Try sayadieh (typical Arab port fish-and-spiced-rice dish), fresh seafood, mansaf (Jordanian national dish), knafeh, Arabic coffee, fresh fruit juices. Logistics: King Hussein International Aqaba airport (AQJ) with direct flights Amman (45 min), Brussels, Berlin European charters.

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 22–28°C, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 16°C + tmax ≥ 26°C)
