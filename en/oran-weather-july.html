Context

Our verdict

Oran is the 2nd city of Algeria, facing the Mediterranean. Dry Mediterranean climate. June is my favourite month (9.9/10): 18-28°C, 5% rain, regular sea breezes. July-August drop to 6.9/10 due to the dry heatwave (21-31°C) and the coastal humidity that makes the heat heavy. The Oranais evacuate to the beaches of Aïn El Turk or Cap Falcon. From November to March, expect 8-20°C and 22-30% rain: humid, frequent wind, but the city keeps its rhythm. The raï scene (Khaled, Mami) was born here: a few historic cabarets persist. Santa Cruz (overhanging Spanish fortress) for the panoramic view. The Abdelkader Alloula Regional Theatre and Place du 1er-Novembre concentrate the animation. International Algerian tourism remains limited: visa to anticipate, basic hotel infrastructure. For prepared travellers, the experience remains strong.
