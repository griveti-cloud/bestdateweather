Entebbe, coastal town of Lake Victoria in Uganda 35 km south of the capital Kampala: 70,000 inhabitants. Historic capital of the British protectorate of Uganda 1894-1962, kept as the presidential capital (presidential residence of Idi Amin Dada then Yoweri Museveni). Main international airport of Uganda = gateway for mountain gorillas Bwindi + East African safaris Murchison Falls + Queen Elizabeth NP + Lake Victoria. Site of the famous Entebbe Raid on 4 July 1976 = Israeli military rescue operation of 102 hostages from an Air France plane hijacked by Palestinian-German terrorists at Entebbe airport with the complicity of Idi Amin Dada, successful operation in 90 minutes (3 hostages dead + 1 Israeli commando killed Yonatan Netanyahu = brother of Benjamin Netanyahu current Israeli Prime Minister) = one of the most memorable military operations of the 20th century. July is my favourite month (8.3/10): 18-24°C, 38% rain, this is the ideal dry season. January-February-June-August-December stay excellent (7.8-8.2/10). Entebbe never tops 8.3/10 (mild equatorial altitude climate). From April to November, it's the wet season with 2 rainy seasons: April-May ("long rains" 65-85% rainfall) + October-November ("short rains" 65-70%). Unique permanent climate: Entebbe never tops 26°C in maximum average and rarely descends below 18°C in minimum average thanks to its altitude 1140m + influence of the great Lake Victoria (the largest lake in Africa 68,800 km², 2nd largest freshwater lake in the world after Lake Superior), ideal "Eternal Spring" tropical altitude climate. Entebbe highlights: Entebbe Botanical Gardens (1898, one of the oldest botanical gardens in Africa, palm trees + exotic trees + bird and vervet monkey watching, filming location of the film "Tarzan" 1932 according to legend), UWEC - Uganda Wildlife Education Centre ("Entebbe zoo" reconverted into Ugandan wildlife conservation centre, lions-rhinoceros-chimpanzees, educational experience for families), Historic Entebbe Airport (the historic terminal of the 1976 Entebbe Raid was demolished in 2007 = lost historical iconic, but new modern terminal 2008), Bahaï House of Worship Mother Temple of Africa (1961, the only Bahá'í temple in Africa, unique iconic religious iconic), Lake Victoria Beach at Entebbe ("lake beach" = swimming in Lake Victoria caution bilharzia risk, relaxing local atmosphere at sunset over the lake iconic view Ugandan), Ngamba Island Chimpanzee Sanctuary 45 min by boat (sanctuary of chimpanzees rescued from poaching, observation during feeding). Essential excursions from Entebbe: Bwindi Impenetrable National Park 9h by car (UNESCO 1994, mountain gorilla tracking = major tourist iconic of Uganda, one of the 2 last refuges of the 1063 world mountain gorillas with neighbouring Volcanoes NP Rwanda, permit 800 USD/person for 1h of observation at <30m of a family group = the most moving experience of life according to many travellers), Queen Elizabeth National Park 6h away (Big Four except rhinoceros, iconic tree-climbing lions, hippopotamuses Kazinga Channel boat safari), Murchison Falls National Park 5h away (the most beautiful waterfall in Africa according to many rankings, the Nile crosses a 7m wide fissure creating a spectacular 43m fall, hippopotamuses + crocodiles + elephants), Source of the Nile at Jinja 2h away (already covered by BDW = place where the White Nile exits Lake Victoria to begin its 6695 km journey to the Mediterranean Egypt), Lake Bunyonyi 8h away (spectacular alpine lake in south-west Uganda), Mt Elgon NP 6h away (dormant volcano 4321m). Try matoke (boiled mashed plantain Ugandan national dish), luwombo (iconic stew in banana leaf), rolex (chapati + omelette + vegetables iconic street food), Lake Victoria tilapia, Ugandan Bugisu Arabica coffee top world, Nile Special Beer + Bell Lager. Entebbe International airport (EBB), main airport of Uganda, direct flights Brussels Airlines + Turkish Airlines + Ethiopian + Emirates.

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 22–28°C, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 16°C + tmax ≥ 26°C)
