Context

Our verdict

Flaine, in the Grand Massif: 1600m, altitude resort (the highest in Grand Massif), Brutalist architecture by Marcel Breuer (Bauhaus architect, student of Mies van der Rohe), opened in 1969 = subject of architectural polemic (raw concrete in the heart of the Alps, considered the ugliest or the most visionary depending on opinion). August is my favourite month (8.2/10): 11-22°C, 39% rain in storms, alpine hiking. From June to September, expect 4-22°C: short summer season. From December to March, expect -6/5°C and 41-48% precipitation often as snow: ski high season, very good snow cover thanks to altitude. Pistes: 265 km Grand Massif (Flaine + Samoëns + Morillon + Sixt + Les Carroz, 1 single ski pass), altitude 700-2500m, vertical drop 1800m. iconic pistes: Cascades (one of the longest green pistes in the world, 14 km from Tête des Lindars 2480m down to Sixt 770m, accessible to beginners!), Combe de Gers (shaded north face, preserved snow). Off-piste: Combe du Fer-à-Cheval, Aiguille du Plan Praz. Audience: families, school groups (Flaine welcomes many snow-class school groups thanks to UCPA residences), functional rather than smart atmosphere. Hotels: Totem (iconic Breuer architecture, Hotel Le Refuge du Golf, Hotel Aujon), 100-400€/night. Sculptures: Picasso ("Tête de femme"), Vasarely, Dubuffet exhibited outdoors in the resort. Try tartiflette, fondue. Logistics: Flaine 1h15 from Geneva (mandatory parking at the foot of the resort, pedestrian access only for cars), Magny-Cluses-Flaine shuttles from the TGV station.

Frequently asked questions

When is the best time to visit Flaine?
It depends on the activity. For skiing: January (ski score 8.7/10). For hiking/summer: January (8.7/10, 2.0°C).
Can you ski in Flaine in winter?
Yes, it's peak ski season. Average ski score December–February: 8.5/10. Cold temperatures (2°C max in January) and frequent snowfall.
Is it warm in Flaine in summer?
In January, temperatures average 2.0°C. Best period for hiking and outdoor activities.
What is the wettest month in Flaine?
November is the wettest month with November has the lowest climate score of the year.
