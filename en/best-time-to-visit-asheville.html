Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Asheville, in North Carolina: 95,000 inhabitants at 660m altitude in the heart of the Blue Ridge Mountains (southern Appalachian chain), renowned for its bohemian atmosphere ("the new Portland"), its craft breweries (the highest density per inhabitant in the USA), and its art scene. June is my favourite month (8.7/10): 15-27°C, 42% rain, mild temperatures thanks to altitude. From May to October, it's the tourist high season: 8-29°C, mild climate. October = season of the spectacular foliage (Blue Ridge Parkway = one of the most beautiful panoramic roads in North America for autumn colours, peak mid-October), extreme crowds at the viewpoints, hotel prices x3. From July to August, expect 17-29°C with 47-53% rain: hot, characteristic afternoon storms. From December to February, expect -3/12°C: cold but mild for the Appalachians, light snow possible. Highlights: Biltmore Estate (the largest private home in the USA, 16,622 m², 250 rooms, built by George Vanderbilt 1889-1895, copy of a French château, gardens by Frederick Law Olmsted creator of Central Park, vineyard — the largest visit in North America, ticket 80$+, to book), Blue Ridge Parkway (754 km of panoramic road, no advertising or red light, panoramas, access from Asheville), Great Smoky Mountains National Park 1h30 away (UNESCO, the most visited in the USA, 12 million visitors/year), Mount Mitchell (2037m, the highest peak east of the Mississippi). City centre: rare art deco architecture (Pack Square, Grove Arcade), Asheville Brewers Trail (35+ breweries including Wicked Weed and Highland Brewing). Try Carolina barbecue (vinegar-based), trout farm, biscuits and gravy, craft beers. Asheville Regional airport, but often you fly to Charlotte/Atlanta + 2h by road.

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 22–28°C, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 16°C + tmax ≥ 26°C)
