Context

Our verdict

Labuan Bajo, gateway port to Komodo National Park on the island of Flores in Indonesia: 7000 inhabitants, transformed in a few years (since 2015) from a small fishing village into a major tourist hub. Designated by the Indonesian government as one of the "5 new Bali destinations" to decongest Balinese tourism (4 others: Lake Toba, Borobudur, Mandalika Lombok, Likupang). August is my favourite month (8.8/10): 22-29°C, 23% rain, this is the ideal dry season. From June to September, it's the dry season: 22-30°C, this is the tourist high season, hotel prices x2-x3, golden landscapes (burnt savannah). From December to March, it's the rainy season: 84-94% rainfall, sometimes rough sea, greener landscapes. Labuan Bajo highlights: Cunca Wulang Waterfall 30 min away (waterfall in the jungle, swimming, jumps), Cunca Rami Waterfall (other waterfall), Batu Cermin Cave ("Mirror Cave", cave with stalactites lit by filtering sun, marine fossils indicating that the cave was underwater), Paradise Bar (panoramic sunset over the bay). Essential excursions = this is why one comes to Labuan Bajo: Komodo National Park by 1-day or 2-3 day boat tour (with dragons at Komodo or Rinca, Padar viewpoint, Pink Beach, Manta Point, Kanawa Island), 3-7 day Liveaboard in the park (ideal formula for divers and the complete experience, 200-1000€/day depending on category). Diving: Labuan Bajo is one of the world bases for Komodo diving (Top 5 worldwide), renowned centres Manta Rhei, Wicked Diving, Dive Komodo. Standard 1-day excursion Komodo: 550,000-1 million IDR (35-65€/person) with BBQ lunch, snorkelling, dragons at Rinca (more accessible than Komodo), Padar viewpoint, Pink Beach. Surrounding islands: Kanawa, Kelor, Sebayur, Pink Beach, Bidadari. Flagship hotels: Plataran Komodo Resort, Ayana Komodo Resort, Sudamala Resort, Le Pirate Boatel (floating hotel). Try ikan bakar (grilled fish), nasi goreng, kolak (coconut milk fruit dessert), arak Flores (very strong local eau-de-vie). Audience: international divers, ecotourists, wealthy on private yachts (the bay of Labuan Bajo has become in recent years a luxury mooring). Logistics: Labuan Bajo Komodo airport (LBJ) with direct flights from Bali Denpasar (1h), Jakarta (2h30), Makassar.
