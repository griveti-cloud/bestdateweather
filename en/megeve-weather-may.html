Context

Our verdict

Megève, in Haute-Savoie: 3000 inhabitants at 1100m, founded in 1916 by Noémie de Rothschild as the French alternative to Saint-Moritz, village-resort with preserved charm (concrete tower blocks banned, traditional Savoyard chalet architecture strictly enforced). August is my favourite month (8.1/10): 11-23°C, 45% rain in mountain storms, alpine hiking, 18-hole golf, opera and baroque music festival. From June to September, expect 4-23°C: active summer season. From December to March, expect -5/8°C and 42-46% precipitation often as snow: prime ski season. Pistes: 445 km Évasion Mont-Blanc (Megève + Saint-Gervais + Combloux + La Giettaz + Praz-sur-Arly), modest altitude for Haute-Savoie (1100-2350m, so snow cover sometimes patchy before Christmas and after Easter). Mont d'Arbois, Côte 2000, Rochebrune (3 separate sectors, free shuttles). Megève is less technical than Val d'Isère or Tignes, gentler and more family-oriented, upmarket class. Crowd: French and Belgian aristocracy, well-off families, more discreet than Courchevel. Hotels: Les Fermes de Marie (Sibuet, 5* relais & châteaux), Four Seasons Megève (5*+ opened 2017), Les Loges Blanches, Mont-Blanc (historic palace), 250-2500€/night in peak season. Try tartiflette, Savoyard fondue, raclette, diots, génépi, Savoie wines. Logistics: Megève 1h15 from Geneva by car (Geneva airport is the fastest access for Haute-Savoie from Paris).

Frequently asked questions

When is the best time to visit Megève?
It depends on the activity. For skiing: January (ski score 8.8/10). For hiking/summer: January (8.8/10, 4.0°C).
Can you ski in Megève in winter?
Yes, it's peak ski season. Average ski score December–February: 8.4/10. Cold temperatures (4°C max in January) and frequent snowfall.
Is it warm in Megève in summer?
In January, temperatures average 4.0°C. Best period for hiking and outdoor activities.
What is the wettest month in Megève?
November is the wettest month with November has the lowest climate score of the year.
