Context

Our verdict

Samos, Greek island in the north of the Dodecanese archipelago 1.3 km from the Turkish coast (the closest of the Greek islands to a foreign coast): 33,000 inhabitants over 477 km². Greener than the Cyclades thanks to its abundant water sources (Nestlé Samos mineral water), mountains including Kerkis 1433m. Birthplace of Pythagoras (570 BC), of the philosopher Epicurus (341 BC) and of the painter Aristarchus. UNESCO 1992 for the sanctuary of Hera and the Tunnel of Eupalinos. June is my favourite month (9.5/10): 21-28°C, 13% rain, sea at 22°C, ideal temperatures. September stays excellent (9.4/10). July-August drop to 6.9/10 due to the heat (24-32°C). From November to March, expect 9-19°C and 33-46% rain: mild but relatively rainy (Samos receives more rain than the Cyclades thanks to its mountains). Highlights: Heraion of Samos (UNESCO, sanctuary of Hera 8th century BC, one of the largest sanctuaries of ancient Greece, modest remains but major historical site), Tunnel of Eupalinos (UNESCO, aqueduct tunnel dug into the mountain in the 6th century BC at the order of the tyrant Polycrates, 1036m long dug simultaneously from both sides and meeting with a gap of only 60 cm = major technical feat of Antiquity, accessible to visitors over 200m), Pythagoreion (UNESCO, ancient port at the current location of Pythagorio = 2nd town of Samos, ancient fortifications, birthplace of Pythagoras symbolised by the port), Vathy Archaeological Museum (capital of the island, monumental kouros statues), Vathy/Samos Town (charming capital in an arc on the port), Pythagorio (the most famous port, more touristy atmosphere), Tsamadou Beach (the most beautiful pebbles, naturist on part), Lemonakia Beach, Kokkari (fishing village in the north, the most picturesque on the island), Manolates (picturesque mountain village, panoramic terraces), Karlovasi (3rd town, ferry port to Athens), Pythagoras Springs karst caves. Excursions: Kuşadası Turkey 1h by ferry (then Ephesus UNESCO 30 min, the main reason for staying in Samos for many), Patmos (UNESCO, island of the apocalypse of Saint John), Ikaria (neighbouring island, one of the world's 5 "Blue Zones" with exceptional longevity of inhabitants 90+ years). Try Samos Vin Doux Naturel (sweet liqueur wine made from white Muscat à petits grains, AOC since 1971, the most prestigious of Greek sweet wines), souvlaki, moussaka, local feta, thyme honey. Samos airport (SMI).
