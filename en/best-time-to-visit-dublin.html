Frequently asked questions

July is the best month with 19°C highs, 13°C lows, 7.3h of sunshine and 47% rainy days. The months of July & August offer comparable conditions.
Dublin averages 7.3 sunshine hours per day in July. It is the sunniest month with a climate score of 7.3/10.
December is the most unfavourable month with 45% rainy days and the lowest climate score of the year.
In July, overnight lows reach 13°C, while afternoon highs average 19°C.
In winter (December-February), Dublin scores 4.1/10 on average — Difficult period — prefer the peak season.. The best month overall remains July with a score of 7.3/10.
