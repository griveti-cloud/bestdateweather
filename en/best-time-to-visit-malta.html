G
Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Malta is the EU's smallest member state: 316 km², 540,000 inhabitants, 3 islands (Malta, Gozo, Comino), thousands-of-years history (Phoenicians, Romans, Arabs, Normans, Knights of the Order of Saint John 1530-1798, British 1814-1964). June is my favourite month (9.7/10): 21-27°C, 3% rain, sea at 23°C, ideal weather before the heatwave. July-August drop to 6.9/10 due to heat (24-30°C) and extreme crowds: Malta concentrates 3 million tourists/year for 540,000 inhabitants, hotel prices x3, packed beaches. From December to March, expect 11-17°C and 21-37% rain: mild for Europe, ideal climate for visiting culture without crowds, European golfers wintering. September is also excellent (9.1/10). Highlights: Valletta (UNESCO capital, founded by the Knights in 1566, St John's Co-Cathedral with Caravaggios, Grand Master's Palace), Mdina ("Silent City", former fortified capital), Hal Saflieni Hypogeum (UNESCO, 4000-year-old underground temple, book 3 months ahead, only 100 visitors/day), Megalithic Temples of Hagar Qim and Mnajdra (UNESCO, 5000 years old, predating the pyramids), Marsaxlokk (fishing harbour with colourful luzzu boats, Sunday market). Gozo 25 min by ferry (quieter island, Citadella in Victoria, Marsalforn salt pans, Dwejra Bay). Comino and its Blue Lagoon (turquoise water, snorkelling, accessible by boat from Malta/Gozo). Sliema and St Julian's = modern beach resorts. Try pastizzi (cheese/peas pastry), rabbit stew (local rabbit), ftira, Marsovin/Meridiana wine.

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 22–28°C, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 16°C + tmax ≥ 26°C)

Frequently asked questions

What is the best time to visit Malta?
June is the best month with 27.0°C highs, 21.0°C lows, 13.5h of sunshine and 3.0% rainy days. The months of June & September offer comparable conditions.
How many hours of sunshine does Malta get in June?
Malta averages 13.5 sunshine hours per day in June. It is the sunniest month with a climate score of 9.8/10.

What are the temperatures at night in Malta in June?
In June, overnight lows reach 21.0°C, while afternoon highs average 27.0°C.
Is Malta good in winter?
In winter (December-February), Malta scores 7.8/10 on average — Decent period conditions for cultural visits.. The best month overall remains June with a score of 9.8/10.
When should you avoid Malta?
July is the most difficult month to visit Malta, with July has the lowest climate score of the year. Climate conditions are at their lowest of the year during this period.
