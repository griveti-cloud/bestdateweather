G
Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Killington, in Vermont, is the largest ski resort in the American East: 6 interconnected mountains, 155 pistes. Particularly long season (October to May in some years) thanks to an advanced snow cannon system. July is my favourite month (7.7/10): 11-20°C, 48% rain, good for the hike to Killington Peak and Camel's Hump. From December to March, expect -16/-2°C: ski season. The resort hosts the women's Alpine Ski World Cup in late November (Killington Cup giant slalom), 1st American stage. From April to May and October-November, it's the slow shoulder season, except for the start of the World Cup season. Vermont in autumn (foliage, mid-September to mid-October) is another reason to come: maple colours. Boston 3h by road, NYC 4h30. More accessible to New Yorkers than the Colorado Rockies.

How we calculate the score

For mountain destinations, we calculate two scores per month — skiing and hiking — and display the higher of the two.

⛷️Ski score DEC → MAY
  • 40% snow cover (slope T° corrected by lapse rate -6.5°C/1000m)
  • 40% winter thermal comfort (-5 to 5°C optimal)
  • 20% sunshine
  • Off-season penalty: ×0.70 adjacent months · ×0.35 outside season
🥾Hiking score JUN → SEP
  • 40% temperature (10–22°C optimal)
  • 40% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 20% sunshine

Questions

Frequently asked questions

When is the best time to visit Killington?
It depends on the activity. For skiing: March (ski score 8.7/10). For hiking/summer: March (8.7/10, -2.0°C).
Can you ski in Killington in winter?
Yes, it's peak ski season. Average ski score December–February: 7.3/10. Cold temperatures (-7°C max in January) and frequent snowfall.
Is it warm in Killington in summer?
In March, temperatures average -2.0°C. Best period for hiking and outdoor activities.
What is the wettest month in Killington?
October is the wettest month with October has the lowest climate score of the year.
