Local context

What to expect in May

Spring marks the start of the good season in Poznań. Temperatures rise (18°C) and tourists have not yet arrived en masse. Expect rain on 32% of days — a light raincoat in the bag is recommended. Not the best window, but acceptable if dates are constrained. June (8.1/10) is significantly better if possible.
