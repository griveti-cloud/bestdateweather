What to expect in May

🎯 Late tulip bloom at Keukenhof (closes mid-May). Last chance Spring marks the start of the good season in Amsterdam. Temperatures rise (18°C) and tourists have not yet arrived en masse. Expect rain on 40% of days — a light raincoat in the bag is recommended. Not the best window, but acceptable if dates are constrained. August (10.0/10) is significantly better if possible.
