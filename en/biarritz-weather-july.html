What to expect in July

Context

Our verdict

Biarritz, the chic beach resort of the French Basque Country: former imperial spa town (Eugénie de Montijo), Europe's surfing capital (since 1957). July is my favourite month (8.5/10): 18-23°C, 32% rain, ideal temperatures, surfers everywhere. August stays excellent (8.4/10) but with massive crowds. From June to September is peak season: 16-24°C, sea at 19-22°C, hotel prices x2-x3. From November to March, expect 6-15°C and 44-52% rain: oceanic and damp, but this is when the big Atlantic swells arrive for experienced surfers (3-5m waves). Highlights: Grande Plage, Plage de la Côte des Basques (historic surf spot), Rocher de la Vierge, Hôtel du Palais (historic 5-star), Biarritz Lighthouse. Aquarium (Cité de l'Océan). Try piperade, axoa de veau, gâteau basque. Saint-Jean-de-Luz 20 min away (more traditional Basque village), San Sebastián 1h away in Spain (pintxos, La Concha beach). May-June and September are the best crowd/weather compromises.
