Context

Our verdict

Arusha is the capital of Tanzanian safari: Kilimanjaro airport 1h away, agencies for Serengeti, Ngorongoro, Tarangire, Manyara. At 1400m altitude, temperate African climate. September is my favourite month (8.8/10): 14-25°C, 19% rain, the right moment between the dry season and the first rains. From June to October, it's the great dry season: wildebeest migration in the Serengeti (Mara River crossing in July-August, peak of 1.5 million animals). From April to May are the long rains (56-90% rainfall): many lodges close or reduce rates (-50%), tracks sometimes impassable, but animals still present in green concentrations. From November to May, it's the green season that follows, with baby season (newborn wildebeest in February in the south of the Serengeti). Plan 200-1500$/night depending on lodge type. The Maasai Market in Arusha for souvenirs and shuka fabrics. Mount Meru (4566m) for acclimatisation before Kilimanjaro.
