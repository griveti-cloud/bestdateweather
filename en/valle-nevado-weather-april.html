Context

Our verdict

Valle Nevado, 1h30 from Santiago de Chile, this is the most accessible South American ski resort for Europeans. Southern hemisphere: open from June to October, ideal for Europeans wanting to ski in summer. January-February are my favourite months (7.5-7.6/10): 7-16°C, 31-38% rain, austral summer, good for altitude hiking. From June to September, expect -5/3°C: ski season. At 3000m altitude, South exposure (cold side in southern hemisphere), reliable snow. The shared Tres Valles area (with La Parva and El Colorado) reaches 100 km of pistes, the largest in South America. From April to May and October-November, it's the off-season: road closed to non-residents, resort closed. Santiago 1h30 by switchback road (50 hairpin bends). Expect 700-1500€ for 6 days all-inclusive in ski season. Mendoza (Argentina) accessible east side but pass closed in winter.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

When is the best time to visit Valle Nevado?
It depends on the activity. For skiing: August (ski score 8.9/10). For hiking/summer: August (8.9/10, 2.0°C).
Can you ski in Valle Nevado in winter?
Yes, it's peak ski season. Average ski score December–February: 1.2/10. Cold temperatures (16°C max in January) and frequent snowfall.
Is it warm in Valle Nevado in summer?
In August, temperatures average 2.0°C. Best period for hiking and outdoor activities.
What is the wettest month in Valle Nevado?
May is the wettest month with May has the lowest climate score of the year.
