Context

Our verdict

Chongqing, largest municipality in China in the heart of the south-west on the Yangtze River: 32 million inhabitants in metropolitan area + direct municipality status (equivalent Beijing-Shanghai-Tianjin) = largest urban zone in the world by administrative area 82,000 km² (larger than Austria). "Mountain-City" (Shancheng 山城) built on steep hills overlooking the Yangtze-Jialing confluence, cyberpunk cinematographic atmosphere with skyscrapers on stairs, monorail crossing buildings, urban escalators. September is my favourite month (7.7/10): 19-30°C, 41% rain, ideal temperatures. April-May stay excellent (7.6/10). From March to May and September-October, these are the best seasons: 12-30°C. From June to August, it's the humid heatwave: 22-37°C with 51-78% rain + fogs = Chongqing is one of the "3 Furnaces of China" with Wuhan + Nanjing (stifling river climate in summer). From December to February, expect 5-12°C with 34-66% rain: mild but foggy winter. Highlights: Hongyadong ("Hongya complex", 11 storeys of traditional Tujia stilt-house architecture on the Yangtze bank, illuminated at night in gold, compared to the world of "Spirited Away" Studio Ghibli, viral Instagram photography), Liziba Light Rail Station (monorail station crossing DIRECTLY at the heart of a 19-storey residential building = unique-in-the-world urban planning experience iconic view), Jiefangbei ("Liberation Monument", central pedestrian square surrounded by modern skyscrapers), Eling Park (iconic night panorama over the cyberpunk skyline of Chongqing at the confluence), Two Rivers Cruise (night cruise on the Yangtze + Jialing with view of the illuminated skyscrapers iconic), Ciqikou Ancient Town (preserved Ming-Qing old town 30 min from the centre, traditional atmosphere + restaurants), Dazu Rock Carvings (UNESCO 1999, Buddhist-Taoist-Confucian rock sculptures 9th-13th century = one of the largest sets of rock sculptures in China after Longmen + Mogao + Yungang Buddhist Caves), 3 Gorges Cruise (3-4 day cruise Chongqing-Yichang on the Yangtze through the Three Gorges = spectacular landscapes + visit to the Three Gorges Dam = the largest hydroelectric dam in the world 22,500 MW, controversial for displacement of 1.4 million inhabitants 1993-2009, major Chinese tourist iconic), Wulong Karst (UNESCO 2007, karst landscapes 3 natural bridges Tianlong-Heilong-Qinglong = filming location "Transformers 4" Hollywood 2014). Chongqing cuisine), Xiaomian (iconic spicy noodles for local breakfast), Chongqing chicken ("chicken with chillies" dry spicy dish), Yangtze seafood. Excursions: Chengdu 1h30 by high-speed train (neighbouring Sichuan capital, already covered by BDW, alternative Sichuan cuisine + giant pandas), Yichang end of the Yangtze. Logistics: Chongqing Jiangbei airport (CKG), direct train major Chinese cities.
