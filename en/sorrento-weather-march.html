Context

Our verdict

Sorrento, smart resort on the northern Amalfi Coast in Italy: 16,000 inhabitants south of the Gulf of Naples, perched 50m on a volcanic tuff cliff. Privileged base for visiting the Amalfi Coast, Capri, Pompeii, Herculaneum, Vesuvius and Naples. Spectacular view over the Gulf of Naples with Vesuvius in the background. June is my favourite month (9.4/10): 20-27°C, 17% rain, sea at 22°C, ideal temperatures before the heatwave. May and September stay excellent (8.1-8.6/10). July-August drop to 6.9/10 due to the heat (23-30°C) and the extreme crowds (Americans in the majority 50% of the clientele, British, hotel prices x4-x5, full restaurants, congested road). From November to March, expect 7-18°C and 38-55% rain: mild but rainy, many hotels close, ideal for calm. Highlights: Historic centre (pedestrian old town, Piazza Tasso = main square dedicated to the poet Torquato Tasso born in Sorrento 1544), Marina Grande (picturesque fishing port on the rocks below, accessible by stairs from the centre, seafood restaurants), Marina Piccola (modern marina, ferries Capri/Naples), Bagni della Regina Giovanna ("Baths of Queen Joan", natural pool on the rocks 4 km to the west, ruins of a Roman villa, swimming between rocks through a natural arch), Sorrento Cathedral (Duomo, 11th-15th century), Cloister of San Francesco (13th century with iconic orange trees), Villa Comunale (public garden on the cliff edge, sunset panorama over the bay = local institution), Museo Correale di Terranova (18th-century palace with Neapolitan art), Valley of the Mills (Valle dei Mulini, sunken valley in the heart of the city with ruins of old mills overgrown with tropical vegetation, mysterious atmosphere). Essential excursions: Capri 25 min by hydrofoil (iconic island, Grotta Azzurra, Anacapri, Villa San Michele, Faraglioni), Amalfi Coast 1h away by SITA Sud bus (vertiginous winding road, Positano-Amalfi-Ravello — already covered by BDW), Pompeii 30 min by Circumvesuviana train (UNESCO, Roman city buried 79 AD), Herculaneum 30 min away (UNESCO, similar but better preserved), Vesuvius (volcano ascent 1281m, panorama over the bay), Ischia and Procida 1h away by ferry (already covered by BDW), Naples 1h by train (Napoli culinary capital). Try gnocchi alla sorrentina (gnocchi tomato mozzarella basil), spaghetti alle vongole, pasta al limone, mozzarella di bufala, sfogliatella (Neapolitan pastry), limoncello from Sorrento (local production, IGP "limoni di Sorrento" lemons). Naples airport (NAP) 1h away by train.
