Context

Our verdict

Ninh Binh, province in northern Vietnam 100 km south of Hanoi: 160,000 inhabitants. Nicknamed the "Halong Bay on land" for its spectacular karst landscapes (limestone peaks rising from the rice paddies and rivers) similar to those of Halong Bay but in green farming country. UNESCO 2014 for the Trang An landscape complex. April is my favourite month (8.1/10): 23-28°C, 30% rain, ideal temperatures. From February to April and October-November, expect 16-28°C: excellent period. From May to August, expect 25-34°C: heavy heat, but also the rice paddies in gold at harvest time (June = spectacular landscapes with karst peaks against golden paddies). From September to December, expect 19-31°C and 33-77% rain, season of the 2nd harvest (landscapes also golden in October). From January to February, expect 15-23°C: relative cold for Vietnam, more mystical mists. Highlights: Tam Coc ("3 caves", boat ride pulled by the rowers' feet on the Ngo Dong river through 3 natural tunnels crossing the karst peaks, 2h return, iconic landscapes), Trang An (UNESCO, alternative to Tam Coc, longer 3h return, crossing 9 caves/temples, more traditional but more touristy, choice between 3 itineraries), Hoa Lu (former capital of Vietnam in the 10th-11th centuries before the transfer to Hanoi in 1010, temples Dinh Tien Hoang and Le Dai Hanh), Bich Dong Pagoda ("Crystal Pagoda", 3 levels in a 15th-century cave), Hang Mua ("Mua Caves", 500 steps to climb to the summit of the Lying Dragon peak, iconic panorama over Tam Coc, one of the viral photos of Vietnam), Pagoda Bai Dinh (the largest Buddhist pagoda complex in South-East Asia, created in 2003-2010, 100-tonne bronze Buddha), Cuc Phuong National Park (Vietnam's oldest national park, created 1962, primates in sanctuary). Activities: cycling in the countryside (ideal alternative to the overloaded boats), trekking in the rice paddies, horse rides. Excursions: Hanoi 2h by train or bus, Halong Bay 3h away, Mai Chau 4h away (Thai minorities valley). Try goat (Ninh Binh specialty, very locally renowned, thit de = goat grilled with sesame), com chay (crispy burnt rice), bun cha. No airport, train transfer from Hanoi 2h.
