Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Toledo, in central Spain: 85,000 inhabitants, former capital of Visigothic then Muslim then Christian Spain (capital until 1561 when Philip II moved it to Madrid), UNESCO 1986. "City of the 3 cultures" (Christian, Jewish, Muslim coexisting from the 8th to the 15th century). June is my favourite month (9.2/10): 17-30°C, 13% rain, ideal temperatures before the July-August heatwave. September stays excellent (9.0/10). From June to September, it's the tourist high season: 12-35°C, but also extreme crowds at midday (Toledo = classic day trip from Madrid 30 min by AVE train, 8 million visitors/year for 85,000 inhabitants). From July-August drop to 6.9/10 due to extreme heat (21-35°C, sometimes 42°C, the Meseta plateau interior). From December to February, expect 2-14°C and 18-23% rain: dry winter cold, but Toledo in winter = spectacular medieval atmosphere without crowds. Highlights: Cathedral Saint Mary (French Gothic 13th-15th century, one of Spain's most impressive, altarpieces, sacristy with 18 Grecos), Alcázar (perched fortress, Renaissance military architecture, Republican defeat in 1936 = episode mythologised by Francoists), Jewish quarter (Judería) (Santa María la Blanca synagogue 12th century with Moorish arches, del Tránsito synagogue 14th century with Sephardic museum, one of the only two preserved medieval synagogues in Spain along with Cordoba), Iglesia de Santo Tomé (Burial of the Count of Orgaz, El Greco masterpiece 1586), El Greco House-Museum (Cretan artist who died in Toledo in 1614), Cristo de la Luz Mosque (10th century, one of the only mosques preserved in Christian Spain), Alcántara Bridge (Roman) and San Martín Bridge (medieval), Mirador del Valle (signature panorama over the fortified city encircled by the Tagus). Try mazapán de Toledo (marzipan since the 12th century), Toledo-style partridge, queso manchego.

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 22–28°C, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 16°C + tmax ≥ 26°C)
