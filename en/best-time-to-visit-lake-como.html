G
Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Lake Como, in an inverted-Y shape, 50 km long, 410m deep (one of the deepest in Europe). George Clooney and Hollywood villas (Bellagio especially). July-August are my favourite months (8.4/10): 21-28°C, 43-50% rain in afternoon storms, swimming in the lake (water at 24°C). From December to February, expect 4-11°C and 24-30% rain: often grey sky (mist over the lake), many villas and restaurants close. Highlights: Como (Duomo, funicular to Brunate for the view), Bellagio (pearl of the lake, gardens of Villa Melzi and Villa Serbelloni), Varenna (charming village, Villa Monastero), Tremezzo (Villa Carlotta, Italian gardens), Villa del Balbianello at Lenno (used in Star Wars Episode II and James Bond Casino Royale). Navigazione Lago di Como boats serving all the villages. Try risotto al pesce persico, missoltini (dried fish), polenta uncia, local cheeses (Bitto). Milan 1h by train, Switzerland (Lugano) 30 min by car. May-June and September-October are the best crowd/weather compromises.

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 22–28°C, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 16°C + tmax ≥ 26°C)
