Context

Our verdict

Rodrigues, smallest island of the Republic of Mauritius in the Indian Ocean: 42,000 inhabitants on 109 km², 560 km east of the main island Mauritius (1h30 by plane). Discovered 1528 by the Portuguese Diogo Rodrigues hence the name, twin sister of Mauritius all proportions kept (similar climate but more windy, similar landscapes but wild preserved from mass tourism, population less than 1% of Mauritius). October is my favourite month (8.2/10): 21-22°C, 42% rain, end of the cool season. From October to December and April-May, these are the best seasons: 20-25°C, 42-66% rain. From November to April, it's the warm season (21-26°C, sea at 26°C). From June to September, it's the cool season ("austral winter"): 19-22°C, 45-54% rain, steady trade winds. Rodrigues never tops 8.2/10 (oceanic tropical climate with trade winds). Highlights: Port Mathurin (capital, 6000 inhabitants, lively Saturday morning market with local products and craftsmanship), Mount Limon (398m, highest point, 360° panorama over the island), Beaches: Saint-François (the most beautiful in the south-west), Trou d'Argent (turquoise water creek accessible only by 1h coastal path, paradise), Anse aux Cocos (long beach with coconut trees), Pointe Cotton, Graviers, Saint-Gabriel (1936 wooden church). Île aux Cocos by boat (nature reserve with masked boobies and brown noddies, paradise beach), Île aux Sables, Caverne Patate (underground limestone caves). Activities: kitesurf and windsurf (Rodrigues is one of the world spots for the PWA thanks to constant trade winds 25-35 knots, base at Anse Mourouk), big game fishing (sailfish, marlin), cycling (island crossed east to west 18 km), diving (largest lagoon in Africa 240 km², preserved corals, green turtles nest at Pointe Cotton). Try ourite (grilled octopus local specialty), palmiste salad, lemon pickles, achards, arranged rum, Rodrigues honey. Logistics: Rodrigues 1h30 by plane from Mauritius (1-2 flights/day Air Mauritius), MV Trochetia boat 36h (little used).
