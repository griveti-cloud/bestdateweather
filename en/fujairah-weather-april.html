The month in detail

What to expect in April

Spring marks the start of the good season in Fujairah. Temperatures rise (35.0°C) and tourists have not yet arrived en masse. Rain is virtually absent (4.0% of days) — ideal for planning without a backup. Not the best window, but acceptable if dates are constrained. March (9.2/10) is significantly better if possible.

Context

Our verdict

Fujairah, one of the 7 United Arab Emirates and only emirate on the east coast on the Gulf of Oman: 250,000 inhabitants. Only emirate without coast on the Persian Gulf = major climatic benefit (cooler water, ocean breezes, less humid) + spectacular Hajar mountain landscapes. "Nature and diving" alternative to Dubai for Emirati locals and expatriates. December-February-March are my favourite months (9.0/10): 16-29°C, 7-11% rain, this is the ideal dry season. January-November stay excellent (6.8-9.0/10). From November to April, it's the tourist high season: 16-35°C, sea at 22-26°C. From May to October drop to 3.6-6.9/10 due to extreme heatwave + coastal humidity (29-39°C, sometimes 47°C+). Highlights: Fujairah Beach (finer sand and more turquoise water than on the Persian Gulf side Dubai/RAK thanks to position on Indian Ocean-Sea of Oman), Fujairah Fort (16th-17th century, one of the oldest forts in the UAE), Sheikh Zayed Mosque Fujairah (2015, main mosque of the emirate), Al Bidya Mosque (the oldest mosque in the UAE, 15th century, tradition saying it was built by a man in a single day, unique design with 4 ornamental mud domes iconic), Wadi Al Wurayah (national park, one of the only UAE wadis with permanent water, waterfalls, 4x4 hike), Hatta (Dubai enclave in the Hajar mountains 30 min from Fujairah, accessible from Fujairah, picturesque green lakes, kayaking, traditional Hatta Heritage Village), Snoopy Island (rocky islet shaped like Snoopy the dog, iconic of Fujairah, snorkelling from the beach with nearby coral reef), Khor Fakkan (Sharjah enclave in Fujairah, urban beach, fishing port, more local atmosphere). Activities: Sea of Oman diving (Fujairah is the main UAE diving base, major sites Martini Rock, Dibba Rock, Inchcape 1 and 2 wrecks = wrecks voluntarily sunk for artificial reefs, hammerhead sharks possible October-December, one of the international Gulf divers' bases), preserved Snoopy beaches, Al Aqah, Hajar Mountains hiking, Friday-fish market at Khor Fakkan. Hotels: Le Méridien Al Aqah Beach Resort, Fujairah Rotana Resort, Iberotel Miramar Al Aqah, prices 30-40% lower than Dubai. Excursions: Dubai 2h by car (motorway crossing the desert and Hajar mountains), Sharjah 1h30 (already covered by BDW), Muscat Oman 4h by car (Hatta border + car, already covered by BDW), Khasab Oman (Musandam Peninsula, spectacular fjords, dolphins, accessible in 1h from Fujairah). Try Emirati machboos, balaleet (sweet vermicelli eggs), harees, luqaimat. Fujairah International airport (FJR) modest, alternative Dubai DXB or Sharjah SHJ airport 1h-1h30 more practical for international flights.
