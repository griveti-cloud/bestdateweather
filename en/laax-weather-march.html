Context

Our verdict

Laax, ski resort in the Grisons Alps in the Vorderrhein valley in Switzerland: 1900 inhabitants at 1100m altitude. Capital of snowboard and freestyle in Switzerland, one of the most avant-garde European resorts for snow culture (the Crap Sogn Gion park has the largest half-pipe in the world 200m long 22m wide). Linked area Flims-Laax-Falera = 224 km of pistes 28 lifts, one of the largest in Romansh-Grisons Switzerland. August is my favourite month (7.7/10): 9-21°C, 50% rain, ideal temperatures for alpine hiking. Laax never tops 7.7/10 (alpine climate with narrow season). From June to September, it's the summer high season: 3-21°C, Grisons hiking, mountain bike (Laax = renowned summer bike park), kayaking, paragliding, Lake Cauma alpine turquoise Caumasee accessible by funicular, swimming in emerald water 18-22°C in midsummer. From December to March, expect -8/0°C and 37-65% precipitation often as snow: ski high season. Pistes: 224 km Flims-Laax-Falera, altitude 1100-3018m (Vorab Glacier), vertical drop 1918m, 28 lifts including modern 3S Vorab gondolas. iconic pistes: FIS Pista da Vauz (occasional World Cup descent), Vorab Glacier (3018m, summer skiing possible some years). Snowpark Laax = considered one of the top 5 in the world with 90 obstacles + the largest natural half-pipe in the world, host of the Burton European Open, freestyle world cup stage, attracts international pros. Off-piste: 3 legally groomed off-piste routes ("freeride routes" marked secured) = unique practice in Switzerland with Verbier, accessible to intermediate-advanced skiers. The Roundshot = panoramic 360° rotating restaurant at 2228m, iconic view of Laax. Strengths: Riders Hotel (design hotel conceived by snowboarders, hostel-chic atmosphere, 50€/night dorms accessible to young-budget clientele = unique in expensive Swiss resort), Galaaxy apple-educative-cultural. Audience: Swiss Germans in the majority (Zurich 1h30 by car), Germans, many British, international snowboarders, younger and cooler atmosphere than Verbier or Saint Moritz. Hotels: Rocksresort (5* modern Domenig design 2009), Riders Hotel, prices 150-700€/night. Excursions: Saint Moritz 2h away (already covered by BDW), Davos 1h30 away (annual economic forum + skiing), Chur 30 min away (the oldest town in Switzerland 5000 years), Glacier Express Saint Moritz-Zermatt crosses neighbouring Laax. Try capuns (iconic Grisons ravioli), pizokels (Grisons gnocchi), Bündner fleisch (IGP dried meat), nusstorte walnut cake, Veltliner red wine. Logistics: Laax 1h30 from Zurich airport (ZRH) by car/train, direct access without painful transfers.
