Context

Our verdict

Furano, town in Hokkaido 200 km north-east of Sapporo: 22,000 inhabitants in the heart of Hokkaido (north island of Japan), famous for its lavender fields in July-August (the largest in Asia, to see at Farm Tomita) and its renowned winter ski resort. July is my favourite month (8.1/10): 17-26°C, 48% rain, this is the full summer tourist season (lavender in full bloom from 20 June to 20 August, peak mid-July to mid-August, crowds of Japanese and Asian tourists for photos). From June to September, expect 5-26°C: summer high season (colourful flower fields Patchwork of nature, golf, cycling). From December to March, expect -11/3°C and 48-74% precipitation often as snow (12m/season, among the snowiest resorts in the world after Niseko, legendary "Japan Powder"): ski high season. Pistes: 23 pistes Furano Ski Resort (managed by Prince Hotels), altitude 235-1074m, vertical drop 839m, gondola access 8 min from the village. Off-piste Furano (with guide, crossroads of the Australians), Furano Backcountry very renowned. Asahi-dake 1h away (highest volcano in Hokkaido 2291m, ski touring). Try Furano melon (the most expensive melon in Japan, can reach 200€ for 1 superior quality melon), cantaloupe melon ice cream, white asparagus, Furano cheese (rare in Japan), Hokkaido sake. Audience: Japanese and Asians in the majority (summer lavender), Australians and Europeans in winter (skiing). Hotels: New Furano Prince Hotel, Furano Natulux Hotel, ryokans with onsen. Logistics: Furano 2h from Sapporo by train (from Sapporo Chitose Airport + Asahikawa train + Furano train), 1h from Asahikawa regional airport.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

When is the best time to visit Furano?
It depends on the activity. For skiing: March (ski score 9.1/10). For hiking/summer: March (9.1/10, 3.0°C).
Can you ski in Furano in winter?
Yes, it's peak ski season. Average ski score December–February: 7.8/10. Cold temperatures (-4°C max in January) and frequent snowfall.
Is it warm in Furano in summer?
In March, temperatures average 3.0°C. Best period for hiking and outdoor activities.
What is the wettest month in Furano?
November is the wettest month with November has the lowest climate score of the year.
