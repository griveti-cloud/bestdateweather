Context

Our verdict

Shanghai plays its role as economic capital of modern China: futuristic Pudong skyline, colonial Bund heritage, 26 million inhabitants. May and October are my favourite months (8.0/10): 16-25°C, 25-37% rain, mild temperatures between summer heatwave and damp winter. July-August are oppressive (26-32°C, max humidity), Shanghainese leaving on holiday: city strangely quieter. From December to February, expect 2-11°C and 20-31% rain: damp cold that gets through, often grey days. The Bund at sunset for the East-West architectural contrasts, Yu Garden and the old town, Pudong (Shanghai Tower 632m, observatory), French Concession for the village atmosphere and trendy cafés. Try xiaolongbao at Din Tai Fung or Jia Jia Tang Bao, hairy crab (autumn season only). Very efficient metro, Maglev from Pudong airport (430 km/h, 8 min to Longyang). 144h transit visa free possible.
