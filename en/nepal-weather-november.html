What to expect in November

🎯 One of the best months — 21°C, maximum sunshine and ideal conditions Autumn in Nepal is often underrated: 21°C, golden light and declining crowds. An interesting window. Rain is virtually absent (9% of days) — ideal for planning without a backup. A good balance between weather and crowds, even if November (8.3/10) remains theoretically better.
