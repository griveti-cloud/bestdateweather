Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Yellowstone, the 1st national park in the world (created 1872): 9000 km² in Wyoming-Montana-Idaho (USA), sitting on a dormant super-volcano, unique geothermal landscapes (geysers, coloured hot springs, fumaroles). July is my favourite month (8.8/10): 8-22°C, 28% rain, this is the tourist high season (4 million visitors/year, 70% in June-August), all interior roads open, visible wildlife (bison, elk, bears, wolves). August stays excellent (8.3/10). From June to September, it's the high season: 3-22°C, but also extreme crowds at the main sites (Old Faithful overrun, 1-2h traffic jams to see the bears), hotel booking 1 year ahead for the historic lodges (Old Faithful Inn, Lake Yellowstone Hotel). From November to March, expect -12/-1°C and 37-50% precipitation often as snow: Yellowstone in winter is spectacular (everything white, very few tourists, wildlife visible more easily, sky often clear), but interior roads closed except Mammoth-Cooke City accessible year-round + snowcoach or snowmobile tour mandatory to explore. From April to May and September-October are the transitions (late spring, short autumn). Highlights: Old Faithful (the most famous geyser, eruption every 90 min ±10 min, 30-55m high), Grand Prismatic Spring (110m diameter hot spring, spectacular colours, viewpoint Fairy Falls Trail 800m walk), Mammoth Hot Springs (white limestone terraces), Grand Canyon of the Yellowstone (Lower Falls 94m), Lamar Valley ("American Serengeti" for wildlife, sunrise or sunset), Hayden Valley (bison), Lake Yellowstone (the largest alpine lake in the USA). Safety: numerous black bears and grizzlies (bear spray mandatory for back-country hikes), aggressive bison (stay 25m away). Try bison burger. Logistics: nearest airport Bozeman (Montana, 90 min from West Entrance) or Jackson Hole (Wyoming, 1h from South Entrance).

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 22–28°C, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 16°C + tmax ≥ 26°C)
