Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Havana, Cuban capital: 2 million inhabitants, museum-city stuck in the 1950s (American embargo since 1962, vintage American cars, dilapidated but charming colonial buildings). February is my favourite month (8.8/10): 21-29°C, 23% rain, end of the dry season, ideal temperatures. From December to April, it's the high season: 20-31°C, relative dry climate, hotel prices x2-x3. From June to November, it's the cyclone season: 51-73% rainfall, real hurricane risk (Irma 2017 category 5 hit Cuba), heavy heat (24-33°C). From May and October-November are the shoulder seasons. Highlights: Habana Vieja (UNESCO, 4 founding squares: Plaza de la Catedral, Plaza Vieja, Plaza San Francisco, Plaza de Armas), Malecón (8 km of seaside promenade, meeting point of Cubans at sunset), Capitol (replica of the Washington Capitol), Vedado (modern 50s quarter, Hotel Nacional, Coppelia ice cream), Miramar (embassies, Florida ferry dock in 1958, no longer since), Fortresses Castillo de la Real Fuerza, Morro, Cabaña (ritual cannonade 9pm every evening since 1762). Hemingway bars: La Bodeguita del Medio (mojito), El Floridita (daiquiri), Hemingway lived in the finca Vigía at San Francisco de Paula (museum). Try ropa vieja (shredded beef), moros y cristianos, congrí, tostones, daiquiri, mojito, Havana Club rum. Safety: Habana Vieja safe by day, vigilance at night. Cuban economy in serious crisis (chronic shortages, dual currency removed 2021, inflation 200%+).

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 22–28°C, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 16°C + tmax ≥ 26°C)
