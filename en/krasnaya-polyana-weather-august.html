Context

Our verdict

Krasnaya Polyana ("Red Glade"), ski resort and holiday destination in the Russian Caucasus 60 km from Sochi: 5000 inhabitants at 600-2200m altitude. Main site of the 2014 Sochi Winter Olympics (all the mountain events were held here: alpine skiing, biathlon, luge, bobsleigh, snowboard, freestyle), modernised on a large scale (50 billion USD invested = the most expensive Olympics in history). Today ski complex 4 connected resorts: Roza Khutor, Gorky Gorod, Gazprom Ski Resort, Alpika. August is my favourite month (8.3/10): 16-26°C, 48% rain, ideal temperatures. July stays excellent (8.0/10). From June to September, it's the summer high season: 8-26°C, Caucasus hiking, mountain bike, paragliding. From December to March, expect -2/9°C and 52-59% rain often as snow: ski high season. Pistes: 100 km combined (modest vs Alps), altitude 540-2320m, vertical drop 1750m (one of the largest in Russia). Roza Khutor = the smartest and largest, designed by French architects from Megève, European atmosphere, 4-5* hotels. Highlights: Roza Plateau (2320m, 360° panorama over the Caucasus, Black Sea visible 60 km to the south-west), Gorky City (winter amusement park, ice rink, luge descent), Achipse Fortress (9th-10th century Byzantine-Georgian remains), Skypark AJ Hackett Sochi (the highest pedestrian suspension bridge in Russia 207m, iconic 207m bungee jumping, 700m zipline), Mzymta River Valley (rafting, canyoning), 33 Waterfalls (33 waterfalls in canyon, hike). Excursions: Sochi 60 km away (Black Sea beach resort, palm trees and 28°C in summer paradoxically 60 km from skiing, already covered by BDW), Sochi National Park, Adler (Sochi suburb, airport, 2014 Olympic Park), Abkhazia 1h by car (separatist territory of Georgia recognised by Russia, border with Russian passport only). Safety: the Russian political situation makes any tourist visit to Russia inadvisable since the invasion of Ukraine in February 2022 (Western sanctions, international payments blocked, direct flights cancelled from Europe, Russian e-visa suspended for most Western nationalities). Logistics: Sochi-Adler airport (AER) 50 min away, direct Lastochka high-speed train from Adler 1h.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

When is the best time to visit Krasnaya Polyana?
It depends on the activity. For skiing: February (ski score 8.8/10). For hiking/summer: February (8.8/10, 6.0°C).
Can you ski in Krasnaya Polyana in winter?
Yes, it's peak ski season. Average ski score December–February: 8.4/10. Cold temperatures (6°C max in January) and frequent snowfall.
Is it warm in Krasnaya Polyana in summer?
In February, temperatures average 6.0°C. Best period for hiking and outdoor activities.
What is the wettest month in Krasnaya Polyana?
June is the wettest month with June has the lowest climate score of the year.
