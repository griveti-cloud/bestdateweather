Context

Our verdict

Miami is the tropical gateway of the USA: Latin (60% Hispanic, mostly Cuban), beaches, Art Deco, Cuban culture. March-April are my favourite months (8.5/10): 20-28°C, 33-42% rain, sea at 24°C, end of the tourist high season. From June to October, it's hurricane season: 65-81% rainfall, oppressive humidity (90%), real hurricane risk (category 4-5 possible, Andrew 1992 left a lasting mark). From December to February is the full high season: 18-26°C, 36-41% rain, hotel prices x3-x4, packed South Beach beaches. Highlights: South Beach and its Art Deco District (800 buildings from the 30s, Ocean Drive), Wynwood Walls (global street art), Little Havana (Calle Ocho, Domino Park, cigars at El Crédito), Vizcaya (Italian palace on the bay). American Spring Break March = party atmosphere. Everglades National Park 1h away (alligators, airboat tour), Key West 4h by car (Hemingway House). Try Cuban sandwich, ceviche, stone crab claws (season Oct-May at Joe's), arepas, key lime pie.
