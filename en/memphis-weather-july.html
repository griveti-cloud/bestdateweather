Context

Our verdict

Memphis, in Tennessee: 630,000 inhabitants, cradle of the blues (Beale Street), of rock'n'roll (Sun Studio where Elvis Presley recorded his first record in 1954), and of soul (Stax Records). Also sadly famous for the assassination of Martin Luther King Jr. at the Lorraine Motel on 4 April 1968. October is my favourite month (8.5/10): 13-24°C, 22% rain, ideal temperatures, foliage in Tennessee, festivals (Indie Memphis Film Festival). From May to September, expect 17-33°C: hot and humid, festivals (Memphis in May = world barbecue contest, Beale Street Music Festival), tornadoes possible in spring. From December to February, expect 1-13°C and 32-41% rain: mild for the south but greyish. From April and October-November are the best shoulder seasons. Music highlights: Graceland (Elvis's home since 1957, family mausoleum, Trophy Building, jets, cars — 4h visit, 80€), Sun Studio (Elvis, Johnny Cash, Jerry Lee Lewis, Carl Perkins, BB King all recorded here, 30-min visit), Stax Museum (Otis Redding, Isaac Hayes), Beale Street (pedestrian blues street, BB King's Blues Club, Rum Boogie Cafe), Rock and Soul Museum. Historic sites: National Civil Rights Museum at the Lorraine Motel (place of MLK's assassination preserved as is, important museum on civil rights in the USA). Mississippi River on cruise cargo. Mud Island River Park. Try Memphis BBQ (pulled pork in sweet vinegared sauce, at Cozy Corner or Central BBQ, distinct from Texas/Kansas City BBQ), barbecue spaghetti at The Bar-B-Q Shop, ribs.
