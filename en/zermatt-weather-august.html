Context

Our verdict

Zermatt, premium Swiss ski resort at the foot of the Matterhorn (4478m, the most recognised Alpine summit): 5800 inhabitants, car-free village (electric taxis, horse-drawn carriages), accessible only by train from Täsch. July is my favourite month (8.0/10): 9-19°C, 36% rain, alpine hiking in high summer. From June to September, expect 6-19°C: summer hiking season (Five Lakes, Riffelsee with Matterhorn reflection), MTB, climbing. From December to April, expect -7/2°C and 35-42% precipitation often as snow: prime ski season (322 km of pistes linked Zermatt-Cervinia cross-border to Italy, year-round summer skiing possible at Plateau Rosa 3883m). May and October-November are the off-seasons: little snow and limited hiking, many hotels close for 4-6 weeks. Highlights: Gornergrat (3089m, rack railway since 1898, panorama over 29 peaks above 4000m, Matterhorn/Monte Rosa view), Klein Matterhorn (3883m, Europe's highest cable car station, year-round glacier skiing), Sunnegga-Rothorn (lakes-side panoramic), Five Lakes Walk (5 lakes with Matterhorn reflections), Bahnhofstrasse (main street, palace hotels since 1854). Logistics: Zermatt 3h30 from Geneva by train (change at Visp). Very expensive: restaurant meals 30-60€, ski pass 80€/day, hotels 150-1000€/night. Try raclette, fondue, cervelas. Matterhorn ascent via local guides (1500€/person, 4 days acclimatisation, advanced mountaineering level).

Questions

Frequently asked questions

When is the best time to visit Zermatt?
It depends on the activity. For skiing: April (ski score 9.3/10). For hiking/summer: April (9.3/10, 6.0°C).
Can you ski in Zermatt in winter?
Yes, it's peak ski season. Average ski score December–February: 7.7/10. Cold temperatures (-1°C max in January) and frequent snowfall.
Is it warm in Zermatt in summer?
In April, temperatures average 6.0°C. Best period for hiking and outdoor activities.
What is the wettest month in Zermatt?
January is the wettest month with January has the lowest climate score of the year.
