Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Chapada Diamantina, national park at the heart of Brazilian nordeste in the state of Bahia: 1520 km² of plateaus and canyons, former diamond extraction region since the 19th century. One of the most famous hiking sites in Brazil. October is my favourite month (8.3/10): 18-28°C, 49% rain, clear sky after the dry season. From July to September, it's the dry season: 16-27°C, 49-55% rainfall. From November to March, it's the rainy season: 50-63% rainfall, but the greenest landscapes, the most impressive waterfalls. Year-round temperatures are stable (16-29°C thanks to average altitude 1000m). Highlights: Cachoeira da Fumaça ("smoke fall" 380m, one of the highest in Brazil, to see from above from the edge of the plateau or from below by 2 days of walking), Poço Azul ("blue well", cave with crystal water pool illuminated by a sunbeam 12pm-2pm, unique snorkelling), Poço Encantado (similar but more difficult to access), Vale do Pati ("Pati Valley", 3-7 day trek considered one of the most beautiful in Brazil, 1000m elevation gain, hot springs, host accommodation), Morro do Pai Inácio (1120m, easy 30 min access, iconic panorama at sunset), Lapa Doce (limestone caves), Cachoeira do Mosquito. Bases: Lençóis (pink-yellow colonial old town, stone bridge, restaurants, main tourist base), Mucugê (calmer, Byzantine cemetery), Igatu (former diamond mines). Logistics: Chapada Diamantina 7h by bus from Salvador (capital of Bahia, direct flights from Paris via Lisbon), local guide strongly recommended for treks. Try moqueca de peixe (fish in coconut milk), acarajé, godó (green plantain), artisanal cachaça.

How we calculate the score

For mountain destinations, we calculate two scores per month — skiing and hiking — and display the higher of the two.

⛷️Ski score DEC → MAY
  • 40% snow cover (slope T° corrected by lapse rate -6.5°C/1000m)
  • 40% winter thermal comfort (-5 to 5°C optimal)
  • 20% sunshine
  • Off-season penalty: ×0.70 adjacent months · ×0.35 outside season
🥾Hiking score JUN → SEP
  • 40% temperature (10–22°C optimal)
  • 40% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 20% sunshine

Questions

Frequently asked questions

When is the best time to visit Chapada Diamantina?
It depends on the activity. For skiing: March (ski score 1.9/10). For hiking/summer: July (7.2/10, 23.0°C).
Can you ski in Chapada Diamantina in winter?
Yes, it's peak ski season. Average ski score December–February: 1.8/10. Cold temperatures (28°C max in January) and frequent snowfall.
Is it warm in Chapada Diamantina in summer?
In July, temperatures average 23.0°C. Best period for hiking and outdoor activities.
What is the wettest month in Chapada Diamantina?
December is the wettest month with December has the lowest climate score of the year.
