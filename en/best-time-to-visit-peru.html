Peru, Andean South American country: 33 million inhabitants on 1,285,000 km² (3rd largest in South America after Brazil and Argentina). 3 classic regions: Costa desert (Pacific coast with Lima), Sierra Andean (Andean cordillera), Selva Amazonian (60% of the territory). Cradle of the Inca civilisation and pre-Inca cultures (Caral, Chavín, Moche, Nazca, Wari, Chimú). February is my favourite month (9.0/10) in Lima: 21-26°C, 14% rain, austral summer on the coast. From May to September, it's the tourist high season for the Andes (notably Machu Picchu): 14-19°C, 0-2% rain at altitude, clear sky. From December to March, it's the rainy season at altitude: Machu Picchu humid and foggy but green landscapes, Inca Trail closed in February for maintenance. Regions/sites to know: Lima (capital, most populated city, see dedicated review), Machu Picchu (UNESCO 1983, one of the most famous sites in the world, 15th-century Inca citadel at 2430m altitude rediscovered by Hiram Bingham in 1911, access by train from Cusco + Aguas Calientes + bus, or trek Inca Trail 4 days/3 nights, 500 visitors/day limit, booking 6 months ahead), Cusco (UNESCO, former Inca capital, base for Machu Picchu — see dedicated review), Sacred Valley (Pisac, Ollantaytambo, Moray, Salineras), Lake Titicaca (3812m, shared with Bolivia, floating Uros islands made of reeds, Taquile island), Nazca Lines (UNESCO, geoglyphs to see from plane, archaeological mysteries), Arequipa (UNESCO, "Ciudad Blanca" in white volcanic stone, base for Cañon del Colca), Iquitos (Peruvian Amazon, accessible only by plane or boat), Trujillo and Chan Chan (UNESCO, largest pre-Columbian adobe city in the world). Try ceviche (national dish, raw fish marinated in lemon), lomo saltado, ají de gallina, anticuchos (beef heart skewers), pisco sour, chicha morada.

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 22–28°C, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 16°C + tmax ≥ 26°C)
