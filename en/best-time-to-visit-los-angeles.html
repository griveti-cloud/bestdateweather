G
Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Los Angeles is Mediterranean stability over 12 months: no truly tough season, but important nuances. June is my favourite month (9.7/10): 15-28°C, 2% rain, and the "June Gloom" fog finally lifts. From July to September, expect 17-32°C with autumn Santa Ana winds fanning fires in the hills (fire season July to October). Smog can be uncomfortable when thermal inversion traps the air. From December to March, it's the Californian "rainy season": 17-27% rain, but still 8-20°C, mild winters. The Pacific Coast Highway towards Malibu stays viable, landslides may close Mulholland after major events. April-May and October-November are excellent transitions: fewer people on the beaches, pleasant terraces, often clear skies after the dying rains.

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 22–28°C, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 16°C + tmax ≥ 26°C)
