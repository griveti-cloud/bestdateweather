Context

Our verdict

Tel Aviv is one of the most secular and most 24/7 cities in the Middle East. UNESCO White City with 4000 1930s Bauhaus buildings, urban beaches, global tech scene. June is my favourite month (9.6/10): 21-29°C, 2% rain, sea at 23°C. From July to September, expect 23-31°C with heavy humidity (the hamsin/sirocco can push the feels-like to 40°C). Tel Avivians live in air conditioning, head out to the seafront in the evening. From December to March, expect 10-20°C and 25-39% rain: humid, sometimes storms, but mild winters. The Carmel and Levinsky markets for Israeli cuisine (sabich, hummus, sambousek). Yafo (Jaffa, old Arab city) to the south for the port and antique dealers. The security context evolves by period: to be checked (notably 2023-2025). Jerusalem 1h by train, Dead Sea 1h30 away, Negev desert 2h away. The nightlife scene is one of the most active in the Mediterranean basin.
