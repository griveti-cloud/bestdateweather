Context

Our verdict

Grindelwald, Swiss village resort at the foot of the Eiger (3970m, famous 1800m-high north face, one of the great alpine walls with the Matterhorn and Grandes Jorasses): 4000 inhabitants at 1000m in the canton of Bern (Bernese Oberland). August is my favourite month (7.8/10): 13-23°C, 59% rain in storms, alpine hiking, unique panorama of Eiger-Mönch-Jungfrau (Big Three of the Bernese Alps). From June to September, expect 6-23°C: very active summer season (climbing, hiking, paragliding, photogenic Bachalpsee altitude lake). From December to March, expect -1/8°C and 47-55% precipitation often as snow: ski high season. Pistes: 100 km Jungfrau Ski Region (Grindelwald + Wengen + Mürren, 1 single ski pass), accessible by train (Wengernalpbahn, one of the rare car-free resorts for Wengen). iconic pistes: Lauberhorn at neighbouring Wengen (mid-January World Cup descent, one of the longest on the circuit at 4.5 km). Eiger Express (cable car inaugurated 2020, 15 min to Eigergletscher 2300m, access to Jungfraujoch 3454m "Top of Europe" via railway tunnel dating from 1912). First (cable car, Bachalpsee hike, First Cliff Walk, zipline). Audience: very many British historically (Grindelwald was "discovered" by the British in the 19th century), Asians on day visits to Jungfraujoch. Hotels: Belvedere Swiss Quality, Sunstar Alpine, Hotel Spinne, 150-800€/night. Try rösti, raclette, fondue, Älplermagronen. Logistics: Grindelwald 1h30 from Bern by train via Interlaken, 3h from Zurich.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

When is the best time to visit Grindelwald?
It depends on the activity. For skiing: February (ski score 8.9/10). For hiking/summer: February (8.9/10, 6.0°C).
Can you ski in Grindelwald in winter?
Yes, it's peak ski season. Average ski score December–February: 8.7/10. Cold temperatures (4°C max in January) and frequent snowfall.
Is it warm in Grindelwald in summer?
In February, temperatures average 6.0°C. Best period for hiking and outdoor activities.
What is the wettest month in Grindelwald?
November is the wettest month with November has the lowest climate score of the year.
