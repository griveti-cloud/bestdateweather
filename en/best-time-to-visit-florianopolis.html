Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Florianópolis ("Floripa" to Brazilians) is the capital of the state of Santa Catarina in southern Brazil: main island (Ilha de Santa Catarina) connected to the mainland by 2 bridges, 500,000 inhabitants, 42 beaches, surf, young/trendy atmosphere. April is my favourite month (8.1/10): 20-25°C, 42% rain, end of the austral summer, mild temperatures. From December to March (austral summer), expect 22-28°C: Brazilian and Argentine tourist peak (Argentinians come en masse, hotel prices x3-x4, packed beaches). From June to August (austral winter), expect 14-21°C and 21-28% rain: cool (sometimes chilly mornings), but sun often delivers, prices halved or thirded, quieter and more local atmosphere. Highlights: Beaches Joaquina (surf, dunes), Praia Mole (lively, young), Praia da Galheta (naturist), Lagoinha do Leste (wild, accessible 1h walk), Praia dos Ingleses (family), Barra da Lagoa (charming), Lagoa da Conceição (lagoon at the centre, kitesurf, evenings). Historic centre on the mainland side (Mercado Público, Catedral). Anhatomirim Island (fortress, dolphins). Surf: Florianópolis regularly hosts World Surf League stops. Try ostras (local oysters, the island is one of Brazil's largest producers), camarão na moranga (prawns in pumpkin), açaí. Major digital nomad destination in Brazil.

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 22–28°C, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 16°C + tmax ≥ 26°C)
