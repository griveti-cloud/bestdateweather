Context

Our verdict

La Paz is the (administrative) capital of Bolivia: 3650m altitude, the airport (El Alto) is at 4150m, the world's highest. The altitude hits immediately: shortness of breath, headache, disturbed sleep. August is my favourite month (7.7/10): 1-16°C, 11% rain, Andean dry season, clear skies. From May to August is the dry season: cool but stable temperatures. From December to March, it's the rainy season: 60-69% rainfall, some Salar de Uyuni and Death Road tracks impassable. Highlights: Mi Teleférico (urban cable car network, the world's largest, 30 km, unique panorama), Witches' Market (Mercado de las Brujas, traditional llama foetuses), Plaza Murillo, Iglesia de San Francisco, Moon Valley (erosion landscapes 30 min away). Excursions: Salar de Uyuni from Uyuni (10h overnight bus, 3-4 days by 4×4 with basic accommodation, 200-400$), Lake Titicaca and Copacabana (3h by bus, 3812m altitude), Death Road by mountain bike from La Cumbre (4670m → 1180m, agencies in La Paz for ~80$). Try salteñas (traditional turnover), api con pasteles, llama steak.
