What to expect in October

Context

Our verdict

Budapest is Buda and Pest separated by the Danube: 2 cities in 1, baroque/Art Nouveau, thermal baths. June-August are my favourite months (8.9-9.1/10): 17-29°C, 26-39% rain, swimming at the Széchenyi Baths (outdoor pools open year-round at 38°C), festivals (Sziget early August, one of Europe's biggest). From December to February, expect -2/8°C and 26-32% rain: dry cold, Christmas markets at Vörösmarty Square, and especially the Gellért and Széchenyi baths under the snow (steam in the cold, a striking experience). The Chain Bridge reopened in 2023 after 4 years of renovation. Goulash at Centrál Kávéház, Tokaji in a borbár, Ruin pubs in the Jewish quarter (Szimpla Kert the most famous). Budapest remains one of Europe's cheapest capitals: traditional meals 8-15€, 4-star hotels 60-100€/night.
