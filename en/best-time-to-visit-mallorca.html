Best time by type of trip

🏖️Beach & CovesJune or October — crystal-clear waters, coves still accessible without crowds.
🥾Serra de TramuntanaApril–May or October — UNESCO site, walkable trails, ideal temperatures.
🚴CyclingMarch–May — Mallorca is THE pro cycling destination in Europe. Ideal temperatures (15–25°C).
👨‍👩‍👧FamilyJune or September — warm sea (23–26°C), bearable heat, fewer crowds.
💰BudgetMay or October — sea still pleasant, prices 30–40% cheaper than summer.
