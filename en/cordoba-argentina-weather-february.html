Context

Our verdict

Córdoba is Argentina's 2nd city, oldest Jesuit university in South America (1613, UNESCO). Student city, surrounding sierras. November is my favourite month (9.0/10): 16-28°C, 33% rain, jacarandas in bloom as in Buenos Aires. From June to August (austral winter), expect 5-19°C and 5-8% rain: dry and cool, ideal for hiking in the Sierras de Córdoba (Cerro Champaquí at 2790m). From December to February, expect 19-30°C and 31-44% storm showers: bearable heat at altitude (400m), violent late-afternoon storms. The Manzana Jesuítica and the Jesuit estancias of the region (Alta Gracia, Santa Catalina, Jesús María) are the major cultural attraction. Alta Gracia is home to Che Guevara's house. Mendoza 11h by bus, Buenos Aires 1h by plane. The Sierras Chicas (Capilla del Monte, La Cumbrecita) attract Argentinians for the weekend.
