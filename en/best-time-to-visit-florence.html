G
Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Florence concentrates the Italian Renaissance into 5 km²: Uffizi, Duomo, Ponte Vecchio. June is my favourite month (9.2/10): 16-29°C, 25% rain, Tuscan light on the Piazzale Michelangelo terraces (city view), aperitivo at Santo Spirito. July-August drop to 6.9/10 due to stifling heat (19-32°C, feels like 38°C+): Florentines fleeing to Tuscany, Uffizi suffocating even with air conditioning. From December to February, expect 2-13°C and 36-42% rain: damp cold, but this is the best museum period (Uffizi attendance down 50%, short waits). The Uffizi and Galleria dell'Accademia (David) require booking in peak season. Oltrarno district for the local side (artisans, popular restaurants), San Lorenzo for the market and popular cuisine, Fiesole 30 min by bus for the Etruscans and the view. May and September-October are the real sweet spots tourism/weather.

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 22–28°C, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 16°C + tmax ≥ 26°C)
