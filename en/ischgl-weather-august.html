Context

Our verdict

Ischgl, Austrian ski resort in the Paznaun Valley (Tyrol): 1600 inhabitants at 1377m, ultra-festive resort nicknamed "the Ibiza of the Alps" (sadly famous as the European epicentre of Covid-19 in March 2020 = thousands of European skiers infected). Linked to Samnaun in Switzerland by lifts (Silvretta Arena = cross-border skiing without customs formalities, duty-free shopping in Switzerland). August is my favourite month (7.7/10): 10-22°C, 58% rain in altitude storms, Paznaun hiking, mountain bike. From June to September, expect 4-22°C: calm summer season. From December to April, expect -8/6°C and 42-64% precipitation often as snow: ski high season, one of the snowiest in Austria. Pistes: 239 km Silvretta Arena (Ischgl + Samnaun, 1 single ski pass), altitude 1377-2872m, vertical drop 1500m, 45 modern lifts (Ischgl is known for its heated and hooded chairlifts, one of the most modern lift parks in Europe). Idalp = central plateau at 2300m, direct access by cable car from the village (15 min). Top of the Mountain Concert = season opening/closing late November/early May with a free open-air major concert by international artists (Mariah Carey, Kylie Minogue, Bon Jovi, Robbie Williams, Elton John, James Blunt have played). Après-ski: Pacha, Trofana Alm, Niki's Stadl, Schatzi Bar = Austrian party institutions (atmosphere until 22-23h, prices x3 vs elsewhere). Audience: Germans and British in the majority, young 25-45, more party than sport. Hotels: Trofana Royal (5*+), Hotel Madlein (5*), Yscla, 200-1500€/night. Try Kasspatzln, Tiroler Gröstl, Glühwein, Stroh 80. Logistics: Ischgl 1h30 from Innsbruck by car/bus.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

When is the best time to visit Ischgl?
It depends on the activity. For skiing: February (ski score 8.8/10). For hiking/summer: February (8.8/10, 4.0°C).
Can you ski in Ischgl in winter?
Yes, it's peak ski season. Average ski score December–February: 8.6/10. Cold temperatures (1°C max in January) and frequent snowfall.
Is it warm in Ischgl in summer?
In February, temperatures average 4.0°C. Best period for hiking and outdoor activities.
What is the wettest month in Ischgl?
May is the wettest month with May has the lowest climate score of the year.
