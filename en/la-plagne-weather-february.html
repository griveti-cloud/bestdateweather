Context

Our verdict

La Plagne, at 2100m altitude, is part of Paradiski with Les Arcs: 425 km of pistes, Bellecôte glacier at 3250m, 1600m skiable vertical drop. One of the largest French resorts in terms of accommodation capacity. July-August are my favourite months (7.4-7.5/10): 7-17°C, 38-47% rain, good for hiking and water sports at Lac de Plagne. From December to April, expect -11/3°C: major ski season, snow guaranteed. The Olympic bobsleigh track (Albertville Olympics 1992) stays open to the public in winter. The resort gathers 11 villages (Plagne Centre, Plagne Bellecôte, Plagne Soleil, Belle Plagne, Aime 2000, Plagne Villages, etc.) of varied design. From October to November and May, it's the slow shoulder season. Aime TGV station 30 min away by shuttle, Eurostar Ski from London in winter.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

When is the best time to visit La Plagne?
It depends on the activity. For skiing: May (ski score 9.5/10). For hiking/summer: May (9.5/10, 7.0°C).
Can you ski in La Plagne in winter?
Yes, it's peak ski season. Average ski score December–February: 7.6/10. Cold temperatures (-2°C max in January) and frequent snowfall.
Is it warm in La Plagne in summer?
In May, temperatures average 7.0°C. Best period for hiking and outdoor activities.
What is the wettest month in La Plagne?
November is the wettest month with November has the lowest climate score of the year.
