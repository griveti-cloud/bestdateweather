Best time by type of trip

🎉Parties & ClubsJuly–August — Paradise Beach, clubs until dawn. Book 6 months ahead.
🤿Diving & Delos TripMay or October — Delos archaeological site without crowds.
🌅Windmills & SunsetSeptember — amber light, windmills without crowds, end-of-season atmosphere.
