Context

Our verdict

Cape Verde ("Cabo Verde"), Atlantic archipelago off West Africa: 560,000 inhabitants over 4033 km², 10 inhabited islands in 2 groups (Barlavento in the north and Sotavento in the south), 570 km west of Senegal. Former Portuguese colony (1462-1975), Portuguese Creole. Extremely arid dry tropical climate (the islands sit in an intermediate rainfall situation between the Sahara and the humid tropical zone, with cyclical historic droughts). From May-June and November are my favourite months (9.4-9.5/10): 21-28°C, 0-2% rain, sea at 23°C, refreshing trade winds, ideal temperatures. From November to June, it's the tourist high season: 19-27°C, 0-3% rain, steady trade winds, ideal dry climate. From August-October drop to 6.9/10 due to heat (24-30°C) and the relative rainfall increase (8-36% but stays dry in absolute value). All year round, stable temperatures (19-30°C) thanks to the maritime effect, sea always navigable, near-constant wind. Islands to know: Sal ("salt", the most touristy thanks to the international airport, long Santa Maria beach 8 km of white sand, kitesurf at Punta Preta ranked top globally, salt pans at Pedra de Lume salt crater), Boa Vista ("beautiful view", 2nd most touristy, desert dunes at Viana, deserted Santa Monica beach 18 km, observation of laying loggerhead turtles August-October), São Vicente (Mindelo cultural capital, music festival in August, Cesária Évora the "barefoot diva" world morna legend born here), Santo Antão (the greenest and most mountainous island, Paul Valley hiking — surprising tropical landscapes for Cape Verde), Fogo ("fire", Pico do Fogo volcano 2829m the country's highest point, last eruption 2014-2015 partially destroyed Chã das Caldeiras village of 1000 inhabitants), Santiago (the largest, capital Praia, Cidade Velha UNESCO 1st European tropical city 1462). Activities: kitesurf (one of the global spots), diving, hiking in Santo Antão and Fogo, humpback whale watching March-May, turtles at Boa Vista. Try cachupa (national dish, corn/bean/cassava/vegetable/meat stew), pastel de massa, lagosta grelhada (grilled lobster), grog (local eau-de-vie), morna música (iconic slow nostalgic musical style).
