Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Saint Vincent, main island of the independent state Saint Vincent and the Grenadines (Commonwealth member): 100,000 inhabitants (110,000 in the entire country including the Grenadines), active volcanic island (La Soufrière 1234m erupted in April 2021 with evacuation of 20,000 people, last major eruption 1979). Capital Kingstown. More traditional and less touristic than neighbouring Saint Lucia or Barbados. April is my favourite month (8.3/10): 25-28°C, 36% rain, end of the dry season. From December to April, it's the tourist high season: 24-28°C, 36-44% rain, sea at 26°C, constant trade winds. From June to November, it's the cyclonic season: 61-72% rainfall, hurricane risk (Saint Vincent is on the classic cyclone trajectory). Saint Vincent highlights: La Soufrière (1234m, 6h round trip hike, still active volcano, last eruption April 2021, sometimes closed access for seismic activity), Dark View Falls and Trinity Falls, Botanical Gardens of Kingstown (the oldest botanical garden in the Americas, founded 1765, the original breadfruit tree brought by Captain Bligh "Mutiny on the Bounty" 1793 is still alive), Fort Charlotte (18th-century British, panorama over Kingstown), Owia Salt Pond (natural marine pool Atlantic side). But mainly the Grenadines = the major attraction: 32 islands and islets in the south, Caribbean paradise for sailing: Bequia (the largest of the Grenadines, 5000 inhabitants, local atmosphere, Princess Margaret Beach), Mustique (private luxury island, Mick Jagger and Bryan Adams have their villas there, legendary Basil's Bar pub), Mayreau (the smallest of the Grenadines, paradisiacal Salt Whistle Bay beach), Tobago Cays (5 uninhabited islets in the heart of a protected coral reef, turquoise water, snorkelling with green turtles, iconic postcard Caribbean photo). Pirates of the Caribbean: filming in 2003 and 2006 (Wallilabou Bay = beach from the film). Try arrowroot (Saint Vincent = world leader), callaloo soup, breadfruit, Sunset Very Strong rum (84.5% the strongest in the world, sold only to residents). Logistics: Saint Vincent 1h30 by plane from Barbados (often stopover), Argyle international airport opened 2017.

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 22–28°C, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 16°C + tmax ≥ 26°C)
