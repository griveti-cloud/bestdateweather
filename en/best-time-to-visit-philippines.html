Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Philippines (Pilipinas), archipelagic country in South-East Asia: 113 million inhabitants on 7641 islands. 3rd English-speaking country in the world by number of speakers (American colonial heritage 1898-1946), 80% Christians (Spanish colonial heritage 1565-1898) = only majority Christian country in East Asia with East Timor. Unique mix of Malay + Spanish + American + Asian cultures. January is my favourite month (8.3/10): 22-29°C, 8% rain, this is the ideal dry season. February-March-December stay excellent (8.0-8.1/10). From November to May, it's the dry season "tag-init" + "tag-tagaraw" hot summer: 22-32°C, sea at 27°C, this is the tourist high season. From June to October, it's the wet + cyclone season "tag-ulan": 47-77% rainfall, real typhoon risk. Highlights / Regions: Luzon (see dedicated review, main island), Visayas central region (paradisiacal archipelagos): Cebu, Bohol with Chocolate Hills = 1268 grassy conical hills browning in the dry season unique iconic view in the world + Philippine tarsiers = 10 cm primates the smallest endemic primates in the world, Negros, Panay with Boracay (already covered), Siquijor, Leyte, Samar, Palawan western region (already covered = El Nido + Coron + Puerto Princesa = top world karst landscapes), Mindanao southern region (inadvisable for Abu Sayyaf-MILF Islamist terrorism, but Davao + Siargao = capital of Filipino surf are accessible). iconic activities: Sulu Sea diving (one of the world cradles of marine biodiversity, Tubbataha Reefs UNESCO 1993 in the Sulu Sea = top 10 worldwide for diving but accessible only by liveaboard cruise March-June, Apo Reef, Anilao = capital of underwater macro-photography), swimming with whale sharks (Donsol = ethical November-May vs Oslob = controversial because sharks artificially fed), Siargao surf Cloud 9 legendary spot, archipelago sailing (rental of bangka = traditional Filipino boat with outriggers), Filipino festivals = among the most colourful in Asia: Sinulog Cebu January (1 million spectators), Ati-Atihan Kalibo, Pahiyas Quezon May (façades covered with fruits-vegetables-leaves iconic), MassKara Bacolod October ("festival of smiles"). Safety: Mindanao + Sulu archipelago inadvisable (Abu Sayyaf-MILF Islamist terrorism, Western kidnappings), but Luzon-Visayas-Palawan globally safe, urban crime Manila with dangerous Tondo quarters. Filipino hospitality = legendary, nicknamed "Pearl of the Orient", most welcoming people in Asia according to many traveller rankings. Try adobo (national dish), sinigang (sour soup), lechon (roast pig), pancit (noodles), halo-halo (icy dessert), San Miguel beer ("SMB" Filipino institution). Main airport Manila NAIA (MNL).

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 22–28°C, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 16°C + tmax ≥ 26°C)
