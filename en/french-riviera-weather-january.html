Context

Our verdict

The Côte d'Azur stretches from Cassis to Menton, 200 km of varied coastline. July is my favourite month (9.7/10): 21-28°C, 8% rain, constant clear sky, sea at 24°C. August excellent (9.3/10) but absolute tourist peak: prices x2-x4, A8 motorway saturated, paid beaches stormed. From December to February, expect 5-14°C and 20-23% rain: mild for France, often clear sky, palm and orange trees in full form. Nice Carnival mid-February, Menton Lemon Festival in February-March. Major cities: Nice, Cannes, Saint-Tropez, Monaco, Menton, Antibes, Saint-Paul-de-Vence (art village), Eze (perched village). Beaches: most are pebble beaches (except Pampelonne, Antibes). Calanques de Cassis and Estérel Massif for nature. Yachting: Saint-Tropez and Monaco concentrate the activity (Yacht Show Monaco late September). Try ratatouille, daube provençale, tarte tropézienne, salade niçoise. May-June and September are the real sweet spots tourism/weather.
