Context

Our verdict

Mumbai (Bombay), India's economic capital: 22 million inhabitants, Bollywood, Marine Drive the "Queen's Necklace". January-February are my favourite months (8.9/10): 19-31°C, 0-2% rain, mild tropical winter. From June to September, it's the monsoon: 87-100% rainfall in July (one of the world's most violent, 2400mm in 3 months), massive flooding, paralysed transport. From March to May, expect 23-33°C and heavy heat on the coast. From October to December, expect 20-32°C and 0-40% rain: return to normal. Highlights: Gateway of India (1924), Taj Mahal Palace Hotel (target of the 2008 attacks), Marine Drive at sunset, Elephanta Caves (UNESCO, 1h by boat), Dhobi Ghat (open-air laundry with 7000 dhobis), CST Station (UNESCO). Bandra and Juhu for Bollywood celebrities. Slums tour at Dharavi (1 million inhabitants per km², controversial). Try vada pav (street food), pav bhaji, butter chicken, biryani. Acceptable safety by day.
