Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Stowe, historic ski resort in Vermont (USA): 4500 inhabitants at 220m altitude (Mount Mansfield summit reaches 1340m, highest point of Vermont), nicknamed "the ski capital of the American East" since the 1930s-40s. More traditional and more accessible than Vail/Aspen but less snowy. July is my favourite month (8.3/10): 16-26°C, 48% rain, Long Trail hiking (long-distance trail crossing Vermont), mild summer, festivals (Stowe Performing Arts Series). From September to October, it's the foliage season (autumn colours): 0-21°C, 30-43% rain, one of the legendary destinations in North America to observe golden and red leaves (sugar maples, birches). Peak mid-October. From December to March, expect -12/4°C and 46-51% precipitation often as snow (5-7m/season, snow conserved by glacial temperatures): ski high season. Stowe Mountain Resort pistes (managed by Vail Resorts since 2017, part of the Epic Pass): 116 pistes, 13 lifts, vertical drop 720m, 2 connected summits (Mount Mansfield + Spruce Peak). Legendary pistes: Front Four (the 4 cult difficult pistes: Goat, Lift Line, National, Starr — the most demanding in the American East). Stowe Recreation Path = 8 km of cycle/pedestrian path in the valley, one of the most beautiful in North America. Audience: Bostonians, New Yorkers (Stowe is one of the favourite resorts of well-off East Coast families), historic skier heritage (von Trapp Family of "The Sound of Music" who settled here after their flight from Austria in 1938). Hotels: Trapp Family Lodge (5*, founded by the von Trapps 1942, now brewery too), Stowe Mountain Lodge, Topnotch Resort, 200-1000$/night. Try Vermont maple syrup (the best maple syrup in the world according to Vermonters), Ben & Jerry's ice cream (origin in neighbouring Burlington), Vermont craft beers (The Alchemist Heady Topper, Hill Farmstead = the most prized beers in the world). Logistics: Stowe 5h from Boston by car, 6h from NY, Burlington airport (BTV) 50 min away.

How we calculate the score

For mountain destinations, we calculate two scores per month — skiing and hiking — and display the higher of the two.

⛷️Ski score DEC → MAY
  • 40% snow cover (slope T° corrected by lapse rate -6.5°C/1000m)
  • 40% winter thermal comfort (-5 to 5°C optimal)
  • 20% sunshine
  • Off-season penalty: ×0.70 adjacent months · ×0.35 outside season
🥾Hiking score JUN → SEP
  • 40% temperature (10–22°C optimal)
  • 40% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 20% sunshine

Questions

Frequently asked questions

When is the best time to visit Stowe?
It depends on the activity. For skiing: February (ski score 8.4/10). For hiking/summer: September (8.6/10, 21.0°C).
Can you ski in Stowe in winter?
Yes, it's peak ski season. Average ski score December–February: 8.2/10. Cold temperatures (-1°C max in January) and frequent snowfall.
Is it warm in Stowe in summer?
In September, temperatures average 21.0°C. Best period for hiking and outdoor activities.
What is the wettest month in Stowe?
April is the wettest month with April has the lowest climate score of the year.
