G
Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Malaga has reinvented itself since 2010: from a transit city you crossed to reach Marbella or Nerja, it has become a real cultural destination (Picasso was born here, Centre Pompidou Malaga, Russian Museum, Carmen Thyssen). June is my favourite month (9.8/10): 20-28°C, 6% rain, sea at 22°C. July-August drop to 6.9/10 due to crushing heat (23-31°C, inland feels like 38°C+): locals go to La Malagueta beach early morning or end of day. From December to February, expect 9-18°C and 16-21% rain: mild climate, ideal for the Alcazaba and the Castillo de Gibralfaro without crowds. The Caminito del Rey 1h away (book 3-4 months ahead) remains the major nature attraction. Ronda 1h30 away for the famous bridge. Marbella 1h, Nerja 1h. May and September-October are the real sweet spots. The renovation of the Soho district has transformed the nightlife.

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 22–28°C, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 16°C + tmax ≥ 26°C)
