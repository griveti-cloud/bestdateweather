Context

Our verdict

Lanzarote was shaped by César Manrique, the artist who imposed low-rise white planning: no skyscrapers, no garish billboards. Near-desert dry climate. August is my favourite month (9.0/10): 18-23°C, 5% rain, steady trade winds. The whole year stays mild (12-23°C, 12-21% rain), it's the most stable island in the Canaries. Timanfaya (near-lunar volcanic landscapes), the Jameos del Agua and the Cueva de los Verdes are the must-sees (Manrique creations). Famara in the north-west has become a global surf and bodyboard spot. La Geria for vines planted in the black sand. Smaller island than Tenerife, but very diverse: 2-3 days minimum recommended. The calima (Saharan sand wind) can hit 3-5 times a year with a sudden ochre sky and 28°C.
