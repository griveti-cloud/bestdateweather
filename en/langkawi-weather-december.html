Context

Our verdict

Langkawi, archipelago of 99 tropical islands in north-western Malaysia in the Andaman Sea: 95,000 inhabitants on the main island (Pulau Langkawi). Duty-free status since 1987 (alcohol, cigarettes, perfumes, electronics = very cheap compared to the rest of Malaysia). UNESCO Global Geopark since 2007. From January to March are my favourite months (6.9/10): 24-32°C, 31-49% rain, this is the ideal dry season. Langkawi never tops 6.9/10 (hot tropical climate, significant monsoon). From November to March, it's the tourist high season: 24-32°C, sea at 28°C, hotel prices x2-x3 (Singaporeans on weekends, Middle Eastern tourists in winter). From April to October, it's the south-west monsoon: 77-91% rainfall, sometimes rough sea, many hotels stay open. Highlights: Langkawi Sky Bridge (Langkawi Sky Bridge, 125m suspension bridge curved in an arc, at 700m altitude above the jungle, accessible by Langkawi Cable Car = one of the steepest in the world at 42°, 2.1 km, 15 min, panoramic view over the 99 islands), Pantai Cenang (the main 2 km beach, white sand, turquoise water, nightlife, hotels in all categories, parasailing, jet ski), Pantai Tengah (south, more upmarket, Tanjung Rhu even more upmarket to the north), Telaga Tujuh ("7 Wells", 7-tier waterfall, swimming, natural slide down on the polished rocks), Eagle Square (Dataran Lang, 12m giant eagle statue, iconic of the town, Kuah main port and capital), Underwater World (aquarium), Mahsuri's Mausoleum (mausoleum of the Mahsuri legend, princess of Langkawi unjustly executed in 1819, her curse is said to have caused 7 generations of misfortune on the island until 1980), Galeria Perdana (museum of gifts to Prime Minister Mahathir Mohamad, fascinating cabinet of diplomatic curiosities), Kilim Geoforest Park (mangroves, caves, kayak, Brahminy Kite eagle observation = symbol of Langkawi). Surrounding islands: Pulau Payar Marine Park (snorkelling, diving), Pulau Beras Basah, Pulau Singa Besar, classic island-hopping tour 4 islands in 1 day. Try nasi lemak, char kway teow, Penang laksa, satay, fresh seafood (the daily night market in rotating villages by day of the week is the institution). Langkawi airport (LGK) with direct flights from KL in 1h, Singapore, Bangkok.
