By trip type

Best time by type of trip

🤿Diving & SnorkellingApril-May or October-November — calm sea between the 2 monsoons, excellent visibility.
🏖️Beach & GraniteYear-round — Anse Source d'Argent, Anse Lazio, unique granite boulders.
🐢Giant TortoisesYear-round — Aldabra (UNESCO), Bird Island, Curieuse Island.
🦜Hiking & WildlifeApril–May or October — Vallée de Mai (coco de mer), endemic birds.
👨‍👩‍👧FamilyDecember–April — calm sea, sheltered beaches, accessible wildlife.
💰BudgetMay-June or October — between seasons, slightly lower prices.
