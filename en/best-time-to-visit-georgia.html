Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Georgia (Sakartvelo), country in the South Caucasus between Russia, Turkey, Armenia, Azerbaijan: 3.7 million inhabitants. Country at the Europe-Asia crossroads, unique Georgian alphabet, isolated non-Indo-European language, Christianised in 327 (4th country in the world after Armenia 301). Independence 1991, conflicts with Russia (5-day war 2008, occupation of 20% of the territory Abkhazia + South Ossetia). Tourism boom 2015-2020. August is my favourite month (8.6/10): 17-26°C, 38% rain, ideal temperatures. July stays excellent (7.9/10). From June to September, it's the tourist high season: 12-26°C. From November to March, expect -4/10°C and 33-46% rain: harsh continental cold, abundant snow (ideal skiing Gudauri/Bakuriani), unique atmosphere under Caucasus snow. Highlights: Tbilisi capital (1.2 million, picturesque old town on the Mtkvari, Old Narikala Fortress 4th-century panorama, Abanotubani sulphur baths = millennial thermal institution, Peace Bridge modern 2010), Mtskheta 30 min away (UNESCO, former Georgian capital, Svetitskhoveli Cathedral 11th-century burial place of the Georgian kings), Kazbegi-Stepantsminda (village at the foot of Mt Kazbek 5054m, Gergeti Trinity Church 14th-century perched at 2170m on a hill = signature visual of Georgia, postcard image of the Caucasus, access on foot 1h30 or 4x4), Vardzia Caves (12th-century troglodyte city-monastery with 600 chambers dug in the cliff over 13 levels), Davit Gareja (6th-century troglodyte monastery Azeri border, medieval frescoes), Svaneti (UNESCO 1996, isolated region of the high Caucasus mountains with villages with Svan defensive towers from the 9th-12th century = signature Georgian cultural, Mestia Svaneti capital, Ushguli highest inhabited village in Europe 2200m), Kakheti (wine region, Georgia = cradle of wine 8000 years ago with qvevri technique buried clay jars UNESCO 2013, orange and red wines Saperavi), Batumi (Black Sea beach resort, already covered by BDW). Try khachapuri (signature cheese bread, Adjarian version in open boat with egg signature visual), khinkali (Georgian dumplings to pinch with 1 hand), churchkhela (walnut sausages with grape juice), qvevri Saperavi/Rkatsiteli wines. Tbilisi airport (TBS).

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 22–28°C, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 16°C + tmax ≥ 26°C)
