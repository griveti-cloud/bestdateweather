Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Cortina d'Ampezzo, "Queen of the Dolomites": 6000 inhabitants at 1224m, premium Italian ski resort surrounded by Dolomite peaks (Tofana 3244m, Cinque Torri, Faloria). UNESCO since 2009. Co-host of the 2026 Winter Olympics with Milan. July is my favourite month (7.4/10): 11-21°C, 75% rain in altitude storms, Dolomite hiking (Tre Cime di Lavaredo the famous one, accessible from Cortina), via ferrata. From June to September, expect 8-21°C: very active summer season, climbing, mountain bike. From December to April, expect -7/9°C and 24-50% precipitation often as snow: ski high season, but moderate altitude (1224m) = sometimes patchy snow cover at the village (snow cannons needed), good conditions at altitude (Tofana 2900m, Faloria). Pistes: 120 km Cortina, 1200 km Dolomiti Superski (12 resorts linked by 1 pass), Sellaronda (26 km circuit around Sella, one of the most prized ski experiences in the world). Pistes: Olympia (1956 Olympic women's downhill), Vertigine (World Cup events). Cinque Torri = open-air museum on WWI (Italo-Austrian front). Audience: Italian aristocracy (Venetians and Milanese), film shoots (Pink Panther 1963, Cliffhanger 1993, James Bond For Your Eyes Only 1981). Hotels: Cristallo Resort, Rosapetra Spa, Miramonti Majestic (5*, 200-1500€/night). Try canederli, casunziei (beetroot ravioli), polenta, speck, Lagrein wine. Logistics: Cortina 2h30 from Venice by bus, 3h from Milan.

How we calculate the score

For mountain destinations, we calculate two scores per month — skiing and hiking — and display the higher of the two.

⛷️Ski score DEC → MAY
  • 40% snow cover (slope T° corrected by lapse rate -6.5°C/1000m)
  • 40% winter thermal comfort (-5 to 5°C optimal)
  • 20% sunshine
  • Off-season penalty: ×0.70 adjacent months · ×0.35 outside season
🥾Hiking score JUN → SEP
  • 40% temperature (10–22°C optimal)
  • 40% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 20% sunshine

Frequently asked questions

When is the best time to visit Cortina d'Ampezzo?
It depends on the activity. For skiing: March (ski score 9.1/10). For hiking/summer: March (9.1/10, 6.0°C).
Can you ski in Cortina d'Ampezzo in winter?
Yes, it's peak ski season. Average ski score December–February: 8.2/10. Cold temperatures (2°C max in January) and frequent snowfall.
Is it warm in Cortina d'Ampezzo in summer?
In March, temperatures average 6.0°C. Best period for hiking and outdoor activities.
What is the wettest month in Cortina d'Ampezzo?
November is the wettest month with November has the lowest climate score of the year.
