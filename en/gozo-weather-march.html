What to expect in March

Spring marks the start of the good season in Gozo. Temperatures rise (15.0°C) and tourists have not yet arrived en masse. Rain risk remains low (23.0% of days), leaving good room for outdoor activities. Not the best window, but acceptable if dates are constrained. July (9.7/10) is significantly better if possible.

Context

Our verdict

Gozo is the rural sister of Malta: 67 km², 30,000 inhabitants, 25-minute ferry from Cirkewwa. No airport. More peaceful, more local atmosphere. July is my favourite month (9.7/10): 24-26°C, 0% rain, sea at 25°C. The Azure Window, the iconic arch, collapsed in 2017 during a storm: no longer search for it in the guides. The Citadella of Victoria (medieval citadel) remains the centrepiece, views over the whole island. Beaches: Ramla Bay (red sand), San Blas (accessible by path). Diving on the Blue Hole of Dwejra (collapsed too but still beautiful) and the HMS Trusty wreck. August drops to 6.9/10 due to the heatwave (25-26°C feels-like 35°C+). From November to March, plan 13-20°C and 23-39% rain: humid and windy, many taverns close, but the cliff walks (Ta' Cenc) stay superb. 2-3 days suffice as a complement to Malta.
