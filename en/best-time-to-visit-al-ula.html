Al-Ula (AlUla), archaeological oasis in north-western Saudi Arabia in the Hijaz desert: 5000 inhabitants. Newly opened tourist jewel of Saudi Arabia since 2019 as part of Vision 2030 (huge investments 50+ billion USD to make Al-Ula the "Saudi Petra" and rival neighbouring tourist Jordan). Major historic site: Hegra (Madâin Sâlih) = the 2nd Nabataean capital after Petra Jordan, created by the same Nabataean peoples 2nd century BC-1st century AD. November is my favourite month (9.3/10): 15-28°C, 5% rain, this is the ideal dry season. March-December stay excellent (8.5-9.1/10). From October to April, it's the tourist high season: 7-32°C, ideal dry and temperate desert climate. From May to September drop to 3.8-6.4/10 due to the extreme desert heatwave (22-42°C, sometimes 47°C+). Highlights: Hegra (Hegra/Madâin Sâlih) (UNESCO 2008, the 1st UNESCO site in Saudi Arabia, 110+ monumental Nabataean tombs sculpted into the pink-red sandstone cliff 2nd century BC-1st century AD, similar to Petra Jordan but much less frequented at only 60 visitors/day by preservation measures, limited access mandatory booking, iconic experience with fewer crowds than the Jordanian rival, Qasr al-Farid = "Solitary Tomb" 22m high iconic view, iconic photograph at sunset), Old Town of Al-Ula (Old Town, atmosphere restored 2019-2024 with paved-restored alleys-galleries, former 12th-century fortified town abandoned 1980, undergoing majestic restoration), Elephant Rock (Jabal AlFil, natural rocky formation in the shape of an elephant 50m high, photogenic iconic view, accessible by 4x4 in the desert at sunset), Dadan and Jabal Ikmah (millennial pre-Nabataean archaeological sites 1st millennium BC, iconic ancient lihyanite Arabic rock inscriptions, "open-air library" with thousands of inscriptions on the cliffs), Hijaz Railway Station (remains of the early 20th-century Ottoman railway, legendary "Lawrence of Arabia railway" attacked by T.E. Lawrence and the Arab revolt 1916-1918 = major historical monuments), Maraya Concert Hall ("Mirror" in Arabic, concert hall designed 2019 by Italian architects Giò Forma = entirely covered with mirror panels reflecting the desert landscape = Guinness Book record entry of the largest mirror building in the world, open for Festival Winter at Tantora winter every year, international concerts Andrea Bocelli/Yannick Noah have played). Activities: 4x4 safari in the Al-Ula desert, hot air balloon ride at sunrise above the desert landscapes (Al-Ula tourist iconic October-March), guided Hegra walk (mandatory booking), night in a desert camp Banyan Tree AlUla 5*+ opened 2022, Habitas AlUla resort 5*+, high prices 800-3000€/night in season. Try mandi (Saudi lamb rice), kabsa, Arabic coffee with Ajwa dates, harees. Logistics: Prince Abdul Majeed Al-Ula airport (ULH) with direct flights from Riyadh, Jeddah, Dubai, Doha (1-2h), from Europe with Riyadh/Jeddah connection.

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 22–28°C, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 16°C + tmax ≥ 26°C)
