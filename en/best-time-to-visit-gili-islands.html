Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Gili Islands, archipelago of 3 islands off the coast of Lombok island in Indonesia (east of Bali): Gili Trawangan (the largest, festive), Gili Air (intermediate, bohemian atmosphere), Gili Meno (the smallest and quietest). 3000 permanent inhabitants in total, explosive footfall since 2010 (Gili Trawangan = former backpacker spot, now much smarter). No cars or scooters on any of the 3 islands (transport: on foot, by bicycle, by cidomo = horse-drawn cart). May is my favourite month (6.9/10): 25-30°C, 55% rain. The Gilis never top 6.9/10 (tropical climate with marked rainy season). From May to October, it's the dry season: 23-31°C, sea at 28°C, this is the tourist high season, hotel prices x2-x3. From November to April, it's the rainy season: 78-96% rainfall, sometimes rough sea for the crossing from Bali, but stable temperatures. Highlights / Activities: Gili Trawangan = the festive one ("Gili T" to regulars), 16 km tour on foot 2h, east-coast beaches more beautiful, nightlife (Sama-Sama Reggae Bar, Tirnanog Irish bar, fire shows every evening), beach clubs (Pearl Beach Lounge, La Moomba Beach Club), many diving centres. Gili Meno = the calm and romantic one ("Honeymoon Island", 5 km tour, 1h on foot), white-sand beaches, central salt lake, ideal for couples, little nightlife, underwater statue Nest (circle of 48 metal statues underwater, work by Jason deCaires Taylor 2016, accessible by snorkelling). Gili Air = the bohemian intermediate (7 km on foot 2h), between Gili T's energy and Meno's calm, smart-backpacker atmosphere. Diving and snorkelling: excellent around the 3 islands (visibility 20-30m, green turtles guaranteed, blacktip reef sharks, rays, clownfish, seasonal manta rays), renowned PADI centres (Manta Dive, Big Bubble), Open Water training 4-5 million rupiahs (250-300€). Snorkelling tour 3 islands by boat (meeting point Turtle Point Gili Meno = swimming with turtles guaranteed). Sunset on Gili Trawangan west side = institution with view of Mount Agung Bali on a clear day. Try nasi goreng, mie goreng, gado-gado, satay, fresh seafood BBQ, Bintang beer, jaffles (toasted filled sandwich). Logistics: fast ferry from Bali (Padang Bai or Serangan ports) in 1h30-2h30 (300,000-500,000 IDR ≈ 18-30€), from Lombok airport (LOP) in 1h taxi + 30-min ferry from Bangsal port.

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 22–28°C, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 16°C + tmax ≥ 26°C)
