Which Season to Choose?

Phuket is visitable year-round, though January offers the best conditions (score 7.9/10). The rainy season (peak 91% in October) is often tolerable thanks to short showers and sunshine between them. Up to 11h of sunshine per day at peak season. 10 years of ERA5 data to pick the ideal date.
