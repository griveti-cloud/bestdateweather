What to expect in November

🎯 Harvest season — fresh local produce at markets and restaurants Autumn in Catania is often underrated: 20°C, golden light and declining crowds. An interesting window. Expect rain on 31% of days — a light raincoat in the bag is recommended. Not the best window, but acceptable if dates are constrained. June (9.5/10) is significantly better if possible.
