Context

Our verdict

Chicago is America's 3rd city, capital of the Midwest, a major architectural city (where the skyscraper was invented in 1885). July is my favourite month (8.6/10): 19-27°C, 40% rain in storms, festivals (Lollapalooza late July, Taste of Chicago), terraces and Lake Michigan beaches. From August to September, expect 16-26°C: still pleasant temperatures, foliage from October. From December to February, expect -5/3°C: the "Windy City" earns its name, freezing winds off the lake, blizzards possible, days down to 9h of light. But Christmas markets (Christkindlmarket) and Millennium Park ice rink. From April to May, expect 4-18°C and 39-46% rain: variable transition. Highlights: Cloud Gate (the "Bean") at Millennium Park, Willis Tower Skydeck and 360 Chicago Tilt for the view, Art Institute of Chicago (Hopper, Picasso, Seurat), Architecture River Cruise (genuine must), Navy Pier. Districts: Wicker Park, Logan Square, Hyde Park (Obama). Try deep-dish pizza at Lou Malnati's, Chicago hot dog, beef sandwich. Comedy at Second City (Bill Murray, Tina Fey).
