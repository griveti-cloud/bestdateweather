FAQ

Frequently asked questions

January is the best month with 21°C highs, 13°C lows, 13.3h of sunshine and 28% rainy days. The months of January & February offer comparable conditions.
Christchurch averages 13.3 sunshine hours per day in January. It is the sunniest month with a climate score of 8.3/10.
June is the most unfavourable month with 35% rainy days and the lowest climate score of the year.
In January, overnight lows reach 13°C, while afternoon highs average 21°C.
In winter (December-February), Christchurch scores 8.1/10 on average — Acceptable conditions for cultural visits.. The best month overall remains January with a score of 8.3/10.
June is the most difficult month to visit Christchurch, with 35% rainy days. Climate conditions are at their lowest of the year during this period.
