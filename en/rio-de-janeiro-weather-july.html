Context

Our verdict

Rio is one of the world's most spectacular cities: 6 million inhabitants between mountains (Pão de Açúcar, Corcovado) and sea (Copacabana, Ipanema, Leblon). September is my favourite month (8.6/10): 20-26°C, 28% rain, end of austral winter, ideal temperatures without stifling heat. From December to March, expect 23-30°C and 42-48% rain: austral summer, tourist peak, sea at 25°C, but also Carnival (4 days before Mardi Gras, variable dates February-March), prices x4-x5, hotels fully booked 6 months ahead. From May to August (austral winter), expect 18-25°C: cool morning temperatures, ideal for hikes (Pedra Bonita, Dois Irmãos). Highlights: Christ the Redeemer at Corcovado (cog train from Cosme Velho), Pão de Açúcar (2-section cable car), Escadaria Selarón (Lapa), Jardim Botânico, Niterói and its MAC (Niemeyer) by ferry. Districts: Copacabana (touristy), Ipanema (chic, more pleasant for swimming), Leblon (upmarket), Santa Teresa (artistic on the heights), Lapa (nightlife). Safety: favelas need a local guide, beaches OK during the day, vigilance at night. Try feijoada (Saturday tradition), churrasco, açaí bowl, caipirinha (born here).
