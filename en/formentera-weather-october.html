Context

Our verdict

Formentera is Ibiza's quiet sister: no airport, access only by ferry from Ibiza (30 minutes). Flat island (19 km long), ideal by bicycle. Caribbean-style turquoise beaches. July is my favourite month (9.7/10): 24-26°C, 1% rain, sea at 25°C. From July to August, the invasion is total: luxury Italians and French, prices x3, restaurants overflowing. Famous beaches: Ses Illetes (regularly ranked among Europe's most beautiful), Cala Saona, Migjorn. From November to March, expect 13-19°C and 17-28% rain: the island almost completely hibernates, 80% of restaurants and hotels close. May-June and September-October are the real sweet spots: still summer weather, pleasant cycling, and the chance to enjoy the beaches almost alone. No nightlife (unlike Ibiza): this is a contemplation destination, not a party one. Faro de la Mola for sunset.
