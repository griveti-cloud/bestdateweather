Gilles's view
Founder · BestDateWeather

Rajasthan, the largest Indian state (342,000 km²): 80 million inhabitants, land of Maharajas, Thar desert, palaces, fortresses, colourful traditions. February-March are my favourite months (8.9/10): 13-31°C, 3-9% rain, ideal temperatures. From November to March, it's the tourist high season: 9-27°C, ideal dry climate. From April to June, it's the extreme heatwave: 23-40°C (sometimes 49°C in the desert), impossible to visit. From July to September, it's the light monsoon: 41-69% rainfall, green landscapes unusual for the region. Classic itineraries: Golden Triangle Delhi-Agra-Jaipur (5-7 days), Royal Loop Delhi-Jaipur-Pushkar-Udaipur-Jodhpur-Jaisalmer-Bikaner-Delhi (15-21 days). Main cities: Jaipur ("pink"), Udaipur ("white"), Jodhpur ("blue"), Jaisalmer ("golden"), Pushkar (Hindu holy city, camel festival in November), Bikaner (Junagarh Fort, Karni Mata rat temple). National parks: Ranthambore (Bengal tigers, October-June, jeeps booked 90 days ahead), Sariska (less frequented alternative). Festivals: Holi (March, festival of colours), Diwali (October-November), Pushkar Camel Fair (November, 50,000 camels). Try dal baati churma, gatte ki sabzi, ker sangri, Jodhpur lassi. October-November and March-April are the best tourism/weather compromises.

How we calculate the score

The score combines 4 weighted criteria using 10 years of real climate data (ERA5).

  • 40% temperature (sweet spot 22–28°C, extreme heat penalty)
  • 35% rain (frequency + daily intensity)
  • 25% sunshine (hours per day)
  • Humidity penalty (dew point ≥ 16°C + tmax ≥ 26°C)

What is the best time to visit Rajasthan?
February is the best month with 26.0°C highs, 13.0°C lows, 10.1h of sunshine and 3.0% rainy days. The months of February & November offer comparable conditions.

Rajasthan averages 10.1 sunshine hours per day in February. It is the sunniest month with a climate score of 9.3/10.

What are the temperatures at night in Rajasthan in February?
In February, overnight lows reach 13.0°C, while afternoon highs average 26.0°C.
Is Rajasthan good in winter?
In winter (December-February), Rajasthan scores 9.0/10 on average — Decent period conditions for cultural visits.. The best month overall remains February with a score of 9.3/10.

July is the most difficult month to visit Rajasthan, with July has the lowest climate score of the year. Climate conditions are at their lowest of the year during this period.
