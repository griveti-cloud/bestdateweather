Context

Our verdict

Hiroshima is one of Japan's major historic destinations: the city rebuilt after the atomic bombing of 6 August 1945 (140,000 deaths in the long term). May is my favourite month (8.2/10): 13-22°C, 30% rain, mild temperatures before the monsoon. From June to mid-July, it's the tsuyu (rainy season, 46% rainfall). July-August drop to 6.9/10 due to humid heatwave (23-31°C). September-October are my real sweet spots (8.0-8.1/10): 14-28°C, clear skies, momiji starts in November. From December to February, expect 0-11°C: dry cold, often clear skies, reduced crowds. Highlights: Hiroshima Peace Memorial (UNESCO, former Industrial Promotion Hall preserved in ruins), Peace Memorial Museum (very emotionally challenging, testimonies, melted objects, 200¥), Peace Memorial Park (cenotaph, dome), Hiroshima Castle (rebuilt 1958). Miyajima 45 min by train + ferry: floating torii of the Itsukushima shrine (UNESCO, iconic of Miyajima), sacred deer, Mount Misen accessible by cable car. Try Hiroshima-style okonomiyaki (different from Osaka, layered), local oysters (winter season), momiji manjū. 1h30 from Osaka by Shinkansen.
