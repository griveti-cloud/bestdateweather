FAQ

Weather is virtually identical — both sit on the UAE coast with the same desert climate: November-April is excellent (24-28°C, sunny), June-September extreme (40-44°C). Choose based on attractions: Dubai for entertainment, Abu Dhabi for culture.
Dubai is the classic first visit — Burj Khalifa, Palm Jumeirah, Dubai Mall, the Creek. Abu Dhabi's Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque is among the world's most beautiful buildings and worth a day trip from Dubai (90 min).
Dubai is generally slightly more expensive. A mid-range Dubai hotel in winter costs $150-300/night. Abu Dhabi equivalent: $120-250. Both expensive by European standards.
Dubai has more variety — JBR public beach, Kite Beach, Palm Jumeirah private beaches. For beach clubs and water sports: Dubai. For a more relaxed experience: Abu Dhabi's Saadiyat Island.
Dubai has more family attractions — IMG Worlds, Legoland, Dubai Frame, Dubai Aquarium. Abu Dhabi has Ferrari World, Warner Bros World and Yas Waterworld. Both excellent for families October-April.
Dubai is best in March (9.2/10). Abu Dhabi is best in December (9.0/10).
