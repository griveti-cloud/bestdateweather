FAQ

The Dominican Republic wins on weather — Punta Cana's east coast location gives significantly less rain than Jamaica. Both share the dry season (December-April) and hurricane season (June-November). Punta Cana averages 280+ sunny days per year.
The Dominican Republic wins — Punta Cana's 50km of white sand and turquoise Atlantic is extraordinary. Playa Bávaro is consistently rated among the world's best beaches. Jamaica has lovely beaches but less variety.
Jamaica wins decisively — birthplace of reggae, ska and dancehall, Bob Marley's legacy, Rastafarian culture, Blue Mountain coffee and jerk cuisine. The Dominican Republic has merengue/bachata and historic Santo Domingo (UNESCO).
Both similar — about 9-10 hours from London. Dominican Republic has more direct charter flights from European cities. Jamaica has direct BA and Virgin Atlantic flights from London.
Both require normal Caribbean precautions. Jamaica has a higher general crime rate than the Dominican Republic outside tourist areas. For resort-based holidays: both equally safe within the resort zones.
Dominican Republic is best in January (8.2/10). Jamaica is best in January (8.2/10).
