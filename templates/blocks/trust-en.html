Why are these estimates reliable?

BestDateWeather is not a standard 7-day weather app. We analyse seasonal climate trends to estimate the most likely conditions for any given date.

📊

10 years of observed data

Every estimate draws on real weather data from 2016 to 2025: temperatures, precipitation, sunshine and wind, hour by hour.

🌍

Integrated ECMWF model

For dates within the next 7 months, seasonal trends from the European Centre for Medium-Range Weather Forecasts correct historical averages.

📐

Probabilistic score P10–P90

The score out of 10 is not an exact forecast. It quantifies the likelihood of good conditions by aggregating 4 key factors: rain, temperature, wind and sunshine.
